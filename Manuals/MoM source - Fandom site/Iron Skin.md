**Iron Skin** is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Enchantment "Enchantment") belonging to the [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **200** it may be cast on a unit on the overland map to give it a lasting [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+5** bonus (per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**), which must then be maintained with an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **5** per turn.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same bonus
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
upkeep costs).

## Effects

**Iron Skin** protects a unit from damage by improving its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") by a large amount.

### Defense Bonus

**Iron Skin** bestows a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+5** bonus on each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**
in the targeted unit. The bonus makes the unit hardier, by giving it an
extra five attempts to deflect damage from any enemy attack or
direct-damage spell. Each attempt has a 30% chance of success, and if
successful will avert 1 point of incoming damage. On average, this means
the unit will deflect an extra 1.5 damage per attack.

[Stone Skin](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stone_Skin "Stone Skin") applies to *each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** within the targeted unit*. [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure units** like the [Basilisk](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk") will therefore gain 5 chances to deflect an extra damage point each. In a unit with [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multiple figures**,
each figure gets the extra chances, but only the figure currently at
the front of the unit (i.e. the one "designated" by the game to take and
block damage for the whole unit) will apply them - unless that figure
dies. In such a case, the next figure will also have 5 extra chances to
block damage!

**Note:**  If the unit is enchanted with both [Stone Skin](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stone_Skin "Stone Skin") and **Iron Skin**, it will only receive the [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** bonus from **Iron Skin** ([Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+5**).

## Usage

**Iron Skin** may be cast during combat for the hefty [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40**.
In this case, the spell will only affect the targeted unit until the
end of the battle - at which point it will dissipate. This is good for
instantly protecting a unit that is about to come under attack from a
stronger enemy, or to ensure that a unit survives the specific battle
you're fighting.

Alternatively, **Iron Skin** may be cast on the overland map for a lasting effect. In this case it costs [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **200** to cast, and requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **5** per turn to maintain. Failure to pay this Upkeep Cost due to lack of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") will cause the spell to dissipate. However, as long as the spell is paid for (and not dispelled by any rival [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")), the unit continues to enjoy its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+5** bonus.

Like all other [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment"), **Iron Skin**
must targeted at a friendly unit, whether in or out of combat. If the
desired target is in an overland army stack, click on that stack to get a
list of units inside it, and then select your desired unit.

Once the spell is in effect, the unit will glow with a [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Green** aura, unless there is a more powerful spell also affecting it.

If you've placed **Iron Skin**
on a unit, you may remove it by examining the unit's details and
clicking the Enchantment's icon. The primary reason to do this would be
in order to remove the spell's [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"), thus conserving [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** for other spells.

## Acquisition

As a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** spell, **Iron Skin** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**2** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is not guaranteed unless the Wizard acquires at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**10** Spellbooks.

Customized Wizards possessing [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**11**
Spellbooks at creation time may choose this spell as one of their
default spells before starting the game, in which case the spell will
already be researched and available for casting immediately on the first
turn.

Wizards with [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**2** to [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**9** Spellbooks have a random chance of being able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") **Iron Skin** during the game. The chance for this spell to appear for research increases with the number of [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay. With [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**10** or [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**11** Spellbooks, the spell is guaranteed to appear for Research at some point, if it is not already available for casting.

**Iron Skin** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **1,120**.

With at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**2** Spellbooks, the **Iron Skin** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

**Iron Skin** can be beneficial to any unit, though it is marginally more useful when targeted at a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure unit**, as explained earlier.

This is an excellent spell to cast on any unit that is likely to engage enemies in [Melee](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") combat or be targeted by [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"). **Iron Skin** also helps somewhat when fighting wizards who often cast direct-damage spells of any kind.
