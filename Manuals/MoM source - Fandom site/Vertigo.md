**Vertigo** is an [Uncommon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** [Unit Curse](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"). For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **25** it may be cast on an enemy unit during combat. The target may make one attempt to [Resist](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") this curse as a unit. If it fails to do so, it is inflicted with penalties of [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-20% To Hit** and [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **-1 Defense** until the end of the battle or until **Vertigo** is dispelled.

Units possessing the [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") abilities may not be targeted by this spell.

## Effects

**Vertigo** can reduce an enemy unit's potential to both inflict and block [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**, unless it succeeds a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") check (with no extra modifier) when targeted by the spell.

### To Hit Penalty

Once a unit has been successfully inflicted with **Vertigo**, it receives a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") penalty of [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-20%**. This applies to any [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") attack made by the unit, with the exception of those that inflict direct [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Spell Damage**. In other words, it affects [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Attacks**, all three types of [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") ([Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack"), [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack"), and [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack")), [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attacks**, and [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Fire Breath**; but not [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation"), or spells and spell-like abilities such as [Fireball Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fireball_Spell "Fireball Spell") [Fireball Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fireball_Spell "Fireball Spell").

This means that whenever the unit makes an [Attack Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll"), its base [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%** chance to inflict a point of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** with each die is reduced to only [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **10%**. This applies to every [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the unit, and is cumulative with all other modifiers [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**, although it's worth noting that the grand total chance may never be reduced below [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **10%** per die, even if multiple penalties apply. For units that only have the default [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%** chance, this translates to a total effective reduction of 66% in the amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**
they cause! This can render even a powerful unit largely unable to
cause any significant amount of Conventional Damage to its enemies
during combat.

### Defense Penalty

While affected by **Vertigo**, the cursed unit also suffers a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") penalty of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **-1**. This means that each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** has one less die to use during any [Defense Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") made in an attempt to block incoming [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage"). This increases the average amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** that the cursed unit takes from such attacks. Since [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** can't go below zero, this effect will be ignored if the target's score is already [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **0**.

## Usage

 [A unit of affected by Vertigo.](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/c/c8/Screenshot_Vertigo.png/revision/latest?cb=20120115144907)

A unit of [War Bears](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/War_Bears "War Bears") affected by **Vertigo**.



**Vertigo** may only be cast during combat. It has a base [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **25**,
and must always be targeted at an enemy unit that is not already under
the effect of this curse. The target gets exactly one chance (as a unit)
to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resist** the effect with no default penalty, although if **Vertigo** is cast by a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"), this may be improved by [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") bearing a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-Spell Save**
enchantment. The curse will last until the end of the battle, until it
is dispelled, or until the target unit is destroyed; whichever occurs
first. While the spell is in effect, the cursed unit will have a [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**blue** rotating spiral shown above it, as seen in the image to the right.

**Vertigo** may not be targeted at units possessing either the [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") abilities. The game will refuse to target such units, asking the player to pick another target instead.

## Acquisition

As an [Uncommon Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") of the [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), **Vertigo** may become available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") who acquires at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**1** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
With none, the spell can naturally not be learned during the campaign.
This is the minimum requirement for it to be traded for, appear in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be found in the spoils of victory when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival Wizard who already knows it.

The base chance for **Vertigo** to be [researchable](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research "Spell Research") (at some point) in the campaign is roughly 10% (with [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**1** book), which gradually increases with the amount of [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** Spellbooks possessed or found during gameplay. With [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**8** or more, the spell is certain to show up sooner or later, unless acquired from another source. It has a [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research#Research_Cost "Spell Research") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **400**, although its research is quicker for Wizards possessing the [Sage Master](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sage_Master "Sage Master"), and/or [Sorcery Mastery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery_Mastery "Sorcery Mastery") [Retorts](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort"); or a bookshelf containing [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**8** or more.

**Vertigo** may also be selected as a starting spell by players who allocate all of of their picks into [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** books when creating their Wizard. In this case, it will be available for casting as soon as the game begins.

In addition, **Vertigo** is available as a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") spell. It is known by both [Valana the Bard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Valana_the_Bard "Valana the Bard"), and [Aerie the Illusionist](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Aerie_the_Illusionist "Aerie the Illusionist"),
although it's worth noting that neither of them can actually cast it at
their initial level. Valana needs to become at least a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Champion** (level 5) to use **Vertigo**, while Aerie will have enough [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** at [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Captain** (level 3), or possibly even at [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Myrmidon** (level 2), if one of her [Random Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Random_Abilities "Random Abilities") is extra [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**. Of course, they can both gain more by equipping appropriate [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item").

## Strategy

**Vertigo** can
significantly reduce a unit's combat effectiveness, and at the same time
make it easier to destroy. Unfortunately, due to allowing the target to
make its [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") roll with no penalties, **Vertigo**
may need to be cast several times before it can affect a unit - and
units with high Resistance scores are completely immune to it.

When **Vertigo** is
cast on one of the player's own units, it may be best to try to distance
that unit from combat as much as possible, as it is now more vulnerable
to the enemy, and won't be able to do a lot of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** anyway. Another alternative is to seek to dispel the **Vertigo** as soon as possible.

## Known Bugs

**Vertigo** is a [Unit Curse](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"), which all suffer from a bug or concept flaw in the latest official game version. Whenever a [dispel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel "Dispel") type spell targets this curse, it will bypass making a [dispelling attempt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel "Dispel"), and instead remove the curse automatically. This is corrected in the [Unofficial Patch 1.50](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unofficial_Patch_1.50 "Unofficial Patch 1.50").
