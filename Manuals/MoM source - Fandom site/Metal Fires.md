**Metal Fires** is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") belonging to the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40** it may be cast during combat to give a lasting effect, whereby the weapons of *all* friendly [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") burst into flames and receive several useful bonuses. For one, these units are now considered as having a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Magical Melee Attack** (if they do not already have a better one). They also receive a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+1**, [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+1** bonus, and a [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **+1** bonus to [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") strength (if they have it).

Units affected by [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade") do not receive the above bonuses - they already have better ones anyway.

## Effects

**Metal Fires** ignites the weapons of *all* friendly [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with a magical flame. This increases the attack properties of all those units by a small amount.

### Magical Attack

The first bonus bestowed by **Metal Fires** is to turn each affected unit's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") into a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Magical Melee Attack**.

With its Magical Weapons, the enchanted unit can now attack targets possessing [Weapon Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Immunity "Weapon Immunity") without increasing those targets' [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score.

This bonus applies only if the unit had [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Normal Melee Weapons** before this spell was cast upon it. Units already possessing [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Mithril Weapons** or [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Adamantium Weapons** will not "downgrade" to Magical Weapons as a result of this spell.

### Melee Attack Bonus

Any friendly [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") or [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") that possesses any [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") at all receive a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+1** bonus to its Melee Attack strength.

This increases the potential amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** caused by the unit's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") (and [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack")), and subsequently the average damage as well.

Note that Melee Attack is increased on a *per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure*** basis. Therefore, a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure unit** benefits much more from this than a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure unit**. For example, an [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **8-figure** unit at full health actually gets an increase to total attack strength worth [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+8** ([Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **8** \* 1 = 8).

### Ranged Attack Bonus

Any friendly [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") or [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") that possesses a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack** receives a bonus of [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+1**.

This increases the potential amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** caused by the unit's [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), and subsequently the average damage as well.

Note that Ranged Attack is increased on a *per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure*** basis. Therefore, a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure unit** benefits much more from this than a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure unit**. For example, an [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **8-figure** unit at full health actually gets an increase to total attack strength worth [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+8** ([Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **8** \* 1 = 8).

This bonus is not applied to units possessing a [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attack** or [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Attack**.

### Thrown Attack Bonus

Any friendly [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") or [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") that already possesses a [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") receives a bonus of [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **+1** to this attack's strength.

This increases the potential amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** caused by the unit's [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), and subsequently the average damage as well.

Note that Thrown Attack is increased on a *per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure*** basis. Therefore, a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure unit** benefits much more from this than a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure unit**. For example, an [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **8-figure** unit at full health actually gets an increase to total attack strength worth [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **+8** ([Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **8** \* 1 = 8).

### No Fire Damage!

Despite its name and implied effect, **Metal Fires** does not cause the affected units' [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") nor [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") to deliver [Fire Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Damage "Fire Damage"). The affected units will still deliver [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") as normal, or another damage type if attained from an ability or another spell.

This is actually a good thing, because [Fire Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Damage "Fire Damage") would only mean that [Fire Immune](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Immunity "Fire Immunity") targets wouldn't suffer this damage from the attack.

### Flame Blade

Note that any unit under the effect of the [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") does not receive any bonuses from **Metal Fires**. It will only receive the bonuses from [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade") ([Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+2**, [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+2**, [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Magical Melee Attack**).

This also applies to units that receive [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade") *after* **Metal Fires** is already in effect. Such units will now be affected by [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade") instead of **Metal Fires**.

## Usage

**Metal Fires** may be cast during combat for the high [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40**. It will immediately affect all friendly [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") that are not under the effect of [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade").

After casting the spell, its icon will appear in the
"Enchantments" window underneath the casting wizard's name. The spell
will remain in effect until the end of battle, or until dispelled by the
opponent. Since it does not carry on after the end of the battle, **Metal Fires** has no [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost").

## Acquisition

As a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** spell, **Metal Fires** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**2** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is not guaranteed unless the Wizard acquires at least [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**10** Spellbooks.

Customized Wizards possessing [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**11**
Spellbooks at creation time may choose this spell as one of their
default spells before starting the game, in which case the spell will
already be researched and available for casting immediately on the first
turn.

Wizards with [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**2** to [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**9** Spellbooks have a random chance of being able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") **Metal Fires** during the game. The chance for this spell to appear for research increases with the number of [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay. With [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**10** or [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**11** Spellbooks, the spell is guaranteed to appear for Research at some point, if it is not already available for casting.

**Metal Fires** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **960**.

With at least [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**2** Spellbooks, the **Metal Fires** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

**Metal Fires** provides a good way to increase the damage output of all friendly [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") simultaneously, though the increase is somewhat small in and of itself.

Naturally, it is better to cast **Metal Fires** when there are plenty of valid targets for it on the battlefield. Casting the spell when only a few valid targets (friendly [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") and/or [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero")) are present is wasteful - it would be better to just enchant them with [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade") (if at all available) instead. This would take a little longer to cast (and possibly a little more [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**), but provides a larger bonus to each unit.

**Metal Fires** can also be useful when confronted by many [Weapon Immune](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Immunity "Weapon Immunity") units, as it allows all friendly units to hurt them more easily. Again, consider [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade") as a substitute if time and [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** permit it.
