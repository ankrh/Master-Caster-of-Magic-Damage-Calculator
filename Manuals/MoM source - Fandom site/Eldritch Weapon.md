**Eldritch Weapon** is a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") belonging to the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **75** it may be cast on a [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") or [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") on the overland map to imbue its weapons with energy, turning its [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") into a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Magical Melee Attack** (if not already better). In addition, when any enemy unit attempts to block damage from this attack, it receives a temporary [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **-10% To Block** penalty, increasing the chance of damage to get through its defenses.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **15**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same bonus
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
upkeep costs).

## Effects

**Eldritch Weapon** imbues the weapons of a [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") or [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with fantastic properties, and makes them considerably harder to defend against.

### Magical Attack

The first bonus bestowed by **Eldritch Weapon** is to turn the enchanted unit's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") into a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Magical Melee Attack**.

With its Magical Weapons, the enchanted unit can now attack targets possessing [Weapon Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Immunity "Weapon Immunity") without increasing those targets' [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score.

This bonus applies only if the unit had [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Normal Melee Weapons** before this spell was cast upon it. Units already possessing [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Mithril Weapons** or [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Adamantium Weapons** will not "downgrade" to Magical Weapons as a result of this spell.

### Target To Block Penalties

When a unit with **Eldritch Weapon** attacks an enemy target, the target's defensive ability is reduced in a special way that alters the normal combat rules.

In normal [Melee Combat](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"), each [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Shield** possessed by the target has a 30% chance of blocking [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1 point of incoming damage**. This is called its "[Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block**" chance. As a result, a unit with [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **10** will block only [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **3** on average from any attack.

However, when the **Eldritch Weapon** unit attacks a target, that target's To Block chance is reduced by [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **-10%**. Normally this would put the target's To Block chance at [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **20%**. As a result, a target with [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **10** will now only block [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **2** on average! Note that the penalty is completely temporary, and disappears once the Enchanted unit's attack is concluded.

Note, that the [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **-10%** penalty also applies for [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown** and [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile** attacks. However, it doesn't apply for [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder** and [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magic** attacks.

## Usage

**Eldritch Weapon** may be cast during combat for the rather low [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **15**. In this case, the spell will only affect the targeted unit until the end of the battle - at which point it will dissipate.

Alternatively, **Eldritch Weapon** may be cast on the overland map for a lasting effect. In this case it has a base [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **75**, and requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1** per turn to maintain. Failure to pay this Upkeep Cost due to lack of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") will cause the spell to dissipate. However, as long as the spell is paid for (and not dispelled by any rival [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")), the unit continues to enjoy its bonus.

**Eldritch Weapon** may only be cast on friendly [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"). It cannot target [Fantastic Creatures](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Creature "Fantastic Creature").
If the desired target is in an overland army stack, click on that stack
to get a list of units inside it, and then select your desired unit.

Note that during battle, **Eldritch Weapon** may not be cast on any units mutated by the [Chaos Channels](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Channels "Chaos Channels") spell. Nonetheless you may target these units freely on the overland map, and the effects will carry into combat. The [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") are not valid targets in either case.

Once the spell is in effect, the unit will glow with a [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Red** aura, unless there is a more powerful spell also affecting it.

If you've placed **Eldritch Weapon**
on a unit, you may remove it by examining the unit's details and
clicking the Enchantment's icon. The primary reason to do this would be
in order to remove the spell's [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"), thus conserving [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** for other spells.

## Acquisition

As a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** spell, **Eldritch Weapon** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least one [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is not guaranteed unless the Wizard acquires at least [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**7** Spellbooks.

Customized Wizards possessing up to [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**10**
Spellbooks at creation time may choose this spell as one of their
default spells before starting the game, in which case the spell will
already be researched and available for casting immediately on the first
turn. Wizards with [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**11** Spellbooks are guaranteed to have this spell available for casting as soon as the game begins.

Wizards with [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**6** or fewer Spellbooks who have not chosen **Eldritch Weapon** as a guaranteed spell have a random chance of being able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") it. The chance for this spell to appear increases with the number of [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay. With [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**−3** Spellbooks, the spell is guaranteed to appear for Research if it is not already available for casting.

**Eldritch Weapon** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **130**.

With at least [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**1** Spellbook, the **Eldritch Weapon** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

The ingame tooltip for **Eldritch Weapon** states rather unnecessarily that the spell stacks with [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**[Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade")**. The truth is that the effects of these two [enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") are very different from one another, and that while the [Uncommon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") spell bestows a simple attack bonus, this [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") spell's effect of inflicting [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **-10%** is an exotic effect available almost nowhere else. This penalty is almost as strong as [Armor Piercing](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Armor_Piercing "Armor Piercing") (which halves shields) against ordinary foes, since their block rates will be reduced from [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30%** to [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **20%**, and it even acts in concert with that ability.

Most importantly of all, **Eldritch Weapon** gets ever stronger as the enemies get stronger. It offsets the blocking conferred by enhancements like [Lucky](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lucky "Lucky"), [Invisibility](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Invisibility "Invisibility"), and [(High)](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/High_Prayer "High Prayer") [Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Prayer "Prayer"), and its effect is most pronounced against bigshots with lots of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Shields**, such as dragons, colossi, and high-level [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero").
