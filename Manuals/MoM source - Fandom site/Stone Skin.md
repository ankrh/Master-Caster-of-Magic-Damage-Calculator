**Stone Skin** is a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Enchantment "Enchantment") belonging to the [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **50** it may be cast on a unit on the overland map to give it a lasting [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+1** bonus (per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**), which must then be maintained with a [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1** per turn [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"). Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **10**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same bonus
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
upkeep costs).

## Effects

**Stone Skin** protects a unit from damage by improving its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") by a small amount.

### Defense Bonus

**Stone Skin** bestows a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+1** bonus on each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**
in the targeted unit. The bonus makes the unit hardier, by adding a
single 30% chance to deflect one extra point of damage from any enemy
attack or direct-damage spell.

**Stone Skin** applies to *each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** within the targeted unit*. [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure units** like the [Basilisk](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk") will therefore have a straightforward 30% chance to deflect an extra damage point. In a unit with [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multiple figures**,
each figure gets that extra chance, but only the figure currently at
the front of the unit (i.e. the one "designated" by the game to take and
block damage for the whole unit) will apply it - unless that figure
dies. In such a case, the next figure will also have an extra chance to
block damage!

**Note:**  If the unit is enchanted with both **Stone Skin** and [Iron Skin](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_Skin "Iron Skin"), it will only receive the [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** bonus from [Iron Skin](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Iron_Skin "Iron Skin") ([Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+5**).

## Usage

**Stone Skin** may be cast during combat for the rather low [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **10**. In this case, the spell will only affect the targeted unit until the end of the battle - at which point it will dissipate.

Alternatively, **Stone Skin** may be cast on the overland map for a lasting effect. In this case it costs [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **50** to cast, and requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1** per turn to maintain. Failure to pay this Upkeep Cost due to lack of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") will cause the spell to dissipate. However, as long as the spell is paid for (and not dispelled by any rival [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")), the unit continues to enjoy its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+1** bonus.

Like all other [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment"), **Stone Skin**
must targeted at a friendly unit, whether in or out of combat. If the
desired target is in an overland army stack, click on that stack to get a
list of units inside it, and then select your desired unit.

Once the spell is in effect, the unit will glow with a [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Green** aura, unless there is a more powerful spell also affecting it.

If you've placed **Stone Skin**
on a unit, you may remove it by examining the unit's details and
clicking the Enchantment's icon. The primary reason to do this would be
in order to remove the spell's [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"), thus conserving [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** for other spells.

## Acquisition

As a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** spell, **Stone Skin** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least one [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is not guaranteed unless the Wizard acquires at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**7** Spellbooks.

Customized Wizards possessing up to [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**10**
Spellbooks at creation time may choose this spell as one of their
default spells before starting the game, in which case the spell will
already be researched and available for casting immediately on the first
turn. Wizards with [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**11** Spellbooks are guaranteed to have this spell available for casting as soon as the game begins.

Wizards with [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**6** or fewer Spellbooks who have not chosen **Stone Skin** as a guaranteed spell have a random chance of being able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") it. The chance for this spell to appear increases with the number of [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay. With [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**−3** Spellbooks, the spell is guaranteed to appear for Research if it is not already available for casting.

**Stone Skin** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **80**.

With at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**1** Spellbook, the **Stone Skin** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

**Stone Skin** is not a
very powerful spell, but it can be very helpful during the early game
for reducing damage to a unit during combat or otherwise.

As mentioned above, the benefit to a unit is marginally greater the more [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures**
it contains. It will generally reduce the rate at which figures from
this unit die off. Therefore, the best application for this spell is
when casting it at a [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit")
containing 8 figures. The spell can be somewhat useful when cast at
units with fewer figure counts, but will usually fall out of favor as
the game matures.
