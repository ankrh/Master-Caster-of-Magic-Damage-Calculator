:   *This article deals specifically with Ranged **Boulder** Attacks. For an overview of how Ranged Attacks work in general, see [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack").*

A **Ranged Boulder Attack** is one of three kinds of [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") available in *Master of Magic*. When a unit is capable of making a **Ranged Boulder Attack**, its details panel will show the strength of the attack as a series of [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Rocks**. The same icon is used on this wiki to represent **Ranged Boulder Attacks** and/or their strength.

**Ranged Boulder Attacks**
are often very strong in numerical terms, capable of piercing through
heavy armor very easily. Furthermore, many units possessing this type of
attack also possess the [Wall Crusher](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wall_Crusher "Wall Crusher") ability which allows them to tear down enemy [City Walls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/City_Walls "City Walls") during siege combat. **Ranged Boulder Attacks** do suffer from [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**
penalties based on the distance to the target: the further away the
target, the higher the penalty. Thus they are significantly less useful
at long range. However, for some units the maximum size of the penalty
is limited, making them almost as useful at long range as they are
up-close.

Finally, **Ranged Boulder Attacks** do not trigger any of the target's immunities. Thus, no target is really safe from **Ranged Boulder Attacks**, and only extremely-heavy armor has any chance of averting their damage.

Like all other [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), **Ranged Boulder Attacks** may only be used a limited number of times per battle. This number is displayed in the unit's details panel.

**Ranged Boulder Attacks** are somewhat uncommon. All [Catapults](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Catapult "Catapult") and [Warships](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Warship "Warship"), which are available to several races, possess a strong **Ranged Boulder Attack**. 2 unique racial units also possess this ability, as well as 3 different [Fantastic Creatures](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Creature "Fantastic Creature"). There are no [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") who possess a **Ranged Boulder Attack**.

## Ranged Boulder Attack Properties

A [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attack** is one of three different types of [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") in *Master of Magic*, with the other two being [Ranged Missile Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") and [Ranged Magical Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack"). There are several important differences between each of these attack types. The advantages and disadvantages of **Ranged Boulder Attacks** are explained below - for an overall comparison, see [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack").

### No Immunities

A **Ranged Boulder Attack** will never trigger any of its target's immunities. [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity"), [Missile Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Missile_Immunity "Missile Immunity") and [Weapon Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Immunity "Weapon Immunity") are useless against this attack. This gives **Ranged Boulder Attacks** an important advantage when fighting an army possessing such units.

Furthermore, due to the relative strength of most **Ranged Boulder Attacks**, which for some units exceeds [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **10**, very few units will have sufficient armor to block all of the [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** the attack delivers. Only well-armored [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"), powerful [Fantastic Creatures](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Creature "Fantastic Creature"), and units with several [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") increasing their [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score have any chance of avoiding this damage entirely.

Thus, when it is important to inflict heavy damage on well-defended targets, **Ranged Boulder Attackers** are a good bet.

### Distance Penalties

On the other hand, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attacks**
suffer from an important disadvantage that seriously affects the way
they are used in combat, especially when compared to the simpler (and
sometimes deadlier) [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks**: they suffer [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** penalties based on distance to the target.

The penalty equals [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10% To Hit** for every 3 tiles of distance between the shooter and the target. Therefore, the shooter suffers [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10%** at a distance of 3 tiles, and [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-20%** at a distance of 6 tiles. The game's original manual indicates that [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-20%** is the largest penalty possible, but the Strategy Guide implies that the bonus could be even higher (i.e. [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-30%** at 9 tiles, and so on).

At a distance of 1 or 2 tiles from the target (i.e. either
adjacent to the shooter or one empty tile apart), the shooter suffers no
[To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** penalty at all.

As a result of this penalty, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attackers**
will sometimes want to close the distance with their target before
spending any of their ammunition. This is particularly important when
the target is in fact heavily-armored, requiring the shot to inflict
more hits to increase the chances of getting through that armor. Against
softer units, a [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attacker** may nonetheless prefer to quickly expend its ammunition despite the long range.

The same principle is also important to remember when fighting against enemy [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attackers**:
if you wish to minimize damage, keep your units as far away from the
enemy Boulder troops until they've depleted all of their ammunition.
After that you can close the distance more safely.

### Supporting Abilities

Many [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attackers** possess important [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") that are specifically tailored to improve the usefulness of their attack.

First and foremost, more than half of these units possess the [Wall Crusher](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wall_Crusher "Wall Crusher") ability, which enables them to take out enemy [City Walls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/City_Walls "City Walls") at a distance. The chance of bringing down a wall with a [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") is set at 25% per shot (regardless of the actual strength of the attack). This is the main reason to create [Catapults](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Catapult "Catapult") and bring them along when expecting siege combat.

Secondly, several [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attackers** possess the [Long Range](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Long_Range "Long Range")
ability. This puts a hard limit on the distance penalties the unit can
suffer when attacking distant targets. The limit is exactly [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10% To Hit**,
which means that a target at 3 tiles is as hard to hit as a target way
across on the other side of the battlefield. Thus, units with this
ability will often unleash their [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attacks** early in the battle, and do not need to risk approaching the enemy in order to get a good damage output.

## Units with Ranged Boulder Attacks

The game contains exactly 7 units that possess a **Ranged Boulder Attack**,
though several of these are widely available to most empires. These 7
units are the only units that possess such an attack: no unit may
acquire a **Ranged Boulder Attack** under any circumstance if it does not have one by default.

### Normal Units

4 of the **Ranged Boulder Attack** units in the game are [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") from various [Races](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Races "Races"). Among these, 2 are standardized units - the [Catapult](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Catapult "Catapult") and [Warship](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Warship "Warship") - which are available to a number of [Races](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Race "Race"). The other two are unique racial units: the [Air Ship](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Air_Ship "Air Ship") and [Steam Cannon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Steam_Cannon "Steam Cannon"). All 4 are [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure units**, but possess a strong attack to compensate.

All 4 units also have plenty of ammunition for their **Ranged Boulder Attacks**, capable of firing at least 10 shots per battle - much more than most [Ranged Magical Attackers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") and slightly more than most [Ranged Missile Attackers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack"). The [Warship](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Warship "Warship")
is unique in this regard, since it has no real limit on the number of
times it can fire - making it extremely powerful in the hands of a
capable player.

[Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") equipped with [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Mithril Weapons** or [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Adamantium Weapons** receive a bonus of [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **+1** or [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **+2** (respectively) to their **Ranged Boulder Attack**. Those weapons as well as [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Magical Weapons** will also give a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **+10% To Hit** bonus to Boulder Attacks.

[Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") acquire [Experience](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience "Experience") over time, which slowly increases the strength of their **Ranged Boulder Attack**.
Some can become surprisingly powerful by the time they reach Level 4
("Elite") or higher. The table below lists a unit's strength when it is

### Fantastic Units

3 [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") may make **Ranged Boulder Attacks**. All 3 have the [Wall Crusher](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wall_Crusher "Wall Crusher") ability.

[Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") with a **Ranged Boulder Attack**
are very limited in terms of ammunition, capable of performing only 2
of these attacks during each battle. Fortunately, all 3 are very
powerful [Melee Attackers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") as well, and can switch to a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") once their shots are depleted. All 3 of these units are also fast enough to enable them to close the distance with the enemy *while* firing, thus potentially negating the distance penalty and inflicting more [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** on their opponents, before rushing in for Melee.
