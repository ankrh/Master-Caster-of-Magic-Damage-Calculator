**Poison Damage** is a type of [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") dealt by the [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") special ability. Each point of **Poison Damage** striking a target forces that target to make 1 [Resistance Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Resistance_Roll "Resistance"), where an unsuccessful roll results in the target suffering [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1** point of [Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points").

**Poison Damage** will inflict no harm on targets possessing [PoisonImmunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/PoisonImmunity "PoisonImmunity") [Poison Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Immunity "Poison Immunity"). Furthermore, targets possessing an overall [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** or higher will never fail their rolls against **Poison Damage**, and thus will suffer no harm from it.

**Poison Damage** is delivered only by units possessing the [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") Poison Touch attacks. There is no other known source of **Poison Damage**, nor any way of adding this trait to a unit that does not possess it by default.

## Effect

**Poison Damage** forces its target to make one or more [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, with each failed roll resulting in [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1** point of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** being suffered by that target.

### Resistance and Damage

Whenever a target is struck by any non-spell attack from a unit possessing the [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") ability, that target must immediately make a number of [Resistance Checks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Resistance_Roll "Resistance") equal to strength of this ability.

Each roll generates a completely random number between 1 and 10. If the result is *higher than* the target unit's current [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score, then the check is considered "failed", and [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1 point of Damage** is inflicted on the unit. For example, a total of 3 "failed" rolls means that the target unit suffers a total of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **3**.

The resulting [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** is added to, and applied simultaneously with, the [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** of the conventional attack that triggered the **Poison Damage** attack. Consequently, if there are multiple [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** in the [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") Poison Touch unit, there will also be this many **Poison Damage** attacks.

## Immunities

Because **Poison Damage** relies on a target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score, and inflicts no modifier on that score, any target with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** or higher is completely immune to it. Such targets could not possibly fail their [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, and thus would block all incoming **Poison Damage** automatically.

Furthermore, any unit possessing [PoisonImmunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/PoisonImmunity "PoisonImmunity") [Poison Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Immunity "Poison Immunity") is completely immune to **Poison Damage**. It does not need to make any checks whatsoever – it simply suffers no harm.

In either case, these immunities will only block the **Poison Damage** component of an attack. They have no effect on the [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") otherwise caused by the triggering attack, which may or may not go through the target's defenses unimpeded.

**Poison Damage** is one of only two types of [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") that are not blocked by a target's [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") in any way.

## Sources of Poison Damage

**Poison Damage** has only one source: the [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch")
ability. Fortunately, this ability is possessed by a wide variety of
units, so it is by no means rare. Please refer to the article on the
ability for a complete list of all units possessing it.
