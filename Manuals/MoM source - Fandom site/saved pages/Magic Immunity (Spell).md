**Magic Immunity** is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") belonging to the [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Sorcery Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **250**
it may be cast on a unit on the overland map to protect it from nearly
all forms of offensive magic. This includes direct-damage spells; [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"); most negative effects from [Combat](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") and [Global Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Global_Enchantment "Global Enchantment"); [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath Attacks** and [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacks**; and finally most [Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") and [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"). **Magic Immunity** must then be maintained with an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **5** per turn.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **50**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same
bonuses on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent
effects or upkeep costs).

## Effects

:   *Main article: [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity")*

**Magic Immunity** protects a unit from nearly all magical damage
and ill effects - making it one of the most valuable Unit Enchantments
in the game.

### Protection from Spells

While affected by **Magic Immunity**, a unit will be unaffected by most spells, regardless of whether they are cast inside or outside of combat.

Spells blocked by **Magic Immunity** include [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"), direct-damage [Instant Spells](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Instant_Spell "Instant Spell"), and [Combat Instants](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Instant "Combat Instant"). Most [Global](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Global_Enchantment "Global Enchantment") and [Combat Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") cast by rival [Wizards](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") will also ignore the **Magic Immune** unit. There are some exceptions though, these are listed in the [main article](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity#Immunity_to_Harmful_Spells "Magic Immunity").

Spells cast by the **Magic Immune** unit's controller may target and affect it as normal.

### Protection from Attacks

**Magic Immunity** sets the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** for the purposes of defending against [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath Attacks** and [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacks**. It also increases [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** by [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **30** against all [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") except [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch"). This latter feature makes the unit essentially immune to all harmful effects apart from [Poison Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage").

### No Protection

[Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") and [Chaos (Item Power)](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_(Item_Power) "Chaos (Item Power)") [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_(Item_Power) "Chaos (Item Power)")-powered weapons are not hindered by **Magic Immunity** in any way. Non-magical attacks ([Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee**, [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Missile**, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Boulder**, and [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**) will, of course, *not* trigger it either. This is true even if the attacks would otherwise be considered magical (e.g. via [Alchemists' Guild](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemists%27_Guild "Alchemists' Guild") or [Flame Blade](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Flame_Blade "Flame Blade")).

## Usage

**Magic Immunity** may be cast during combat for a base [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **50**. In this case, the spell will only affect the targeted unit until the end of the battle - at which point it will dissipate.

Alternatively, **Magic Immunity** may be cast on the overland map for a lasting effect. In this case it costs [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **250** to cast, and requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **5** per turn to maintain. Failure to pay this Upkeep Cost due to lack of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") will cause the spell to dissipate. However, as long as the spell is paid for (and not dispelled by any rival [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")), the unit continues to enjoy its newfound ability.

Like all other [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment"), **Magic Immunity**
must targeted at a friendly unit, whether in or out of combat. If the
desired target is in an overland army stack, click on that stack to get a
list of units inside it, and then select your desired unit.

Once the spell is in effect, the unit will glow with a [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Blue** aura, unless there is a more powerful spell also affecting it.

If you've placed **Magic Immunity** on a unit, you may remove
it by examining the unit's details and clicking the Enchantment's icon.
The primary reason to do this would be in order to remove the spell's [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"), thus conserving [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** for other spells.

## Acquisition

As a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Sorcery** spell, **Magic Immunity** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**2** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is not guaranteed unless the Wizard acquires at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**10** Spellbooks.

Customized Wizards possessing [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**11**
Spellbooks at creation time may choose this spell as one of their
default spells before starting the game, in which case the spell will
already be researched and available for casting immediately on the first
turn.

Wizards with [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**2** to [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**9** Spellbooks have a random chance of being able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") **Magic Immunity** during the game. The chance for this spell to appear for research increases with the number of [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Sorcery** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay. With [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**10** or [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**11** Spellbooks, the spell is guaranteed to appear for Research at some point, if it is not already available for casting.

**Magic Immunity** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **1,600**.

With at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**2** Spellbooks, the **Magic Immunity** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

**Magic Immunity** is one of the most powerful [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment")
available to any wizard, because it protects a unit against so many
different sources of damage and ill effects. In fact, the only danger to
such a unit will be from [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"), non-magical [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") and the [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** spell [Cracks Call](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cracks_Call "Cracks Call"). Therefore, **Magic Immunity** on an already physically-powerful unit can make it nigh invincible.

**Magic Immunity** is especially potent when attacking armies
belonging to other wizards: those wizards will not be able to target any
of their combat spells at the unit, and thus must resort to defeating
it with brute force. A **Magic Immune** unit is also dreadfully powerful when fighting armies of [Dark Elves](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Elves "Dark Elves"), as they rely heavily on their [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacks**.

## Known Bugs

If **Magic Immunity** is dispelled in combat, the [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") given by it seems to stay for the remainder of that combat, despite the fact the unit no longer has that Enchantment.

Also note that the computer AI won't cast **Magic Immunity** in combat.
