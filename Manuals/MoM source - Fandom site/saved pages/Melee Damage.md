**Melee Damage** is the type of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") delivered by a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack").
When two units are locked in face-to-face combat, the attacker will
deliver this damage to its target. Simultaneously, the target delivers **Melee Damage** against its attacker, in a process called a [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"). The amount of damage delivered is based primarily on each unit's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Attack Strength** as indicated in the unit's details window.

As with all other types of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage"), **Melee Damage** attempts to inflicts actual [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage Points** on the target. To do this, the attacker must make [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** rolls to determine the accuracy of the attack, and the defender may make [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls to try and block some of this incoming damage.

Much of the process is based on random rolls - and many of them. Therefore, a unit will not always deliver the same amount of **Melee Damage** with each [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") it makes. However, statistical calculations can reveal how much damage a unit is *likely* to deliver with each attack, and how much damage it can deliver in an exceptionally lucky attack.

## Effect

**Melee Damage** is a simple type of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage"). This means that it attempts to inflict actual [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage Points** on the target, and involves both [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** rolls and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls to determine the final amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage**.

The ***maximum*** amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** caused to the target is equal to the strength of the [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") itself. This has to be calculated (see [below](#Attack_Strength)) before the damage can be processed.

### Rolling To Hit

With all types of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage"), the attacker is required to make [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** rolls, which determine how many hits are registered against the target. The number of rolls is equal to the strength of the [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") itself. Each roll results in a value between 1 and 100, which is then compared to the attacker's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** score (usually [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30**, before modifiers are taken into account). Each successful roll registers one hit against the target.

### Rolling To Block

The target of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") is then allowed to make [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls, which will help it block or dodge some of these registered hits. The number of rolls is equal to the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score, and again each roll results in a number between 1 and 100, which is compared with the defender's [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** score (usually [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30**, for nearly all units). Each successful roll blocks one of those registered hits.

### Applying Damage

Finally, any registered hits that were not blocked are immediately translated into actual [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** which is applied to the target's current [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Health**. During this process, the target unit may lose one or more of its [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures**, and may end up being completely destroyed as a result.

Since [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") requires both [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls, the actual amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** delivered to the target is almost always lower than the calculated maximum. It is even possible that no actual [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** will result, whether the attacker failed all his/her [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** rolls or the defender managed to succeed in a sufficient number of [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls.

There are also complex rules regarding [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units** making or suffering **Melee Damage**. The attack is stronger the more [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** there are in the attacking unit, and the defender unit has more chance to survive if it contains more [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** as well.

For a complete explanation of how [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") works, including all the rolls it is required to make, see the article on [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage").

### Attack Strength

The total amount of **Melee Damage** delivered by a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") is determined primarily by the [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Attack strength** of the attacking unit. This is indicated by a row of [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Swords** in the unit's details panel.

For each [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Sword**, the unit delivers 1 point of **Melee Damage**. Thus, a unit with [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **3 Swords** deliver 3 points of **Melee Damage** each time it makes a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack").

This becomes somewhat more complex with [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units**, since each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** makes a separate [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") upon the target. For example, a unit with [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **3 Swords** and [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **4 Figures** makes 4 separate [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"), each delivering 3 points of **Melee Damage**. This is not the same as a single attack delivering 12 points of **Melee Damage** (see [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") for a thorough explanation of why this is so).

The calculated Attack Strength indicates the absolute *maximum* amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** that can be caused to the target. Again, due to the [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** rolls made in the process of calculating inflicted damage (see above), the actual results are usually *lower* than this maximum.

## Immunities

**Melee Damage** differs from other types of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") primarily by its delivery method (i.e. through [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack")), but also by the immunities that can help the target block it more effectively.

**Melee Damage** is not blocked by [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") nor [Missile Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Missile_Immunity "Missile Immunity"). However, it will trigger a target's [Weapons Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapons_Immunity "Weapons Immunity") in some specific cases.

When [Weapons Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapons_Immunity "Weapons Immunity") is in fact triggered, it temporarily sets the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score at [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **10**, usually making the target much better at blocking registered hits, and thus usually suffering less [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** as a result. This Defense bonus applies only when blocking **Melee Damage**.

[Weapon Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Immunity "Weapon Immunity") is triggered whenever the attacker delivers **Melee Damage** through a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Normal Melee Attack**. This includes the following:

* Any [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") that *has not* been equipped with [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Magical Weapons** (such as given by the [Alchemy](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemy_(Retort) "Alchemy (Retort)") [Retort](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort") or the [Alchemists' Guild](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Alchemists%27_Guild "Alchemists' Guild")).
* Any [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") that *is not* holding a [Magical Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") of any kind.

[Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") will never trigger a target's [Weapon Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Immunity "Weapon Immunity"), since their weapons are considered "magical" by default (even though this isn't displayed in their details panel).

## Sources of Melee Damage

Virtually all units possess a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"), and this is the only source of **Melee Damage** found in the game.

The amount of **Melee Damage** delivered by a unit is affected by many different factors, and can be adjusted very easily by modifying a unit's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Attack Strength**. Even more importantly, the total amount of **Melee Damage** delivered by a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure unit** can change radically during combat as the unit loses or regains [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures**.
