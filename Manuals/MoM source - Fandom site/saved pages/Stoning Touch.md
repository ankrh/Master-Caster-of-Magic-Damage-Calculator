**Stoning Touch** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*. With this ability, each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in a unit possessing this ability makes a powerful [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that forces the target unit to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resist** or suffer damage equivalent to the full health of one of its own [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures**— effectively thinning that unit's ranks with a petrifying venom.

The strength of a **Stoning Touch** ability is noted after its name (e.g. "**Stoning Touch -3**"). The negative value indicates a temporary penalty applied to the target figure's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score while the attack is taking place. The higher the penalty, the harder it is for the target to survive the attack.

Only one creature in the game possesses **Stoning Touch** by default: [Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices"). It is however possible to bestow a weakened version of this ability on [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"), by equipping them with a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") that has the "Stoning" bonus.

Note that a similar ability exists, [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze"), which makes a single petrifying attack on *each and every* [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the target unit, *prior* to melee action. This gaze attack is furthermore only fired *once* per instance of Melee combat, regardless of how many figures are present in the unit possessing the ability.

## Description

[Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices") may look like overgrown, mutated chickens - but they possess a rather dangerous weapon in the form of a **Stoning Touch** attack. The [Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices")
are essentially venomous, and their venom can instantly turn a living
creature into stone. This transformation is absolutely deadly to the
vast majority of enemies. Fortunately for these enemies, the venom only
has a certain chance of working, and a particularly hardy creature may
be able to survive it without harm. A [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** attuned Wizard can imbue weapons with a *dissolutus* which presents the same basic type of threat.

## Effect

Stoning Touch is automatically used whenever the unit engages in close combat - whether voluntarily making a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") against a target or [Counter Attacking](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") to an enemy assault.

When used, it forces the target unit to make one [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the source unit. To resolve this effect, the game rolls a random number between **1** and **10**. This is then compared to the target's current [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance**
, minus the resistance modifier of the Stoning Touch, if any. If the
rolled number is higher than the modified Resistance score, the target
unit is struck for [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") [Irreversible Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points#Irreversible_Damage_Points "Damage Points") equal to the full [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** of one of its [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures**, slaying one figure instantly and possibly inflicting further [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** to the next figure in line.

The modifier to the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score is equal to the strength of the Stoning Touch attack, as noted after the name of the ability. Thus, **Stoning Touch -3** gives a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-3 Resistance**
penalty to the target. This penalty applies only for the purpose of
this specific attack, and is removed once the attack is over.

#### Timing within the Melee Attack

:   As a [Melee Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack#Melee_Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), Stoning Touch is executed simultaneously with the unit's [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"); thus, [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** destroyed by a [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") **Gaze Attacks** or Ability FirstStrike [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike")
    will not deliver Stoning Touch, and figures destroyed by Stoning Touch
    will still deliver damage unless the unit is attacking and has Ability FirstStrike [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike").

#### Immunity

:   Units with [Stoning Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Immunity "Stoning Immunity") and/or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") are impervious to Stoning Touch. They do not need to make any [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, instead completely ignoring the attack.

:   Also, any unit with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** remaining after all factors have been computed, as well as any [Charmed](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Charmed "Charmed") hero, is rendered effectively invulnerable to Stoning Touch by virtue of its resistance score.

#### Example

:   A unit of [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **4 Cockatrices** engages a unit of [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **2 War Bears**. The **War Bears** must make *four* resistance rolls, one for each **Cockatrice**. Each time a roll fails, the unit is struck for the full health of a single **War Bear** figure, or [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **8 Damage**.

:   In point of contrast, when exposed to the [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") of [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **4 Gorgons**, the bears will only make *two* resistance rolls— one for each **War Bear**.

## Units with Default Stoning Touch

There is only one unit that possesses **Stoning Touch** by default:

[Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices") [Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices")

The [Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices") have **Stoning Touch -3**, which means that they inflict a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-3 Resistance** penalty on the target when using this ability. This makes it very difficult for low-to-mid tier units to resist the effect. **Stoning Touch -3** is the strongest known **Stoning Touch** in the game.

## Acquiring Stoning Touch

It is possible to add **Stoning Touch** to pretty much any [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") unit, and furthermore, to add a **Ranged** dimension to the ability. This is accomplished by imbuing a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") with an [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power") called **"Stoning"** (or "Petrifying" in the game manual), and equipping that item on a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero").

The **"Stoning"** bonus can only be added to [Swords](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Maces](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace"), [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe"), [Wands](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand"), [Staves](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff") and [Bows](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow"). During [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting"), the "Stoning" bonus is only available if you possess at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**2 Nature** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). Of course, it is always possible to find an item already imbued with this bonus as a reward for defeating an [Encounter](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter "Encounter").

The **Stoning Touch** given by a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") has a strength of -1 (it gives a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-1 Resistance** penalty to the target), making it significantly weaker than the **Stoning Touch** ability of [Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices"), but still quite useful against low-[Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") enemies. The **Stoning Touch** icon does not appear in the **Hero's**
details panel while holding such an item. The property is also
transient: the hero only possesses this ability while holding the
weapon, and will lose it immediately if the item is ever removed or
lost.

**"Stoning"** is a weapon-derived power. It may only appear on a magical weapon as mentioned above, and only triggers a **Stoning Touch** during the type of attack for which that weapon is designed. These are, respectively:

* **[Bow](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow")**: [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack**
* **[Staff](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff"), [Wand](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand")**: [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attack**
* **[Mace](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace"), [Sword](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword")**: [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee**
* **[Axe](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe")**: [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee** *and* [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attack** (if applicable)
