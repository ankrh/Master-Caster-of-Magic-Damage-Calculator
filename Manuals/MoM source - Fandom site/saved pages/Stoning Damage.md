**Stoning Damage** is a type of [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") that can be delivered by [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"), [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), and a specific combat spell. An attack delivering **Stoning Damage** stands a good chance of killing one or more enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** outright, regardless of the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score.

When a target is struck by **Stoning Damage**, it does not make any [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** rolls against it. Instead, each affected [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the target unit makes a single [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll. The result of this roll determines whether the figure is killed instantly or left completely unharmed.

**Stoning Damage** is commonly delivered by several [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") from the [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** and [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** realms, most of whom deliver it by way of a [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") ([Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze")), and one by way of a [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") ([Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch")). [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") may also add a **Stoning Damage** (Touch Attack) component to their [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee**, [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack")/[Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack")/[Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged**, or [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attacks** by equipping a [Magical Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") that contains the "[Stoning](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_(Item_Power) "Stoning (Item Power)")" enchantment.

Finally, the [Petrify](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Petrify "Petrify") spell delivers a **Stoning Damage** attack against every [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in a target enemy unit on the battlefield.

## Effect

**Stoning Damage** forces its target to make one or more [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls - with each failed roll killing off one [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the enemy unit. **Stoning Damage** delivered by units behaves somewhat differently depending on whether it is delivered by a [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") or a [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack").

### Resistance and Death

Whenever an attack containing a **Stoning Damage** component strikes any target on the battlefield, each affected [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure**
within the target unit must make a single die roll - resulting in a
random number between 1 and 10. This number is then compared to the
target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score (with possible modifiers, which are explained below).

The number of affected [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** depends on the type of the ability. For [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"), one [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance roll** is made for each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the *target* unit. For [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), on the other hand, one [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **roll** is made for each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the *attacking* unit (i.e. the unit performing the Touch Attack).

Each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** that rolls a number *higher than* its unit's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score is immediately killed off - regardless of how many [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** it has remaining or how high its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score is. The figure is simply destroyed. If all Figures in the unit have failed their [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls simultaneously this way, then the entire unit is destroyed.

Figures that roll *equal to or lower than* their units' [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** are completely unharmed by this **Stoning Damage**.

#### Resistance Modifiers

:   Each attack that delivers **Stoning Damage** inflicts a certain penalty on the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score. The stronger this penalty, the less chance each target [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** has to "win" the roll and avert its own death.

:   The [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **Resistance penalty** varies from attacker to attacker, with the "strongest" penalty being that inflicted by a [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")'s attacks: [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-4 Resistance**! This attack has no problem affecting units with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **4** or less (it will completely destroy them on the spot!), and can occasionally affect targets with Resistance scores as high as [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **13**.

:   The weakest **Stoning Damage** attack is that delivered by the [Petrify](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Petrify "Petrify") spell, which gives no [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **Resistance penalty** whatsoever.

## Immunities

**Stoning Damage** will never hurt any unit possessing [Stoning Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Immunity "Stoning Immunity") [Stoning Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Immunity "Stoning Immunity"). Such units ignore the effects of any **Stoning Damage** attack. Similarly, **Stoning Damage** is also entirely blocked by [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity").

Finally, since the weakest [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **Resistance penalty** applied by **Stoning Damage** is [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **0**, any unit with a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** or more may be completely invulnerable to **Stoning Damage** - it cannot possibly fail its Resistance roll. Of course, it's always important to remember that most **Stoning Damage** attacks inflict a greater penalty; if the penalty lowers the target's Resistance below [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10**, the attack has at least some chance to hurt that target.

Note that these immunities will only block the **Stoning Damage** component of an attack. If the attack also delivers any [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"), [Ranged Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Damage "Ranged Damage") or any other type of damage, that damage may or may not go through the target's immunities unimpeded.

## Sources of Stoning Damage

**Stoning Damage** is a relatively common [Damage Type](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Type "Damage Type"), at least compared to similar types such as [Death Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Damage "Death Damage"). Its primary sources are units possessing either [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") or [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") - and there are 4 such units in the game. Furthermore, one spell delivers **Stoning Damage** directly, and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") may equip themselves with a [Magical Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") containing the "[Stoning](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_(Item_Power) "Stoning (Item Power)")" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power").

### Units with Stoning Gaze

:   *Main article: [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze")*

The [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") ability is the more common source of **Stoning Damage**. Whenever a unit possessing this ability makes a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), it will first execute a separate [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") that delivers one **Stoning Damage** attack for each living enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure**.

A [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") occurs *before* the exchange of [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") between the attacker and the target. As a result, any enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** killed by the **Stoning Damage** do not get a chance to cause any [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"). Thus, [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") protects the attacker by reducing the number of enemy Figures that retaliate against it. It may, potentially, kill the *entire* enemy unit outright, and thus end the combat round before the target can deliver any damage at all!

The strength of a [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") ability is very important: it indicates how much [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **Resistance penalty** is inflicted on the target. The greater the penalty, the greater the chance of killing each enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure**.

### Units with Stoning Touch

:   *Main article: [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch")*

The [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") ability is very rare - possessed only by a single unit (the [Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices")). Whenever a unit possessing this ability makes a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), each living [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** of it will also execute a separate [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") that delivers **Stoning Damage**.

A [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") occurs *simuntaneously* with the exchange of [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") between the two units. This means that the target gets to deliver its own [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") and/or [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") before it loses any [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** to the ensuing **Stoning Damage**. As a result, [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") offers no protection to the unit using it.

The [Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices")' [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") ability applies a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **Resistance penalty** of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-3** while affecting the target, so targets need at least [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **13** to shrug it off every time. Targets with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **3** or less are always completely destroyed.

### Stoning Weapons

A further source of **Stoning Damage** comes from weapons imbued with the "[Stoning](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_(Item_Power) "Stoning (Item Power)")" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power"). This enchantment can be placed only on weapons ([Swords](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Maces](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace"), [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe"), [Bows](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow"), [Wands](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand"), and [Staves](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff")).

When the enchanted item is equipped on a Hero, it adds a **Stoning Damage** ([Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack")) component to the attack associated with the item. Therefore, [Swords](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Maces](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace"), and [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe") add **Stoning Damage** to the Hero's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Attacks** (and [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Counter Attacks**); while [Bows](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow"), [Wands](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand"), and [Staves](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff") only add **Stoning Damage** to the Hero's [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack")/[Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Attacks**. [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe") further add **Stoning Damage** to [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attacks**, if applicable.

The **Stoning Damage** delivered by a Hero's attack always inflicts a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") penalty of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-1** on the target.

During the custom [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting") process, the "[Stoning](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_(Item_Power) "Stoning (Item Power)")" Power is only available to [Wizards](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") possessing at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**2 Nature** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). Naturally, it's always possible to find a weapon containing this bonus as a reward for defeating [Encounters](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter "Encounter").

The Hero's attack will only deliver **Stoning Damage** while the Hero is holding the enchanted item. If the item is removed or lost, the [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero")'s attack no longer delivers **Stoning Damage**.

### The Petrify Spell

:   *Main article: [Petrify](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Petrify "Petrify")*

[Petrify](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Petrify "Petrify") is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Combat Instant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Instant "Combat Instant") belonging to the [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature Realm**. When cast upon an enemy unit on the battlefield, it will deliver a single **Stoning Damage** attack against each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** of that target.

The spell has a basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **35**, making it only mildly expensive. It is worth the price when cast at low-[Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance**, [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure** targets, where it can do a lot of damage. It is often way too expensive to cast at powerful [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure units**, but can sometimes have a chance of killing such a dangerous opponent outright, so keep it as a last-ditch option.

Since [Petrify](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Petrify "Petrify") inflicts no [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **Resistance penalty** on its target, units need only [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** to be completely immune against this **Stoning Damage**.
