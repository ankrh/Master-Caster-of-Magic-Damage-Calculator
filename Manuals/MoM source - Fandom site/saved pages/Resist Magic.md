**Resist Magic** is a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") of the [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"). For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **25** it may be cast on a unit on the overland map to give it a conditional [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+5** bonus, which applies whenever the unit is attempting to resist any magical effects. The spell must then be maintained with an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1** per turn.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **5**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same bonus
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
Upkeep Costs).

## Effects

**Resist Magic** protects a unit against malicious spells and effects.

### Resistance Bonus

Whenever the enchanted unit is required to make a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") roll in order to avoid an ill-effect originating from any spell or [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack"), it may do so with a bonus of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+5**.
This makes the unit much harder to affect with such spells, as it
increases the chance of succeeding the Resistance roll. The only
condition is that the effect must be associated with one of the magical [Realms](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"). This includes practically everything that can be [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resisted**, with one single exception: [Poison Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage").

Any unit brought up to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** thanks to this bonus is immune to any spell effect that does not inflict its own penalties on [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") scores. Units already possessing [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** or higher can be rendered totally immune to even the most powerful spell effects.

Because the bonus is conditional, the unit's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score is not visibly altered in the *unit statistics window*. As such, it may be important to check enemy units for the **Resist Magic** effect before deciding whether it would be a good idea to use ill effects against it.

## Usage

**Resist Magic** may be cast both overland and in combat, for the basic [Casting Costs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **25** and [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **5**
respectively. If the casting is successful, the game will prompt for a
friendly unit to be selected as the spell's target. On the overland map,
this typically requires clicking on the army stack that contains the
unit which, in turn, brings up a list view of all units in that stack,
allowing for the target to be picked individually. Only units not
already under the effect of **Resist Magic** may be chosen. Once the spell is cast, a glowing [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[blue](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** outline appears around the unit (unless it is overridden by a stronger enchantment); and **Resist Magic**, along with its icon, will be displayed in the *unit statistics window* when inspecting this unit.

The overland version of **Resist Magic** has an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1**
that must be paid at the beginning of every turn to keep the spell in
effect. This is automatically deducted by the game from the owner's [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** pool as long as the enchantment remains in existence. If there is insufficient [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**
in the pool to pay for the spell, then it will dissipate, ceasing its
effect immediately. It may also be cancelled manually by left-clicking
its name in the *unit statistics window* of its target unit. However, this action can only be performed outside of combat, during the caster's own turn.

The combat version of **Resist Magic**
does not carry over to the overland map: it will disappear
automatically as soon as the battle is over. As such, it has no Upkeep
Cost; but at the same time may not be manually removed by its caster
either (as enchantments can not be cancelled during a battle).

## Acquisition

As a [Common Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") of the [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), **Resist Magic** may become available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") who acquires at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**1** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
With none, the spell can naturally not be learned during the campaign.
This is the minimum requirement for it to be traded for, appear in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be found in the spoils of victory when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival Wizard who already knows it.

The base chance for **Resist Magic** to be [researchable](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research "Spell Research") (at some point) in the campaign is roughly 30% (with [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**1** book), which gradually increases with the amount of [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** Spellbooks possessed or found during gameplay. With [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**7** or more, the spell is certain to show up sooner or later, unless acquired from another source. It has a [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research#Research_Cost "Spell Research") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **20**, although its research is quicker for Wizards possessing the [Sage Master](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sage_Master "Sage Master"), and/or [Sorcery Mastery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery_Mastery "Sorcery Mastery") [Retorts](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort"); or a bookshelf containing [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**8** or more.

**Resist Magic** may also be selected as a starting spell by any Wizard with at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**2** books. In this case, it will be available for casting as soon as the game begins.

In addition, **Resist Magic** is available as a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") spell. It is known by [Alorra the Elven Archer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Alorra_the_Elven_Archer "Alorra the Elven Archer"), who can cast it at least once per battle even at her initial [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level").

## Item Power

**Resist Magic** can also be duplicated as an [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power") and imbued onto a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item"). This requires either the [Arcane](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Arcane "Arcane")**[Enchant Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Enchant_Item "Enchant Item")** or the [Arcane](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Arcane "Arcane")**[Create Artifact](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Create_Artifact "Create Artifact")** spells, and has the advantages of being non-dispellable, and requiring no [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"). While enchanting [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Resist Magic](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resist_Magic_(Item_Power) "Resist Magic (Item Power)")** onto an item can't be done unless the [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") possesses at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[2](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"), knowledge of the actual spell is *not necessary*. Selecting this Power increases the item's total value by [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **200**.

[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Resist Magic](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resist_Magic_(Item_Power) "Resist Magic (Item Power)")** can be enchanted onto any type of Magical Item. Naturally, this means that it is available to any [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"), as all of them possess at least one [ItemSlot Misc](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/d/d8/ItemSlot_Misc.png/revision/latest?cb=20120702054432) miscellaneous [Equipment Slot](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item#Equipment_Slots "Magical Item").
If an item with this Power is created and given to a Hero, then they
will benefit from the effect described above for as long as they keep
the item equipped. Since [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Resist Magic](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resist_Magic_(Item_Power) "Resist Magic (Item Power)")** is present on [12](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resist_Magic_(Item_Power)#Overland "Resist Magic (Item Power)") pre-fabricated Magical Items, it is also possible to occasionally find an item already imbued with this Power in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be offered one for purchase by a wandering [Merchant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant "Merchant").

## Strategy

**Resist Magic** is useful when combating wizards who possess and often use [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse") or other negative spells that require their targets to make a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") roll. It may also save a unit when fighting against monsters that inflict [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage").

## Known Bugs

The in-game tooltip for **Resist Magic** indicates that it gives the unit [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+3 Resistance**. This is incorrect: the actual bonus is [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+5 Resistance**, as indicated in the game's *Official Strategy Guide*.
