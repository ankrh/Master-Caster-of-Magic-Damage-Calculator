**Doom Damage** is a type of [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") that can be delivered by various spells, as well as by various units as part of their [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") and/or [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"). An attack delivering **Doom Damage** will cause an exact amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** to the target.

**Doom Damage** is the simplest type of damage. When inflicting **Doom Damage**, the attacker does not need to make any [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**
rolls - it automatically inflicts a number of hits equal to the
strength of the attack. Similarly, the defender may not make any [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** rolls to try and block any of this damage. As a result, the target suffers a number of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** points exactly equal to the strength of the **Doom Damage** attack.

**Doom Damage** is not as rare as its strength might indicate. It is delivered by several types of spells - primarily [Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt") and [Magic Vortex](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Vortex "Magic Vortex"). [Chaos Spawns](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn"), possessing the [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") ability, also deliver **Doom Damage** as part of their devastating attacks. [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") may also add a **Doom Damage** component to their [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") by equipping a [Magical Melee Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item")
that contains the "Chaos" enchantment (though this halves the strength
of their attacks, as explained below). Other than these, no other units
or attacks will deliver **Doom Damage**.

## Effect

**Doom Damage** has the simplest effect of any [Damage Type](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Type "Damage Type"): it inflicts an exact amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** on the target, completely unaffected by any [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**, [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** or [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls.

### Exact Damage

Whenever an attack containing a **Doom Damage** component strikes any target on the battlefield, it inflicts an amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** on that target identical to the strength of the attack itself. For example, an attack of strength 10 delivering **Doom Damage** will inflict exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **10 points of Damage** on the target.

There are no [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** rolls involved in determining how many hits are successfully inflicted, so the attacker's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** score is irrelevant. Similarly, the target is not allowed to make any [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** or [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, and thus cannot reduce the number of hits suffered.

As a result, the amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** inflicted by a **Doom Damage** attack does not change unless the strength of the attack itself changes.

## Immunities

Only two sources of **Doom Damage** can be blocked, namely the [Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt") spell and the damage a [Magic Vortex](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Vortex "Magic Vortex") does when passing over a unit. Units possessing the [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") ability will never be harmed by these spells at all. This is also true for all units enchanted with [Righteousness](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Righteousness "Righteousness") or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Spell) "Magic Immunity (Spell)").

## Sources of Doom Damage

There are several [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** spells that deliver **Doom Damage**. One unit (the [Unit Icon ChaosSpawn Transparent](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/9/9a/Unit_Icon_ChaosSpawn_Transparent.png/revision/latest?cb=20120407155401)[Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")) uses a [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") attack, and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") can be equipped with [Magical Weapons](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") that deliver this type of damage.

### Doom Bolt Spell

:   *Main article: [Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt")*, *[Doom Bolt Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt_Spell "Doom Bolt Spell")*

[Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt") [Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt") is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Combat Instant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Instant "Combat Instant") belonging to the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos Realm**. For a base cost of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40**, it delivers a strength-10 **Doom Damage** attack against any target on the battlefield. As explained above, this causes exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **10 points of Damage** to the target. If the target has [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") however, the spell has no effect against it (entirely wasting the [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** spent on casting the spell).

The [Warlocks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Warlocks "Warlocks") unit possesses an ability [Ability DoomBoltSpell](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/1/1e/Ability_DoomBoltSpell.png/revision/latest?cb=20120409142459) [Doom Bolt Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt_Spell "Doom Bolt Spell"), which allows it to cast [Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt") once per battle, without spending any [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** whatsoever. This version of the spell is identical in effect to the regular [Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt") described above.

### Doom Gaze

:   *Main article: [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze")*

The [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") ability is a powerful source of **Doom Damage**, possessed by only one unit in the game: the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn"). Whenever the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") makes a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), it will first execute a separate [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") that delivers **Doom Damage**. This attack has a strength of 4 (boosted to 6 in Chaos Nodes), and thus inflicts exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4 points of Damage** on the target, as explained above in this article.

A [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") occurs *before* the exchange of [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") between the attacker and the target. As a result, any enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** killed by the **Doom Damage** do not get a chance of cause any [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") nor [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") against the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn"). Thus, [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") protects the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") by reducing the number of enemy Figures that retaliate against it. It may, potentially, kill the *entire* enemy unit outright, and thus end the combat round before the target can deliver any damage at all!

### Magic Vortex

If a [Magic Vortex](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Vortex "Magic Vortex") [Magic Vortex](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Vortex "Magic Vortex") passes over any unit, it will deliver [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **5** points of **Doom Damage** to that target. Note, that a Magic Vortex may also strike adjacent units, but this will deal no **Doom Damage**.

### Chaos Weapons

The final source of **Doom Damage** comes from weapons imbued with the [ItemPower Chaos](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/6/61/ItemPower_Chaos.gif/revision/latest?cb=20130819233759) "Chaos" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power"). This enchantment can be placed only on weapons - including [Swords](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Maces](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace"), [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe"), [Bows](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow"), [Wands](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand"), and [Staves](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff").

When a [Sword](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Mace](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace") or [Axe](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe") with the "Chaos" enchantment is equipped, the Hero's [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") and [Counter Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") will deliver **Doom Damage** *instead* of regular [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"). Similarly, a [Bow](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow"), [Wand](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand") or [Staff](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff") enchanted with "Chaos" cause the Hero's [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") to deliver **Doom Damage** *instead* of regular [Ranged Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Damage "Ranged Damage").

This is extremely powerful, as it renders the Hero's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**
score meaningless - inflicting the same amount of damage on the target
with each and every attack, even when directed against the more
heavily-armored opponents in the game.

*On the other hand*, the "Chaos" enchantment has another important effect: it *halves* the strength of the Hero's attack, and rounds it down. For example, if the Hero normally has a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") of strength [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **9**, when he/she equips a "Chaos" weapon his/her attack will now deliver 4 **Doom Damage** - causing exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4 Damage** to any target. This attack doesn't have the potential of the Hero's original attack (which would deliver up to [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **9 Damage**), but is far more dependable and ignores the target's armor to boot. Overall, this is quite a worthwhile tradeoff!

During the custom [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting") process, the "Chaos" bonus is only available if you possess at least [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**6 Chaos** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). Naturally, it's always possible to find a weapon containing this bonus as a reward for defeating [Encounters](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter "Encounter").

The Hero's attack will only deliver **Doom Damage** while the Hero is holding the enchanted item. If the item is removed or lost, the [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero")'s attack no longer delivers **Doom Damage** - but its strength returns to normal as well.

### Black Sleep

The effect of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Black Sleep](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Sleep "Black Sleep")** can also be linked to **Doom Damage**. If a unit was cursed by Black Sleep, the unit is not only unable to attack: all incoming [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") (attacks checked against [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense**) will also be converted to **Doom Damage** - dealing maximum damage and ignoring immunities. For example, if a [UnitEnchantment BlackSleep](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/8/88/UnitEnchantment_BlackSleep.png/revision/latest?cb=20120302173549)**sleeping** unit is attacked by an unexperienced unit of Bowmen ([Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **1** attack strength, [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **6** figures), the Bowmen will inflict exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **6 damage** points - ignoring stuff like [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll"), [Defense Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll"), ranged penalties and [Missile Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Missile_Immunity "Missile Immunity"). However, the sleeping unit is still able to defend against [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") (attacks checked against [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance**) as usual.
