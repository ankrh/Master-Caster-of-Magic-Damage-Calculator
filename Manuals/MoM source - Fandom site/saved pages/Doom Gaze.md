[Ability DoomGaze](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/c/c2/Ability_DoomGaze.png/revision/latest?cb=20120205050629)

**Doom Gaze** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*. With this ability, a unit can make a powerful [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that bypasses almost any type of defense. This attack will, almost always, inflict a set amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** on its target.

The strength of a **Doom Gaze** ability is noted after its name. This indicates the amount of damage delivered by the attack.

Only one unit in the game possesses **Doom Gaze** by default: the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn"). No other unit will ever be able to acquire **Doom Gaze** by any known method.

## Description

One of the many eyes of the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") is capable of shooting a special form of beam made out of refined [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos**
energy. The intensity of the beam is such that it can go through pretty
much any object, and is wide enough to hit any target - making this
beam virtually impossible to avoid or block. The same principle can be
used by spellcasters to create a [Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt"), but the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") does this "naturally" - it is simply a part of its bizarre physical form.

## Effect

**Doom Gaze** is automatically used whenever the unit engages in Melee combat - whether voluntarily making a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") against any target, or when [Counter Attacking](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") to an enemy assault.

When triggered, **Doom Gaze** makes a single [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") against the target. This attack delivers [Doom Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Damage "Doom Damage"), which means that the target does not get to make *any* [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** rolls nor [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls in order to reduce it.

The strength of the **Doom Gaze** attack is defined after the name of the ability. Thus, **Doom Gaze** 4 (such as the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") possesses) will deliver exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4 points of Damage** to the target. Since the target has no means of blocking or averting this damage, it will always suffer exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4**.

As a [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"), **Doom Gaze** is executed prior to the exchange of [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") between the attacker and the target - simultaneously with damage from [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike"), if applicable, but coming *after* any [Breath Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Breath_Attack "Breath Attack") or [Thrown Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"). Any [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** in the target unit that are killed by the **Doom Gaze** do not get to deliver any [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") nor any damage from their [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), if they have one.

Note that no immunity in the game helps specifically against **Doom Gaze**, making it useful against pretty much any target.

## Units with Default Doom Gaze

There is only one unit that possesses **Doom Gaze** by default:

[Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")

The [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") has *several* [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") abilities, with **Doom Gaze** being one of the most potent since it cannot be blocked by [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** or [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls. [Chaos Spawns](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") have a **Doom Gaze** of strength 4, which means they deliver exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4 points of Damage** to their target.

## Acquiring Doom Gaze

There is no known method of acquiring **Doom Gaze**
for any unit that does not possess it by default. As a result, the only
unit that will have this ability during an unmodded game is the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn"). However, [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") may be able to acquire a similar attack. See [Doom Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Damage "Doom Damage") for more information.

## Modifying Doom Gaze

There are three known effects that can change a unit's **Doom Gaze** strength: The [Chaos Node](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Node "Chaos Node")'s aura will raise it, when appropriate, [Chaos Surge](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Surge "Chaos Surge") too, while the spells [Black Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Prayer "Black Prayer") and [Mind Storm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mind_Storm "Mind Storm") will decrease it.

When a [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") is fighting inside or within the vicinity of a [Chaos Node](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Node "Chaos Node"), the aura from the node raises many of its properties by +2, as it would for any other [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") from the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** realm. In addition to basic property bonuses, the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")'s **Doom Gaze** strength is also increased by +2, to a total of 6. This means that the **Doom Gaze** will deliver exactly [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **6 points of Damage** instead of the original [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4**. The bonus applies *only* while the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") is fighting near a [Chaos Node](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Node "Chaos Node"), it is not permanent.

In a similar manner, the [Global Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Global_Enchantment "Global Enchantment") [Chaos Surge](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Surge "Chaos Surge") will improve units associated with the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** realm, as long it is in effect. This effect is cumulative to the aura of [Chaos Nodes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Node "Chaos Node").

Conversely, The [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") [Black Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Prayer "Black Prayer") will reduce most enemy unit properties by 1. This includes **Doom Gaze**.

Whenever the [Mind Storm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mind_Storm "Mind Storm") spell successfully affects a [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn"), its **Doom Gaze** strength is reduced by -5, which normally gives it 0 **Doom Gaze** strength. If this is the case, the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") will not inflict any [Doom Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Damage "Doom Damage") at all. Moreover, its [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze") and [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") will stop working, making the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") harmless.

If a [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") is affected by [Mind Storm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mind_Storm "Mind Storm") while fighting in the vicinity of a [Chaos Node](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Node "Chaos Node"), both effects apply together. Thus, the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") will end up with a **Doom Gaze** strength of 4 + 2 - 5 = 1. It will thus inflict only 1 [Doom Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Damage "Doom Damage") point.
