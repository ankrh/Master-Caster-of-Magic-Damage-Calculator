[Ability MagicImmunity](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/c/c7/Ability_MagicImmunity.png/revision/latest?cb=20120125011449)

**Magic Immunity** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*. It is arguably the most powerful immunity in the game.

A unit with **Magic Immunity**
will shrug off the vast majority of enemy spells cast directly at it -
being completely unaffected by them. Many indirect spells will also fail
to affect the unit.

Furthermore, whenever a unit with **Magic Immunity** is struck by [Magical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Damage "Magical Damage") of any kind, its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") rating is temporarily raised to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** for purposes of averting that damage. Almost all [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") will fail to do any harm to the **Magic-Immune** unit. Only [Poison Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage") and certain types of [Doom Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Damage "Doom Damage") can get through.

**Magic Immunity** is a rather rare type of immunity possessed by only 5 different units by default. 3 of these are [Champions](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Champion "Champion"), 1 is a [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit"), and one is a [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit").

**Magic Immunity** can be added to any unit thanks to the [Magic Immunity Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Spell) "Magic Immunity (Spell)"). [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") can also obtain the same ability by holding a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") imbued with the "[Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Item_Power) "Magic Immunity (Item Power)")" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power").

## Description

In a world where magic is quite commonplace, it's not surprising that
some creatures and skilled warriors have developed an immunity to
magic. Whether it is an innate property that's part of a creature's
nature, acquired by divine intervention, or simply a magical effect in
itself, **Magic Immunity** is one of the most potent types of protection a creature could ask for.

The anti-magical field surrounding the creature nullifies only
adverse magical energy. It does not prevent the creature from being
affected by benign magic.

## Effect

**Magic Immunity** is a
passive ability that protects a unit from a very wide variety of spells,
spell effects, and even direct damage that's based on a magical source.

There are many consequences to this immunity, and each is discussed separately below.

### Immunity to Harmful Spells

First and foremost, **Magic Immunity**
is designed to prevent harmful spells from affecting the unit. This
includes most enemy spells, as well as friendly spells that could
potentially hurt it.

On the overland map, the only spell that could adversely affect a unit with **Magic Immunity** is the [Stasis](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stasis "Stasis")
spell. All other harmful spells will do no harm to the unit, regardless
of how powerful they are or how they are targeted. Only beneficial
effects will apply to the unit, so it is still possible to cast [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") upon it. The unit will also enjoy beneficial effects from other magical sources, such as [Global Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Global_Enchantment "Global Enchantment").

During combat, the majority of harmful spells - especially those
targeted directly at the unit - will cause it no harm whatsoever. This
includes all spells that deliver direct damage - they will cause *none*.

Note that the game will not object when trying to cast a spell at a **Magic-Immune** unit that will fail to harm it, like it would for example object to cast a [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Sorcery** spell at a unit with [Illusion Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusion_Immunity "Illusion Immunity"). In other words, if you cast an "invalid" spell at a unit with **Magic Immunity**, the spell will not ask for a different target, and instead will simply result in a complete waste of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**.

#### Exceptions

:   Whether due to logical reasons or due to developer oversight, several spells *can* be used to harm a **Magic-Immune**
    unit. Some of them simply work by targeting the tile underneath the
    unit rather than the unit itself, targeting its weapons, indirectly
    affecting the battlefield in some way, and so forth.

:   The following spells will apply their normal effects to a **Magic-Immune** unit just like any other unit:

:   [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life") [High Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/High_Prayer "High Prayer")
:   [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature") [Cracks Call](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cracks_Call "Cracks Call")
:   [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature") [Earth to Mud](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Earth_to_Mud "Earth to Mud")
:   [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature") [Entangle](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Entangle "Entangle")
:   [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature") [Web](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Web "Web")

### Immunity to Magical Ranged Attacks

**Magic Immunity** also protects the unit from any and all [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacks**.

The protection against such attacks comes in the form of temporarily raising the **Magic-Immune** unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** for the purposes of blocking this damage. An exceptionally-powerful [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attack**
may be able to overcome such a massively-high Defense rating, but even
so would be extremely hard-pressed to do more than a few points of
damage - if any.

Note that the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score is only raised for the purposes of blocking damage from [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacks**, and is reduced back to its original value once the attack is concluded.

#### Exceptions

:   If the [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacker** possesses the [Illusion](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusion "Illusion") ability, whether innately or through a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item"), his/her attacks deliver [Illusion Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusion_Damage "Illusion Damage"), which prevents the target from making any [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** rolls.

:   As a result, the **Magic-Immune** target's impressive [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** is *overridden* by the [Illusion Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusion_Damage "Illusion Damage") effect: instead of making 50 Defense rolls, the target makes *no defense rolls*, suffering as much damage as the attacker manages to inflict. Provided, of course, that the **Magic-Immune** unit doesn't also have [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity").

:   Furthermore, [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") wielding a [Staff](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff") or [Wand](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand") enchanted with the [Chaos (Item Power)](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_(Item_Power) "Chaos (Item Power)") [Chaos Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_(Item_Power) "Chaos (Item Power)") will deal their [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical** damage as normal to a **Magic-Immune** unit.

### Immunity to Special Attacks

Aside from [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacks**, **Magic Immunity** also protects the unit from the majority of [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") and [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") types. For [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath Attacks**, it provides the same protection as described above for [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacks**. For most other Special Attacks, the unit will suffer no harm whatsoever.

The only [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that ***do not*** trigger **Magic Immunity**'s protection are:

* [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attacks**,
* [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch"), and
* [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze").

All other attacks will cause no harm to the **Magic-Immune** unit in any way. The unit automatically succeeds any [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll the attack might force it to make, and will not suffer any damage whatsoever.

## Acquiring Magic Immunity

There are two ways to add **Magic Immunity** to a unit that does not possess it by default:

* Casting the [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Spell) "Magic Immunity (Spell)") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") on the unit.
* Equipping a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with an item imbued with the "[Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Item_Power) "Magic Immunity (Item Power)")" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power").

Both of these methods add the same kind of **Magic Immunity** described above in this article.

### Magic Immunity Unit Enchantment

[Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Spell) "Magic Immunity (Spell)") is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") belonging to the [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Sorcery** realm. When cast on a unit, it will add the **Magic Immunity** ability to that unit.

When cast on the overland map, [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Spell) "Magic Immunity (Spell)") has a basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **250**. It has a lingering effect, so that the targeted unit will keep its **Magic Immunity** ability so long as the spell continues to exist. While it exists, the spell requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **5** to be paid at the start of each turn.

Alternatively, it is possible to cast [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Spell) "Magic Immunity (Spell)") during combat as an impromptu [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment"). In this case the spell's basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") is only [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **50**,
but its effects will only last until the end of the battle (or until
dispelled by the enemy). Since neither the spell nor the effect will
carry over to the overland map, there is no associated [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") with this version of the spell.

### Magic Immunity Item Power

A permanent version of the [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Spell) "Magic Immunity (Spell)") spell can be imbued into any type of [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item"). When a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") wears this item, he/she receives the **Magic Immunity** ability as described in this article.

During [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting"), it is only possible to select the "[Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity_(Item_Power) "Magic Immunity (Item Power)")" Power if you possess at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**5 Sorcery** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
Otherwise, "Magic Immunity" is not available. Of course, it is always
possible to find an item already enchanted with "Magic Immunity" as
reward for defeating an [Encounter](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter "Encounter").

The **Magic Immunity** ability bestowed by an item is transient: it will disappear if the Hero ever removes or loses the enchanted item.
