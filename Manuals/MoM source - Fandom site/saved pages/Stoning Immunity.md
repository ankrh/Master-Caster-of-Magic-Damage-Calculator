**Stoning Immunity** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*.

A unit with **Stoning Immunity** is completely unaffected by any attack or spell that inflicts [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage"). When such an attack strikes the unit, it does absolutely nothing to that unit.

**Stoning Immunity** is a fairly-common immunity, found on a wide variety of [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") from different realms. It is not possible to add **Stoning Immunity** to a unit that does not already have it.

## Description

Several creatures and spells are capable of turning a living creature
into a stone statue. This is a terrible fate, and the intensity of
magic involved often makes it very difficult to avert. Fortunately for
some creatures, their physical nature prevents any such effect from
occurring.

In particular, creatures already made of stone are obviously
immune. Elementals, comprised entirely of one material, are not actually
alive to begin with. Some creatures possess such affinity with earth
and rock that they simply shrug off the attack. Finally, some have no
physical presence to begin with, and thus there is nothing there to be
turned into stone.

## Effect

**Stoning Immunity** is a passive ability that protects a unit specifically from [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage").

Normally, attacks that deliver [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage") force the target to make [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, and outright killing any [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** that fails that roll. Harsh penalties are often applied to the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score to further reduce its chance of surviving.

The **Stoning-Immune** unit, on the other hand, has absolutely nothing to fear from such effects. It will never have to make any [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls against them, and will not suffer any harm.

There are currently only 4 known sources of [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage"):

* The [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") ability, possessed by [Cockatrices](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cockatrices "Cockatrices").
* The [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") ability, possessed by [Basilisks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk"), [Gorgons](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gorgons "Gorgons") and [Chaos Spawns](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn").
* [Petrify](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Petrify "Petrify"), a [Combat Instant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Instant "Combat Instant") from the [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature Realm**.
* A [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") carrying a [Magical Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") that's been enchanted with "Stoning".

While this does not make [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage") as common as some other types of damage, the Stoning effect is dangerous enough to make **Stoning Immunity** a very desirable quality, especially when at war with a [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature**-wielding wizard.

Note that **Stoning Immunity** protects *only* against [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage"). If an attack delivers several types of damage simultaneously, the **Stoning Immunity** ability will only shrug off the [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage") component of that attack.

## Acquiring Stoning Immunity

There is currently no known method of adding **Stoning Immunity** to any unit that does not possess it by default, whether through the use of spells or [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item").
