**Stoning Gaze** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*. With this ability, a unit can make a powerful [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that forces each individual [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the target unit to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resist** or die instantly of [Irreversible Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points#Irreversible_Damage_Points "Damage Points").

The strength of a **Stoning Gaze** ability is noted after its name (e.g. "**Stoning Gaze** -2"). This indicates a temporary penalty applied to the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score while the attack is taking place. The higher the penalty, the harder it is for the target to survive the attack.

Three units in the game possess **Stoning Gaze** by default, and all three are [Fantastic Creatures](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Creature "Fantastic Creature"). It is not possible to add **Stoning Gaze** to any unit that does not possess it, though it is possible to add either [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage") or [Death Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Damage "Death Damage") to a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item"). Both are similar to **Stoning Gaze**, but slightly less powerful.

Note also that **Stoning Gaze** is similar to another ability, [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze").
Both attacks are executed in the same manner. The type of damage dealt
is different; a successful Death Gaze deals normal damage (it can be
healed or regenerated in combat). The Death Gaze can be averted with [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity"), while the Stoning Gaze can be averted with [Stoning Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Immunity "Stoning Immunity").

## Description

In *Master of Magic*, some creatures possess the ability to
attack their target by gaze alone. All the creature needs to do is train
its eyes on the opponent, and unleash a powerful stream of energy at
that opponent. The target can only rely on its own willpower and skills
to try and [Resist](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") this outburst of power, otherwise it may be destroyed on the spot.

For the purposes of **Stoning Gaze**, the unit's eye(s) releases a huge quantity of [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature**
energy. This energy can turn living tissue into stone - thus turning
what was a live creature into a statue, killing it on the spot.

## Effect

**Stoning Gaze** is automatically used whenever the unit engages in close combat - whether voluntarily making a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") against a target or [Counter Attacking](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") to an enemy assault.

When used, it forces the target unit to make one [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the target unit. To resolve this effect, the game rolls a random number between **1** and **10**. This is then compared to the target's current [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** , minus the resistance modifier of the **Stoning Gaze**, if any. If the rolled number is higher than the modified Resistance score, the target unit is struck for [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") [Irreversible Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points#Irreversible_Damage_Points "Damage Points") equal to the full [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** of one of its [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures**, slaying one figure instantly and possibly inflicting further [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** to the next figure in line.

The modifier to the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score is equal to the strength of the **Stoning Gaze** attack, as noted after the name of the ability. Thus, ****Stoning Gaze** -3** gives a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-3 Resistance**
penalty to the target. This penalty applies only for the purpose of
this specific attack, and is removed once the attack is over.

#### Timing within the Melee Attack

:   As a [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"), **Stoning Gaze** is executed prior to [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"), but if used when defending, after the attacker's [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, and [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") **Gaze Attacks**. Thus, any [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** destroyed by **Stoning Gaze** will not deliver [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"), and a unit completely destroyed by the attacker's [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, or [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") **Gaze Attacks** will not deliver a **Gaze Attack**.

#### Immunity

:   Units with [Stoning Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Immunity "Stoning Immunity") and/or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") are impervious to **Stoning Gaze**. They do not need to make any [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, instead completely ignoring the attack.

:   Also, any unit with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** remaining after all factors have been computed, as well as any [Charmed](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Charmed "Charmed") hero, is rendered effectively invulnerable to **Stoning Gaze** by virtue of its resistance score.

## Units with Default Stoning Gaze

There are only three units that possesses **Stoning Gaze** by default. All three are [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit"), one from the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** realm and two from the [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** realm.

[Basilisk](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk") [Basilisk](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk") - **Stoning Gaze** -1
[Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") - **Stoning Gaze** -4
[Gorgons](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gorgons "Gorgons") [Gorgons](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gorgons "Gorgons") - **Stoning Gaze** -2

All three units are quite dangerous to opponents with low [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") scores, though the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")
is significantly more dangerous than the others. All three units are
very dangerous to attack in Melee combat as a result, except with units
possessing very high Resistance.

Note also that several creatures possess a similar ability called [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze"). There is no procedural difference between **Stoning Gaze** and [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze"), though it is impossible to heal or resurrect units killed primarily by the former. The [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") possesses *both* attacks simultaneously - with very deadly results.

## Acquiring Stoning Gaze

There is no known method of adding a **Stoning Gaze** ability to any unit that does not already possess it, nor to increase a unit's **Stoning Gaze** strength beyond its default.

Note however that it is possible to add a [Stoning Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Touch "Stoning Touch") ability (a similar attack also delivering [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage")) to a Hero by imbuing a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") with the [Stoning Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_(Item_Power) "Stoning (Item Power)"). This magic is significantly weaker than **Stoning Gaze**, because the Touch is rolled once per *attacking* figure (rather than once per *defending* figure)— and all heroes are single-figure units.
