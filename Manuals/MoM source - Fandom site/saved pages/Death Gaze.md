**Death Gaze** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*. With this ability, a unit can make a powerful [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that forces each individual [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the target unit to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resist** or die instantly.

The strength of a **Death Gaze** ability is noted after its name (e.g. "**Death Gaze** -2"). This indicates a temporary penalty applied to the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score while the attack is taking place. The higher the penalty, the harder it is for the target to survive the attack.

Two units in the game possess **Death Gaze** by default, and both are [Fantastic Creatures](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Creature "Fantastic Creature"). It is not possible to add **Death Gaze** to any unit that does not possess it, though it is possible to add [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage") or [Death Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Damage "Death Damage") to a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item"). Both are similar to **Death Gaze**, but slightly less powerful.

Note also that **Death Gaze** is almost identical to another ability, [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze"). Both act almost identically, with the difference that the former can be averted with [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity"), while the latter can be averted with [Stoning Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Immunity "Stoning Immunity").

## Description

In *Master of Magic*, some creatures possess the ability to
attack their target by gaze alone. All the creature needs to do is train
its eyes on the opponent, and unleash a powerful stream of energy at
that opponent. The target can only rely on its own willpower and skills
to try and [Resist](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") this outburst of power, otherwise it may be destroyed on the spot.

For the purposes of **Death Gaze**, the unit's eye(s) releases a huge quantity of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** energy. This essentially attacks the very life force of the target.

## Effect

**Death Gaze** is automatically used whenever the unit engages in close combat - whether voluntarily making a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") against a target or [Counter Attacking](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") to an enemy assault.

When used, it forces the target unit to make one [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the target unit. To resolve this effect, the game rolls a random number between **1** and **10**. This is then compared to the target's current [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** , minus the resistance modifier of the **Death Gaze**, if any. If the rolled number is higher than the modified Resistance score, the target unit is struck for [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") equal to the full [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** of one of its [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures**, slaying one figure instantly and possibly inflicting further [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** to the next figure in line.

The modifier to the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score is equal to the strength of the **Death Gaze** attack, as noted after the name of the ability. Thus, ****Death Gaze** -3** gives a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-3 Resistance**
penalty to the target. This penalty applies only for the purpose of
this specific attack, and is removed once the attack is over.

#### Timing within the Melee Attack

:   As a [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"), **Death Gaze** is executed prior to [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"), but if used when defending, after the attacker's [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, and [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") **Gaze Attacks**. Thus, any [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** destroyed by **Death Gaze** will not deliver [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"), and a unit completely destroyed by the attacker's [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, or [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") **Gaze Attacks** will not deliver a **Gaze Attack**.

#### Immunity

:   Units with [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity") and/or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") are impervious to **Death Gaze**. They do not need to make any [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, instead completely ignoring the attack.

:   Also, any unit with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** remaining after all factors have been computed, as well as any [Charmed](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Charmed "Charmed") hero, is rendered effectively invulnerable to **Death Gaze** by virtue of its resistance score.

## Units with Default Death Gaze

There are only two units that possesses **Death Gaze** by default. Both are [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit").

[Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") - **Death Gaze** -4
[Night Stalker](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Night_Stalker "Night Stalker") [Night Stalker](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Night_Stalker "Night Stalker") - **Death Gaze** -2

Both units are quite dangerous to opponents with low [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") scores, though the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")
is significantly more dangerous. Both units are very dangerous to
attack in Melee combat as a result, except with units possessing very
high Resistance.

Note also that several creatures possess a similar ability called [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze"). There is very little difference between [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") and **Death Gaze**. In fact, the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") possesses *both* attacks simultaneously - with very deadly results.

## Acquiring Death Gaze

There is no known method of adding a **Death Gaze** ability to any unit that does not already possess it, nor to increase a unit's **Death Gaze** strength beyond its default.

Note however that it is possible to add [Death Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Damage "Death Damage") to a Hero's attack by imbuing a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") with the "Death" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power"). This is slightly weaker than **Death Gaze**, but can be almost as dangerous, particularly to enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure units**.
