**Darkness** is a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") of the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"). For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **25** it may be cast during battles to both enhance [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") of the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** Realm and hinder those of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**. This manifests as bonuses of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+1**, [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+1**, and a **+1** to all non-spell [Attack Strengths](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** and [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") units; and respectively penalties of the same magnitude for [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** ones. The effect lasts until the end of the battle, or until dispelled by the opponent.

## Effects

**Darkness** covers the battlefield in infernal energy. This increases the combat prowess of all [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** creatures, while simultaneously reducing that of all [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** units.

### Death Creature Bonuses

While **Darkness** is in effect over the battlefield, *each and every* [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") of the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), including all [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead")
units, will receive the bonuses outlined below. The spell doesn't care
about the unit's allegiance though, so it will affect enemy [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** creatures as well as friendly ones.

#### Attack Strength Bonus

:   **Darkness** grants a universal [Attack Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") bonus of **+1** that applies to nearly all [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") attacks that the affected units make, with the exceptions being those that deal [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **spell damage**. It improves [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Strength**, [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") / [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") / [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Attack Strength**, [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attack Strength**, [Breath Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Breath_Attack "Breath Attack") **Breath Attack Strength**, and even Conventional [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") strength. It does not affect [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") attacks (such as [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") and most actual Gaze *effects*), [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation"), or any spells cast by these units.

:   This increases the amount of dice used during [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll") by one which, in turn, improves the average "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** output of the affected attacks by [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.3** plus [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.1** for every additional [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **+10% To Hit** that the unit has over the baseline. Since this applies on a per-attack basis, [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units** naturally benefit more from it than [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single Figures**, as do units that can utilize multiple different [Attack Types](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Type "Attack Type").

#### Defense Bonus

:   **Darkness** improves the [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") of the affected units by [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+1**. As a result, they can use an extra die on their [Defense Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") when trying to mitigate [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage"), which increases their average reduction by [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.3**. Although [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units**
    can be said to receive more of a benefit, this is only true if, after a
    first Defense Roll, an attack still inflicts at least as much [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** as they have [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hits** per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** - the condition for triggering another roll.

#### Resistance Bonus

:   **Darkness** also improves the [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") of the affected units by [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+1**. This makes them harder to affect with [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse") and most forms of [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage"). Any unit that reaches [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10**
    thanks to this bonus is now immune to all ill effects that don't impose
    a Resistance penalty, as they can no longer fail their checks against
    them.

### Life Creature Penalties

While **Darkness** is in effect over the battlefield, *each and every* [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") of the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm") will receive the penalties outlined below.

#### Attack Strength Penalty

:   **Darkness** reduces the [Attack Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") of nearly all [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") attacks made by the affected units, with the exceptions being any attacks that deal [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **spell damage**. In practice though, for [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** units, this will actually only be their [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Strength**, as none of these units have any other conventional [Attack Type](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Type "Attack Type") in the original game. This penalty means that they will use one less die on their [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll"), which translates to an average "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** loss of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.3** per attack, plus another [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.1** for every [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **+10%** that the unit has over the base chance [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**. This effect is stronger against [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Unicorns](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unicorns "Unicorns")** than other [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** units, as they are [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure**, and can thus perform multiple attacks with a single combat action.

#### Defense Penalty

:   **Darkness** inflicts a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") penalty of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **-1** to the affected units. As a result, they will have one less die to use on [Defense Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") when trying to mitigate [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage"). This decreases their [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** reduction by an average of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.3** (or [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.4** if they are under the effect of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Prayer "Prayer")** of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[High Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/High_Prayer "High Prayer")**).

#### Resistance Penalty

:   **Darkness** also inflicts a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") penalty of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-1** on the affected units, which makes them slightly easier to affect with [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"), and more prone to suffering [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage").

### Darkness vs. True Light

**Darkness** and [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[True Light](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/True_Light "True Light")** are designed to be identical and opposite. **Darkness** gives bonuses to [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** creatures and penalizes those of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**, while [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[True Light](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/True_Light "True Light")** exerts the exact same effect the other way around. The two spells are *not*
mutually exclusive on the battlefield. Therefore, it is possible to
have both spells affecting the battle simultaneously. Because they are
equal and opposite effects, they essentially cancel each other out.

### Cloud of Shadow, Eternal Night

There are two overland spells that can also activate a **Darkness** effect automatically, without having to cast the spell in battle. [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Cloud of Shadow](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cloud_of_Shadow "Cloud of Shadow")** is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Town Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Town_Enchantment "Town Enchantment") that triggers this effect every time the enchanted [Town](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Town "Town") comes under siege by an opposing army; while [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Eternal Night](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Eternal_Night "Eternal Night")**, a [Very Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Very_Rare_Spell "Very Rare Spell") [Global Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Global_Enchantment "Global Enchantment"), will enshroud any battlefield where the caster's units are engaged, whether they are the offending or defending force.

Neither of these spells indicate their effect in the combat *enchantment windows*, where **Darkness**
would normally be shown. Instead, they are both listed in the combat
information window, invoked by pressing the "Info" button during battle.
Even though they are named differently ("Cloud of Darkness" and
"Eternal Night" respectively), they do in fact apply the same **Darkness** effect, to the point of preventing this spell from being re-cast as a [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") by the same party.

## Usage

**Darkness** may only be cast during combat, for a basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **25**. If the casting is successful, the spell's icon will appear in one of the *enchantment windows* at the bottom of the screen, underneath the name of the [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") whose side has cast the spell (left or right depending on whether it is cast by the defending or attacking force). **Darkness**
will then start exerting its effect immediately, and remains active
until the end of the battle, or until it is dispelled. Since it does not
carry over to the overland map, the spell has no [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost").

## Acquisition

As a [Common Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") of the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), **Darkness** may become available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") who acquires at least [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**1** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
With none, the spell can naturally not be learned during the campaign.
This is the minimum requirement for it to be traded for, appear in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be found in the spoils of victory when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival Wizard who already knows it.

The base chance for **Darkness** to be [researchable](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research "Spell Research") (at some point) in the campaign is roughly 30% (with [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**1** book), which gradually increases with the amount of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** Spellbooks possessed or found during gameplay. With [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**7** or more, the spell is certain to show up sooner or later, unless acquired from another source. It has a [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research#Research_Cost "Spell Research") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **250**, although its research is quicker for Wizards possessing the [Sage Master](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sage_Master "Sage Master") [Retort](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort"), or a bookshelf containing [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**8** or more.

**Darkness** may also be selected as a starting spell by any Wizard with at least [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**2** books. In this case, it will be available for casting as soon as the game begins.

In addition, **Darkness** is available as a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") spell. It is known by [Morgana the Witch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Morgana_the_Witch "Morgana the Witch"), although it's worth noting that this feature is bugged in the latest official version of the game, and **Darkness**
is one of the few affected spells. This means that Morgana will likely
not be able to actually access this spell unless the player installs one
of the unofficial patches. Even then, she can't cast it at her initial
level either. She needs to become at least a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Captain** (level 3), become a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Myrmidon** (level 2) if one of her [Random Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Random_Abilities "Random Abilities") is extra [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**, or otherwise equip [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") to gain enough [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** to use **Darkness**.

## Strategy

**Darkness** is an excellent spell to empower an army made up primarily of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** and/or [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead")
units. Naturally, the more such units are in an army, the more
cost-efficient the spell becomes, especially if many of them are [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units**, as they get a noticeably bigger boost to their offensive power. **Darkness** is also one of only a handful effects capable of enhancing [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks**, including the [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") components of [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") (hidden or otherwise); and can be used to offset a penalty applied by [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[True Light](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/True_Light "True Light")**.

It may be worth noting though, that casting **Darkness** may be counter-productive when trying to rely on the [Life Steal](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Steal "Life Steal") [Life Steal](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Steal "Life Steal") ability to create [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") Undead, particularly with small groups of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Wraiths](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wraiths "Wraiths")**. Although it does increase their survivability, it also shifts their [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** ratio towards more Conventional- and less [Life Stealing Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Stealing_Damage "Life Stealing Damage"), ultimately reducing the chance of slain enemy units being turned. While this can be partially offset by [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Black Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Black_Prayer "Black Prayer")**, if available, to maximize the chance, it is typically better to not cast **Darkness** unless the units take too much of a beating without it.

Alternatively, **Darkness** can also be used to hinder an opposing army that is comprised primarily of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** units. Although such stacks are not commonly seen being used by AI [Wizards](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard"), and there are no [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** [Rampaging Monsters](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rampaging_Monster "Rampaging Monster") in the original game either, the spell can occasionally come in handy nonetheless, when trying to clear [Encounter Zones](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter_Zone "Encounter Zone") featuring [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** creatures.
