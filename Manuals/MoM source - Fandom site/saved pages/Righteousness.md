**Righteousness** is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") belonging to the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **200**
it may be cast on any friendly unit on the overland map to give that
unit protection from virtually every kind of magical effects related to
the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos Realm** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death Realm**. This includes protection from spells, [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack"), and [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks**
associated with these realms. The unit may not be affected or damaged
by such magic in any way. However, the unit can still be hurt by enemy [Fantastic Creatures](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Creature "Fantastic Creature") belonging to either realm.

The spell has an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **2** per turn.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same bonus
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
upkeep costs).

## Effects

**Righteousness** offers a unit almost total protection from nearly all types of [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** magic, including (but not limited to) offensive spells from either realm.

### Protection from Spells

A unit affected by **Righteousness** is completely impervious to the majority of offensive spells from both the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos Realm** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death Realm**, and extremely resistant against a few more such spells. This includes direct-damage spells, [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"), and even most [Combat Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") and [Global Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Global_Enchantment "Global Enchantment") of either of these realms.

During combat, the unit receives a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50 Defense**
rating against direct-damage spells from either realm, meaning that
none but the most powerful spells have any chance of hurting the unit.
In game terms, [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50 Defense** means that the unit may make 50 [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** rolls against such spells, blocking on average about [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **15 points of Damage**. Therefore, while the unit may still be targeted by such spells, it will almost always suffer 0 damage from them.

[Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse") from either realm may also be targeted at this unit, but do not have any effect. This is because the unit receives a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+30 Resistance** bonus when attempting to deflect such spells.

The same goes for direct-damage spells, which will cause 0 damage to the unit while it is under the **Righteousness** effect. [Global Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Global_Enchantment "Global Enchantment") and [Combat Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") that should negatively affect this unit will do nothing to it either.

Note that beneficial spells, such as [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") from either realm cast by the unit's owner, do not trigger **Righteousness**'s blocking powers. Such spells can still be cast on the enchanted unit.

Unfortunately, it seems that the game's designers have "missed a few spots", and so some offensive spells from the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** Realms will still have an effect on the enchanted unit. They are listed below.

#### Call Chaos

:   [Call Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Call_Chaos "Call Chaos"), a [Combat Instant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Instant "Combat Instant") from the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos Realm**,
    applies one of eight possible effects on its targets. The effect for a
    given target is chosen completely at random. If the unit affected by **Righteousness** is struck by this spell, two of those possible effects *will* get through the enchantment and affect the unit anyway. The other six effects, if chosen, will have no impact on the unit.

:   Firstly, [Call Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Call_Chaos "Call Chaos") may randomly *heal* the unit (similar to the [Healing](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Healing "Healing") spell). This is actually a *beneficial* effect for the unit, and so **Righteousness** will not block it.

:   Secondly, [Call Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Call_Chaos "Call Chaos") may decide to *disintegrate* the unit (similar to the [Disintegrate](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Disintegrate "Disintegrate") spell). In this case, **Righteousness** does not apply its [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+30 Resistance** bonus to the unit and does not block the effect. Thus, if the unit has a Resistance score of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **9** or fewer, it will be completely annihilated as per the rules of the [Disintegrate](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Disintegrate "Disintegrate") spell.

:   **Note:**  This is one of the only ways (or possibly the only way) to actually kill a **Righteous** unit with [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** magic. Of course, it is highly unlikely to occur at all, so it should be a last-ditch effort.

#### Terror

:   [Terror](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Terror "Terror"), a [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") from the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death Realm**, will actually affect **Righteous** units despite their divine protection.

:   This spell makes a unit unable to act if they fail a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") roll at [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+1**. The unit may still be able to resist the spell, but would need to succeed in a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll first, as per the rules of the [Terror](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Terror "Terror") spell, without the benefit of the Resistance bonus from **Righteousness**.

### Protection from Ranged Magical Attacks

Furthermore, whenever the enchanted unit is attacked with a [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attack** that's associated with the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** realm, its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score is temporarily set to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** for purposes of blocking damage from said attack.

With [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50**, the unit is extremely unlikely to suffer any [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** from such attacks, since it would block (on average) about [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **15 points of Damage**. Enemy units with exceptionally high [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attack** scores *might* get through this much [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense"), but even then would be hard-pressed to do so.

Units that have a [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos**-related [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attack** include the [Efreet](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Efreet "Efreet"), [Shadow Demons](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shadow_Demons "Shadow Demons"), [Demon Lord](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Demon_Lord "Demon Lord"), [Storm Giant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Storm_Giant "Storm Giant"), [Magicians](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magicians "Magicians"), and all [Dark Elf](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Elves "Dark Elves") units except [Dark Elf Priests](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Elf_Priests "Dark Elf Priests"). See [Ranged Magical Attack Subtypes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack#Ranged_Magical_Attack_Subtypes "Ranged Magical Attack") for further details.

### Protection from Special Attacks

Finally, the enchanted unit is also nearly-invulnerable to all [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that are associated with the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realms.

Once again, when struck by these [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack"), the unit receives either [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** or a bonus of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+30 Resistance**
(as appropriate) in order to block such attacks, making them totally or
almost-totally useless against it. Again, an extremely powerful attack *may* be able to get through [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** to inflict some damage on the unit, but this is exceptionally rare.

[Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") associated with the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** realm include [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath"), [Lightning Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lightning_Breath "Lightning Breath"), [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") and [Destruction](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Disintegration_Damage "Disintegration Damage").

[Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") associated with the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realm include [Cause Fear](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cause_Fear "Cause Fear") and all kinds of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Damage "Death Damage") and [Life Stealing](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life_Stealing_Damage "Life Stealing Damage") attacks.

## Usage

**Righteousness** may be cast during combat for the rather high [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40**. In this case, the spell will only affect the targeted unit until the end of the battle - at which point it will dissipate.

Alternatively, **Righteousness** may be cast on the overland map for a lasting effect. In this case it has a base [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **200**, and requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **2** per turn to maintain. Failure to pay this Upkeep Cost due to lack of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") will cause the spell to dissipate. However, as long as the spell is paid for (and not dispelled by any rival [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")), the unit continues to enjoy its bonus.

**Righteousness** may
only be cast on friendly units. If the desired target is in an overland
army stack, click on that stack to get a list of units inside it, and
then select your desired unit.

Once the spell is in effect, the unit will glow with a [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**white** aura, unless there is a more powerful spell also affecting it.

If you've placed **Righteousness**
on a unit, you may remove it by examining the unit's details and
clicking the Enchantment's icon. The primary reason to do this would be
in order to remove the spell's [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"), thus conserving [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** for other spells.

## Acquisition

As a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life** spell, **Righteousness** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**2** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is not guaranteed unless the Wizard acquires at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**10** Spellbooks.

Customized Wizards possessing [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**11**
Spellbooks at creation time may choose this spell as one of their
default spells before starting the game, in which case the spell will
already be researched and available for casting immediately on the first
turn.

Wizards with [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**2** to [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**9** Spellbooks have a random chance of being able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") **Righteousness** during the game. The chance for this spell to appear for research increases with the number of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay. With [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**10** or [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**11** Spellbooks, the spell is guaranteed to appear for Research at some point, if it is not already available for casting.

**Righteousness** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **1,120**.

With at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**2** Spellbooks, the **Righteousness** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

**Righteousness** will mostly protect a unit from enemy [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** *spells*, which are often quite deadly. Therefore, it is used for protecting specific units from enemy wizards possessing such spells.

**Righteousness** should be cast on units heading off to war against such wizards. You never know when an enemy wizard will decide to cast [Disintegrate](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Disintegrate "Disintegrate") or [Doom Bolt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Bolt "Doom Bolt") (or possibly even worse spells!) on your valuable units, so **Righteousness** is an insurance against such eventualities.

Other than this, **Righteousness** may also come in handy when taking high-level [Encounter](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter "Encounter") zones containing [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** or [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** creatures, as they may contain units possessing dangerous [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that **Righteousness** can fend off.

**Righteousness** may also be extremely handy when starting a war against a [Dark Elven](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dark_Elves "Dark Elves") empire. Most units from this race use a [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos**-based [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attack**, which **Righteousness** will block effortlessly.

On the other hand, simply keeping **Righteousness** on a unit that isn't going to see combat against [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** or [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** assets is a waste of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**. It offers no benefit against creatures or spells from the other realms whatsoever. You might even be better off removing **Righteousness** from a unit that isn't expected to encounter [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** assets anytime soon.
