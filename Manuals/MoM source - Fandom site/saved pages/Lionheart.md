**Lionheart** is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") from the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"). For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **200** it may be cast on any friendly unit on the overland map to give that unit a significant **+3** bonus to its [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") strength, [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score, and total [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") *per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure***. If the unit has a non-magical [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") or [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), its ranged [Attack Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") will also be improved by **+3**. To keep this effect in play, an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **4** per turn must be paid.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow these benefits
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
Upkeep Cost).

## Effects

**Lionheart** inspires a
single unit with an almost supernatural zeal, increasing both its combat
ferocity and its survival ability by a significant amount!

### Melee Attack Bonus

While **Lionheart** is affecting a unit, its [Melee Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength#Melee_Strength "Attack Strength") is increased by an outstanding [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+3**. This means that whenever the unit makes a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), it can use an additional 3 dice on its [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll"), potentially delivering up to [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **3** extra points of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** to its target with each attack. For [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units**, this applies to the attack of each individual [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**, often resulting in a massive overall increase in the average [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** output of the unit. However, this bonus will not work at all for units whose base Melee Strength is [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **0**.

### Ranged Attack Bonus

If the target unit has a non-magical [Ranged-](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") or short-range attack, **Lionheart** will also enhance the strength of this attack by **+3**. Much like the [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Strength** bonus, this grants the unit an extra 3 dice to use on its [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll") to determine the "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** of this attack and, for the same reasons, can result in a massive average [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** increase for [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units**. Similarly though, this benefit only applies to units that have a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile-**, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder-**, or [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attack** to begin with and, in the first two cases, also still have ammunition to fire.

### Resistance Bonus

While **Lionheart** is affecting a unit, that unit's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score is raised by [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+3**. This makes the unit significantly harder to affect with [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"), and much more resilient against [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") attacks that require it to make Resistance rolls, such as [poison](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage") or [petrification](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage").

### Hit Point Bonus

**Lionheart** grants an enchanted unit an bonus of [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **+3** to its [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hits** per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** value. That is, it increases the maximum [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** of *each individual [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure*** in that unit by additional [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **+3**. As a result, each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** can take [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **3** extra points of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** before dying, thereby increasing the survivability of the unit. Because this bonus is given to each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** separately, [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units** benefit much more from this effect than [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure Units**. A unit with [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **8 figures**, for example, can now take [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **8** × [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **3** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **24** extra points of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** before being destroyed - a *massive* increase in survivability, putting it roughly on par with a high-tier [Fantastic Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit")!

The casting of **Lionheart**
on a damaged unit can exhibit different behaviour based on whether this
happens in- or outside of combat, as a result of the game tracking [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") differently in these two scenarios. In combat, when **Lionheart** is cast, it also indirectly heals the "top" or "lead" [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** of an injured target unit by [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **3**, as it increases its maximum [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** without affecting the [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** done to this [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**. For example, if a unit has [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **2** per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**, and its "top" [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** has already suffered [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1** when **Lionheart** is cast on it, it will now have [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **4** out of [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **5 Hit Points**, rather than [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **1** out of [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **5**. However, slain [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** are not restored back to life, although if they are revived by other means, those [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** will have elevated maximum [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** just like the others.

On the other hand, the situation is entirely different if **Lionheart** is cast overland. In this case, the game preserves only the total [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** done to the unit *as a whole* when applying the spell's effect. This means that the unit's current [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** count will be immediately recalculated using the new maximum [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points**, quite possibly reviving lost [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures**, and changing the amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** recorded to the "top" [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**. For instance, a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **6 figure** unit with [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **3** per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** and a total of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **11 Damage Points** has [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **3** remaining with a "top figure damage" value of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **2**. Casting **Lionheart** on this unit will result in [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **5** live [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures**, with [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **5** points recorded on the "top" [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**.

## Usage

**Lionheart** may be cast both overland and in combat, for the basic [Casting Costs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **200** and [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40**
respectively. If the casting is successful, the game will prompt for a
friendly unit to be selected as the spell's target. On the overland map,
this typically requires clicking on the army stack that contains the
unit which, in turn, brings up a list view of all units in that stack,
allowing for the target to be picked individually. Only units not
already under the effect of **Lionheart** may be chosen. Once the spell is cast, a glowing [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[white](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** outline appears around the unit (unless it is overridden by a stronger enchantment); and **Lionheart**, along with its icon, will be displayed in the *unit statistics window* when inspecting this unit.

The overland version of **Lionheart** has an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **4**
that must be paid at the beginning of every turn to keep the spell in
effect. This is automatically deducted by the game from the owner's [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** pool as long as the enchantment remains in existence. If there is insufficient [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**
in the pool to pay for the spell, then it will dissipate, ceasing its
effect immediately. It may also be cancelled manually by left-clicking
its name in the *unit statistics window* of its target unit. However, this action can only be performed outside of combat, during the caster's own turn.

The combat version of **Lionheart**
does not carry over to the overland map: it will disappear
automatically as soon as the battle is over. As such, it has no Upkeep
Cost; but at the same time may not be manually removed by its caster
either (as enchantments can not be cancelled during a battle).

## Acquisition

As a [Rare Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") of the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), **Lionheart** may become available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") who acquires at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**2** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
With any less, the spell can not be learned during the campaign. This
is the minimum requirement for it to be traded for, appear in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be found in the spoils of victory when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival Wizard who already knows it.

The base chance for **Lionheart** to be [researchable](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research "Spell Research") (at some point) in the campaign is roughly 10% (with [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**2** books), which gradually increases with the amount of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** Spellbooks possessed or found during gameplay. With [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**10** or more, the spell is certain to show up sooner or later, unless acquired from another source. It has a [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research#Research_Cost "Spell Research") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **880**, although its research is quicker for Wizards possessing the [Sage Master](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sage_Master "Sage Master") [Retort](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort"), or a bookshelf containing [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**8** or more.

**Lionheart** may also be selected as a starting spell by players who allocate all of of their picks into [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** books when creating their Wizard. In this case, it will be available for casting as soon as the game begins.

In addition, **Lionheart** is available as a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") spell. It is known by [Torin the Chosen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Torin_the_Chosen "Torin the Chosen"), although it's worth noting that he can't actually cast it at his initial level. He needs to either become at least a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Captain** (level 3), become a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Myrmidon** (level 2) if *both* of his [Random Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Random_Abilities "Random Abilities") are extra [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**, or otherwise equip [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") to gain enough [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** to use **Lionheart**.

## Item Power

**Lionheart** can also be duplicated as an [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power") and imbued onto a [Magical Item](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item"). This requires the [Arcane](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Arcane "Arcane")**[Create Artifact](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Create_Artifact "Create Artifact")** spell, and has the advantages of being non-dispellable, and requiring no [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"). While enchanting [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Lion Heart](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lion_Heart_(Item_Power) "Lion Heart (Item Power)")** onto an item can't be done unless the [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") possesses at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[6](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"), knowledge of the actual spell is *not necessary*. Selecting this Power increases the item's total value by [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1,000**.

[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Lion Heart](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lion_Heart_(Item_Power) "Lion Heart (Item Power)")** can be enchanted onto magical [Accessories](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Jewelry "Jewelry") only. However, this still means that it is available to any [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"), as all of them possess at least one [ItemSlot Misc](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/d/d8/ItemSlot_Misc.png/revision/latest?cb=20120702054432) miscellaneous [Equipment Slot](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item#Equipment_Slots "Magical Item").
If an item with this Power is created and given to a Hero, then they
will benefit from the effect described above for as long as they keep
the item equipped. Since [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Lion Heart](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lion_Heart_(Item_Power) "Lion Heart (Item Power)")** does not appear on any pre-fabricated items, it is only available through [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting"), as no items bearing it can be acquired otherwise.

## Strategy

**Lionheart** is one of the most powerful [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard").
Its bonuses dramatically increase the unit's survivability and
resilience, not to mention its combat strength. As explained above, [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units** receive spectacular benefits from this spell - and the more [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures**, the better. Since it increases *each* [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**'s [Attack Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") simultaneously, **Lionheart** will allow the unit to inflict far more [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** than normal. More importantly however, the more [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** there are in the unit, the more its total [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** are increased, causing even the weakest [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **8 figure** unit to become incredibly durable.

Although units relying primarily on [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magical Ranged Attacks** do not get a [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **+3** bonus, the increased [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** and the [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** bonus still make this spell quite potent when cast on such units. In fact, **Lionheart** is one of only a few effects in the game that can increase the [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hits** per [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**
value of a unit, essentially providing a unique benefit that can be
worth exploiting even if none of the enchantment's other bonuses are
expected to come into play.
