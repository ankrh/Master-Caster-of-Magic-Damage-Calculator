**Ranged Damage** is a name used on this wiki and elsewhere to refer to the [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") caused be any type of [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack").

As with all other types of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage"), **Ranged Damage** attempts to inflict actual [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage Points** on its target, and requires the attacker and target to perform multiple [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls, respectively, in order to determine the final [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** amount.

**Ranged Damage** is
only a collective name that's used for simplicity's sake. In practice,
it is actually a group of three separate damage types, namely [Ranged Missile Damage](#Ranged_Missile_Damage), [Ranged Boulder Damage](#Ranged_Boulder_Damage) and [Ranged Magical Damage](#Ranged_Magical_Damage), which are delivered by [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Missile**, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Boulder** and [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Magic** attacks respectively. Each of these behaves slightly differently from one another, and from the rest of the [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") types found in the game.

## Effect

Although **Ranged Damage** is actually a collective name for three different types of damage (see [below](#Ranged_Damage_Types)), all three have a very similar effect on the target - as defined by the rules common to all [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") types. This always involves both [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** rolls and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls to determine the final amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** done to the target.

The ***maximum*** amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** caused to the target is equal to the strength of the [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") itself. This has to be calculated (see [below](#Attack_Strength)) before the damage can be processed.

### Rolling To Hit

With all types of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage"), the attacker is required to make [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** rolls, which determine how many hits are registered against the target. The number of rolls is equal to the strength of the [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") itself. Each roll results in a value between 1 and 100, which is then compared to the attacker's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** score (usually [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30**, before modifiers are taken into account). Each successful roll registers one hit against the target.

Some types of **Ranged Damage** (as explained [below](#Distance_Penalties)) also modify the attacker's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** score based on the distance to the target. This is always a penalty, and serves to reduce the number of registered hits when **Ranged Damage** is inflicted from a greater distance. This goes to offset the tactical advantages of [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), since the target is not allowed to retaliate (unlike with [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack")).

### Rolling To Block

The target of [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") is then allowed to make [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls, which will help it block or dodge some of these registered hits. The number of rolls is equal to the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score, and again each roll results in a number between 1 and 100, which is compared with the defender's [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** score (usually [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30**, for nearly all units). Each successful roll blocks one of those registered hits.

### Applying Damage

Finally, any registered hits that were not blocked are immediately translated into actual [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** which is applied to the target's current [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Health**. During this process, the target unit may lose one or more of its [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures**, and may end up being completely destroyed as a result.

Since [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") requires both [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls, the actual amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** delivered to the target is almost always lower than the calculated maximum. It is even possible that no actual [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** will result, whether the attacker failed all his/her [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** rolls or the defender managed to succeed in a sufficient number of [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense** rolls.

There are also complex rules regarding [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units** making or suffering [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage"). The attack is stronger the more [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** there are in the attacking unit, and the defender unit has more chance to survive if it contains more [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** as well.

For a complete explanation of how [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") works, including all the rolls it is required to make, see the article on [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage").

### Attack Strength

The total amount of **Ranged Damage** delivered by a [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") is determined primarily by the Ranged Attack strength of the attacking unit. This is indicated by a row of [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Bows**, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Boulders** or [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Bolts** in the unit's details panel, depending on what kind of [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") the unit is using.

For each [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Bow**, [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Boulder** or [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Bolt**, the unit delivers 1 point of **Ranged Damage**. Thus, a unit with [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **3 Bows** deliver 3 points of **Ranged Damage** each time it makes a [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack").

This becomes somewhat more complex with [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units**, since each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** makes a separate [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") upon the target. For example, a unit with [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **3 Bows** and [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **4 Figures** makes 4 separate [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), each delivering 3 points of **Ranged Damage**. This is not the same as a single attack delivering 12 points of **Ranged Damage** (see [Physical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Physical_Damage "Physical Damage") for a thorough explanation of why this is so).

The calculated Attack Strength indicates the absolute *maximum* amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** that can be caused to the target. Again, due to the [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** rolls made in the process of calculating inflicted damage (see above), the actual results are usually *lower* than this maximum.

## Distance Penalties

Two of the three types of **Ranged Damage**, namely [Ranged Missile Damage](#Ranged_Missile_Damage) and [Ranged Boulder Damage](#Ranged_Boulder_Damage), are subjected to [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**
penalties based on the distance between the attacker and the target.
The greater the distance, the higher the penalty. This influences the
damage output of the unit's [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack")
as explained above. This penalty is unique to these two damage types,
and is not applied to any other attack found in the game; it doesn't
even apply to [Ranged Magical Damage](#Ranged_Magical_Damage).

The size of the penalty is based only on distance, as shown in the table below:

| Range | [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** Penalty |
| --- | --- |
| 1 Tile (adjacent)\* | No Penalty |
| 2 Tiles | No Penalty |
| 3,4 or 5 Tiles | [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10%** Penalty |
| 6,7 or 8 Tiles | [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-20%** Penalty |
| Etcetera | [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-30%** Penalty |

*\* "Adjacent" means the attacker and the target are right next to each other (including diagonals).*

Note that some units possess the [Long Range](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Long_Range "Long Range") ability. In this case, they will never suffer a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") penalty greater than [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10%**.
In other words, they will suffer exactly -10% when firing at any target
that is at least 3 tiles away. Also note that the lowest the distance
penalty can bring your to hit score is 10%.

## Ranged Damage Types

As mentioned earlier, the term "**Ranged Damage**" does not actually describe one specific [Damage Type](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Type "Damage Type"), but rather a group of three separate types, called **Ranged Missile Damage**, **Ranged Boulder Damage** and **Ranged Magical Damage**. They are all delivered by [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), and are similar enough that they can be safely explained together in a single article.

Each of these is defined as being delivered by a different kind of [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"). As you probably already know, there are three [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") Types in the game. The table below shows which attack delivers which kind of damage:

| Attack Type | Icon | Ranged Damage Type |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") | [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") \* | Ranged Missile Damage |
| [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") | [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") \* | Ranged Boulder Damage |
| [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") | [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") | Ranged Magical Damage |

*\* [Slingers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Slingers "Slingers") have a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") marked with [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Boulders**. This is still a missile attack though.*

The sections below explain how each of these "sub-types" differs from the others. In all other aspects, they are identical.

### Ranged Missile Damage

**Ranged Missile Damage** is always delivered by [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks**. Its strength is thus based on the attacker's [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack strength**.

**Ranged Missile Damage** suffers from [Distance Penalties](#Distance_Penalties),
as described above. It is thus very important to try and get close to
the target before firing, in order to avoid or at least minimize the [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** reduction caused by this effect.

**Ranged Missile Damage** will always trigger a target's [Missile Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Missile_Immunity "Missile Immunity"), if present. This increases the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50**, making that target impervious to all but the strongest [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks**. However, it will not normally trigger any other kind of immunity, and so may be useful against [Magic-Immune](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") units.

### Ranged Boulder Damage

**Ranged Boulder Damage** is always delivered by [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attacks**. Its strength is thus based on the attacker's [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attack strength**.

**Ranged Boulder Damage** suffers from [Distance Penalties](#Distance_Penalties),
as described above. It is thus usually important to try and get close
to the target before firing, in order to avoid or at least minimize the [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** reduction caused by this effect. Fortunately, several units possessing a [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attack** also possess the [Long Range](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Long_Range "Long Range") ability, which limits that penalty to some degree.

**Ranged Boulder Damage** will not trigger any of the target's immunities, and thus can be effective against a wide variety of targets.

### Ranged Magical Damage

**Ranged Magical Damage** is always delivered by [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks**. Its strength is thus based on the attacker's [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attack strength**.

**Ranged Magical Damage** does not suffer from any [Distance Penalties](#Distance_Penalties).
Therefore, it is just as effective when the target is way across the
other side of the battlefield as it is when the target is very close by.
This allows [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks** to be used early in the battle, and makes them very deadly throughout.

On the other hand, **Ranged Magical Damage** will always trigger a target's [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity"), if present. This increases the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50**, making that target impervious to all but the strongest [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks**. However, it will not normally trigger any other kind of immunity, and so may be useful against [Missile-Immune](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Missile_Immunity "Missile Immunity") units.

The reaction with the target's [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") means that **Ranged Magical Damage** is often lumped into another [Damage Type](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Type "Damage Type") known simply as [Magical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Damage "Magical Damage") - the damage delivered by most combat spells. There are slight differences, but it is usually fine to use the term [Magical Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Damage "Magical Damage") instead of **Ranged Magical Damage**.
