**Negate First Strike** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*.

When a unit with this ability is attacked by an enemy possessing [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike"), that [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike") ability is ignored: combat is handled as though that attacker does not possess [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike").

Five different [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") possess **Negate First Strike**. All five are either [Pikemen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Pikemen "Pikemen") or [Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Halberdiers "Halberdiers"), of various types.

There is no way to add **Negate First Strike** to a unit that does not already possess it.

## Description

In the sort of medieval-style combat occurring in *Master of Magic*,
various types of cavalry and other fast units can charge in for a kill,
causing damage to the opponent before that opponent can react. That
confers some protection to the charger: if it can kill several opponents
immediately, fewer will be left to deal retaliatory damage.

To counter this age-old tactic, some units utilize weapons and
training that allow them to prepare for a charge, presenting it with a
wall of sharp, long spikes. This breaks the charge, and forces the
cavalrymen to fight on more equal terms. Thus, **Negate First Strike** deprives the enemy of its ability to deliver damage prior to straight-up hand-to-hand combat.

## Effect

The **Negate First Strike**
ability is only triggered in a specific situation: When the unit
possessing this ability comes under attack by an enemy unit possessing [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike").

Normally what should happen in such a case is that the enemy unit gets to deliver all of its [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") and any available [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") *before* the defender gets to react at all. Thus, the enemy unit can eliminate several of the defending [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** immediately, reducing the defender's strength during the ensuing [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack").

**Negate First Strike** units, however, temporarily disable the enemy's [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike") ability. Thus, if a [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike") unit attacks a **Negate First Strike** unit, combat is handled in the "normal" order, with both units delivering [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") and [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") (if any) simultaneously - neither gets the advantage.

Note that the attacker may still deliver [Thrown Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), [Breath Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Breath_Attack "Breath Attack") and/or [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") before the exchange of [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"). These are not affected by **Negate First Strike**.

Also note that **Negate First Strike** does not permanently disable the target's [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike") ability. The effect applies only when a [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike") unit goes up against a **Negate First Strike** unit, and disappears immediately after the attack is over.

## Units with Default Negate First Strike

**Negate First Strike** is a fairly-common ability among a specific type of [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit"), namely the [Pikemen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Pikemen "Pikemen") and [Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Halberdiers "Halberdiers") - though not all types of [Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Halberdiers "Halberdiers") possess this ability. In total, only 5 units possess **Negate First Strike**:

[Gnoll Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gnoll_Halberdiers "Gnoll Halberdiers") [Gnoll Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gnoll_Halberdiers "Gnoll Halberdiers")
[High Men Pikemen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/High_Men_Pikemen "High Men Pikemen") [High Men Pikemen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/High_Men_Pikemen "High Men Pikemen")
[Lizardmen Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lizardmen_Halberdiers "Lizardmen Halberdiers") [Lizardmen Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lizardmen_Halberdiers "Lizardmen Halberdiers")
[Nomad Pikemen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nomad_Pikemen "Nomad Pikemen") [Nomad Pikemen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nomad_Pikemen "Nomad Pikemen")
[Troll Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Troll_Halberdiers "Troll Halberdiers") [Troll Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Troll_Halberdiers "Troll Halberdiers")

These units are fairly important early on in the campaign, especially against enemies using plenty of [Cavalry](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Cavalry "Cavalry") or other units possessing [First Strike](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/First_Strike "First Strike").

## Acquiring Negate First Strike

There is no known method of adding a **Negate First Strike** ability to any unit that does not already possess it, whether by spells or by [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item").
