**Death Damage** is a type of [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") that can be delivered by various units as part of their [Melee](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"), [Ranged](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), [Thrown](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), or [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"). An attack delivering **Death Damage** stands a good chance of killing one or more enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** outright, regardless of the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score.

When a target is struck by **Death Damage**, it does not make any [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** rolls against it. Instead, each affected [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the target unit makes a single [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll. The result of this roll determines whether the figure is killed instantly or left completely unharmed.

**Death Damage** is commonly found as the component of a [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") possessed by the [Night Stalker](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Night_Stalker "Night Stalker") and [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn"), thanks to their [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze") ability. [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") may also add a **Death Damage** component to their [Melee](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"), [Ranged](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), or [Thrown Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") by equipping a [Magical Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") that contains the "Death" enchantment. Other than these, no other units or attacks will deliver **Death Damage**.

## Effect

**Death Damage** forces its target to make one or more [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls – with each failed roll killing off one [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the enemy unit. **Death Damage** behaves somewhat differently depending on whether it is delivered by a [Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") or a [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack").

[Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") (in this case called a **Death Touch**) are exclusive to [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") wielding "Death" enchanted weaponry, and will only affect a single figure of the enemy unit. This is because only one [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") of a specific type can be made by each figure in a unit, and Heroes are all [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **single figures**. [Axe](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe") [Throwing](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown "Thrown") is the only exception to this rule, as detailed below.

On the other hand, [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") will affect every [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** of the opposing unit. This type of attack also occurs before [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack")
do their damage, and thus will reduce the damage of the enemy unit if
any figures are successfully dispatched by it, making this [Damage Type](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Type "Damage Type") rather useful and dangerous.

### Resistance and Death

Whenever an attack containing a **Death Damage** component strikes any target on the battlefield, the affected [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures**
within the target unit must make a single die roll – resulting in a
random number between 1 and 10. This number is then compared to the
target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score (with possible modifiers, which are explained below).

Each Figure that rolls a number *higher than* its unit's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score is immediately killed off – regardless of how many [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** it has remaining or how high its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score is. The figure is simply destroyed. If all Figures in the unit have failed their [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls simultaneously this way, then the entire unit is destroyed.

Figures that roll *equal to or lower than* their units' [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** are completely unharmed by this **Death Damage**.

#### Resistance Modifiers

Each attack that delivers **Death Damage** inflicts a certain penalty on the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score. The stronger this penalty, the less chance each target [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** has to "win" the roll and avert its own death.

The [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** penalty varies from attacker to attacker, with the "strongest" penalty being that inflicted by a [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")'s attacks: [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-4 Resistance**! This attack has no problem affecting units with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **4** or less (it will completely destroy them on the spot!), and can occasionally affect targets with Resistance scores as high as [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **13**.

The weakest **Death Damage** attack is that possessed by a [Night Stalker](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Night_Stalker "Night Stalker"), at [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-2**. [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") delivering a **Death Damage** component inflict [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-3**.

## Immunities

**Death Damage** is considered to be associated with the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realm. As a result, it will never affect any unit that possesses [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity").

Similarly, **Death Damage** is also completely nullified by [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity").

Finally, since the weakest [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") penalty given by **Death Damage** is [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-2**, any unit with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **12 Resistance** or more may be completely invulnerable to **Death Damage** – it cannot possibly fail its Resistance roll. Of course, it's always important to remember that some **Death Damage** attacks inflict a greater penalty; if the penalty lowers the target's Resistance below [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10**, the attack has at least some chance to destroy that target.

Note that these immunities will only block the **Death Damage** component of an attack. If the attack also delivers any [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"), [Ranged Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Damage "Ranged Damage"), or any other type of damage, that damage may or may not go through the target's immunities unimpeded.

## Sources of Death Damage

**Death Damage** is a very rare [Damage Type](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Type "Damage Type"). It can only come from either the [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze") ability or from a [Magical Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") containing the "Death" enchantment.

### Units with Death Gaze

:   *Main article: [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze")*

The [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze") ability is the more common source of **Death Damage**. Whenever a unit possessing this ability makes a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), it will first execute a separate [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") that delivers **Death Damage**.

A [Gaze Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") occurs *before* the exchange of [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") between the attacker and the target. As a result, any enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** killed by the **Death Damage** do not get a chance of cause any [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") or use [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"). Thus, [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze") protects the attacker by reducing the number of enemy Figures that retaliate against it. It may, potentially, kill the *entire* enemy unit outright, and thus end the combat round before the target can deliver any damage at all!

The strength of a [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze") ability is very important: it indicates how much [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** penalty is inflicted on the target. The greater the penalty, the greater the chance of killing each enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure**.

### Death Weapons

:   *Main article: [Death (Item Power)](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_(Item_Power) "Death (Item Power)")*

The other source of **Death Damage** comes from weapons imbued with the "[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_(Item_Power) "Death (Item Power)")" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power"). This enchantment can be placed only on weapons – [Swords](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Maces](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace"), [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe"), [Bows](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow"), [Wands](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand"), and [Staves](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff"). When the enchanted item is equipped on a Hero, it adds a **Death Damage** component to the attack associated with the item. [Swords](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Maces](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace"), and [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe") add the **Death Damage** component to the Hero's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") (and [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack")), while [Bows](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow"), [Wands](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wand "Wand"), and [Staves](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Staff "Staff") add **Death Damage** to the Hero's [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") (this excludes [Counter Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack")).

[Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe") also add a **Death Damage** component to the Hero's [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), if they have any. In this case, voluntary attacks (but not counterattacks) made by this Hero will actually inflict **Death Damage** twice: once when executing the [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), and then again with the [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"). Since the [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") occurs before the exchange of Melee damage, any [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** killed by it will not get a chance to retaliate against the Hero.

**Death Damage** delivered by a Hero's attack always inflicts a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") penalty of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-3** on the target.

During the custom [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting") process, the "[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_(Item_Power) "Death (Item Power)")" power is only available to [Wizards](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") possessing at least [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**3 Death** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). Naturally, it's always possible to find a weapon containing this bonus as a reward for defeating [Encounters](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter "Encounter") or as an offer from a [Merchant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant "Merchant").

The Hero's attack will only deliver **Death Damage** while the Hero is holding the enchanted item. If the item is removed or lost, the [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero")'s attack no longer delivers **Death Damage**.
