**Prayer** is an [Uncommon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** magic [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment"). For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **30** it may be cast during combat to provide a lasting effect, whereby *all* friendly creatures receive a +10% bonus to the success chance of any [Attack Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll"), [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll"), or [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") check they make. As a result, they become more deadly, and harder to damage, hinder, or incapacitate.

## Effects

**Prayer** raises the
morale of all friendly units on the battlefield, encouraging them to
perform better in almost every respect. As a result, units will strike
more truly with their attacks, dodge incoming damage more deftly, and
become more resilient in the face of curses and other maledictions.

### To Hit Bonus

While **Prayer** is in effect, each and every friendly unit on the battlefield receives a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **+10% To Hit** bonus. This means that every die of their [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll") is more likely to cause a point of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**, thus increasing their overall average [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") output. The bonus applies equally to all attacks, with the exception of those that deliver [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **spell damage**. That is, it will affect [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee-** and [Counter Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"); [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") / [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") / [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"); [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown-** and [Lightning Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lightning_Breath "Lightning Breath")[Breath Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Breath_Attack "Breath Attack") **Breath Attacks**; and even the hidden Conventional component of [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"). On the other hand, it does *not* affect [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation"), or any spells, spell-like abilities, or [Spell Charges](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Charges_(Item_Power) "Spell Charges (Item Power)") used by the unit.

#### Example

:   We have a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with a [Melee Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength#Melee_Strength "Attack Strength") score of [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **10**. He does not have any [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") bonuses, which puts the success chance of his [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll") at [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%**, like most other units.

:   The maximum amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** this Hero can inflict with his [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") is [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **10**, as he has 10 dice to roll, corresponding to his Attack Strength. However, on average, he will deliver only [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **10** × [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **3** points of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**, as each those 10 dice only have a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%** chance of being successful in scoring a hit.

:   With **Prayer**, this Hero's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** score is raised to [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **40%**. He can still inflict a maximum of only [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **10**, as his Attack Strength did not change, but *on average* will now deliver [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **10** × [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **40%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4** points of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**. Therefore, although the Hero did not become *stronger*, on average, he will now realize more of his damage potential with each Melee Attack he makes.

### To Block Bonus

While **Prayer** is in effect, all friendly units on the battlefield get a +10% chance of success for each die of every [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") they make to reduce incoming [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage"). The spell does not increase the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score itself - instead, it raises the chance that each [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Shield** will manage to block a point of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**. Therefore, while the unit can still potentially block the same amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**, it is likely to block more *on average*.
Naturally, this does not apply against attacks which do not allow a
Defense Roll in the first place, but the benefit is otherwise
proportional to the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** attribute.

#### Example

:   We have a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **5**. Like all units without the [Lucky](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lucky "Lucky") [Lucky](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lucky "Lucky") trait, his chance [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** is the default [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30%**. Whenever this Hero is hit with a [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") attack that allows a [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll"), he can use 5 dice (equal to his [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense**), each of which has a [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30%** chance to reduce the incoming "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** by [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1** point. The average reduction will thus be [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **5** × [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1.5**.

:   With **Prayer**, the success chance of each die is raised to [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **40%**. The Hero can still block a maximum of only [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **5**, but *on average* will now avoid about [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **5** × [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **40%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **2** points from Conventional Damage attacks. Therefore, while the Hero's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score hasn't been increased, he will now make a better use of that score in combat.

### Resistance Bonus

While **Prayer** is in effect, each and every friendly unit on the battlefield receives a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") bonus of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+1**.
In other words, their chance to shrug off malicious effects is
increased by +10%. As a result, they will be slightly harder to affect
with [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"), and will stand up better against [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") attacks like [poison](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage") or [petrification](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage").

### Enemy Melee To Hit Malus

While **Prayer** is in effect, each and every enemy unit on the battlefield receives a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10% To Hit** malus to their [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"). That is, every time they perform such an attack against a unit affected by **Prayer**, including during [Counter Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), the success chance of each die of their [Attack Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll") is reduced by [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **10%**. While this does not affect their *potential* [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**, it does decrease their *average* "raw" [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") output by an amount proportionate to their [Melee Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength#Melee_Strength "Attack Strength"). Other [Attack Types](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Type "Attack Type") are not affected.

It should be noted that this effect is removed from the game starting with the unofficial [Insecticide](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Insecticide "Insecticide")
patch. Examination of the game code has led multiple patch-makers to
the conclusion that this is actually not an intentional benefit. It is
most likely left over from an earlier stage of the game development,
where Melee Attacks were handled differently from other Conventional
Damage types (hence why the effect only triggers for Melee Damage).

## High Prayer

**Prayer** has a more powerful version: the [Very Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Very_Rare_Spell "Very Rare Spell") [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[High Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/High_Prayer "High Prayer")**. In addition to providing the same benefits as **Prayer**, this spell also boosts the [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Strength** and [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** attributes of all friendly units, and grants an even higher increase to their [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance**. Although the two spells are not mutually exclusive, their effects *are not cumulative*. That is, while it may be possible to cast both, it is typically not worth it, as the bonuses will not stack.

## Usage

**Prayer** may only be cast during combat, for a basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **30**. If the casting is successful, the spell's icon will appear in one of the *enchantment windows* at the bottom of the screen, underneath the name of the [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") whose side has cast the spell (left or right depending on whether it is cast by the defending or attacking force). **Prayer**
will then start exerting its effect immediately, and remains active
until the end of the battle, or until it is dispelled. Since it does not
carry over to the overland map, the spell has no [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost").

## Acquisition

As an [Uncommon Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") of the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), **Prayer** may become available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") who acquires at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**1** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
With none, the spell can naturally not be learned during the campaign.
This is the minimum requirement for it to be traded for, appear in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be found in the spoils of victory when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival Wizard who already knows it.

The base chance for **Prayer** to be [researchable](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research "Spell Research") (at some point) in the campaign is roughly 10% (with [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**1** book), which gradually increases with the amount of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** Spellbooks possessed or found during gameplay. With [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**8** or more, the spell is certain to show up sooner or later, unless acquired from another source. It has a [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research#Research_Cost "Spell Research") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **800**, although its research is quicker for Wizards possessing the [Sage Master](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sage_Master "Sage Master") [Retort](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort"), or a bookshelf containing [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**8** or more.

**Prayer** may also be selected as a starting spell by players who allocate all of of their picks into [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** books when creating their Wizard. In this case, it will be available for casting as soon as the game begins.

In addition, **Prayer** is available as a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") spell. It is known by [Elana the Priestess](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Elana_the_Priestess "Elana the Priestess"), although it's worth noting that she can't actually cast it at her initial level. She needs to either become at least a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Captain** (level 3), become a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Myrmidon** (level 2) if one of her [Random Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Random_Abilities "Random Abilities") is extra [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**, or otherwise equip [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") to gain enough [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** to use **Prayer**.

## Strategy

**Prayer**'s bonuses may be
hard to understand, and may not seem all that effective. Yet, it is
actually one of the most powerful combat spells in the game. Not only
does it enhance both the offensive and defensive performance of every
friendly unit on the battlefield, it does so in a way that scales both
with the base power of those units, and most other beneficial effects
they may be under - whether these are already present at the time the **Prayer**
is cast, or applied afterwards. Therefore, it is worth using in
virtually any situation where there are two or more units that will be
affected by it. Unless, of course, [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[High Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/High_Prayer "High Prayer")** is also available, considering that their effects are not cumulative.
