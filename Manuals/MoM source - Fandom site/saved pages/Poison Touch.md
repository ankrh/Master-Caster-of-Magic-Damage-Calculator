**Poison Touch** (simply called "**Poison**" in the game) is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*.

With this ability, a unit can make a powerful [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that forces the target to make several consecutive [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, suffering [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1 point of Damage** for each failed roll.

The strength of a **Poison Touch** ability is noted after its name (e.g. "Poison 4"). This indicates the number of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls the target must make for each **Poison Touch** attack, and thus the maximum amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** that this attack can cause.

**Poison Touch** is one of the more common [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") abilities in the game, possessed by no fewer than 9 units, of which 5 are [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit"), 3 are [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit"), and one is a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero").

There is no known way of adding **Poison Touch** to a unit that does not already possess it.

## Description

Whether through bite, sting or weapon, a poison (or, more correctly,
"venom") inserted into a living target can cause serious harm. Poisons
are chemicals that are designed (whether naturally or artificially) to
attack the living tissue that makes up the target's body. Poison
essentially disintegrates the target from inside.

Though resistance to poison can be acquired, and is even innate
to some species, it is not too common. Most poisonous attacks will
likely cause at least some damage to the target. The primary benefit of
such an attack, since it attacks from inside one's body, is that poison
cannot be stopped by any armor!

## Effect

**Poison Touch** is added to any other non-spell [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") attack that its possessor makes. That is, it is executed *in addition to* a unit's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee**, [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack")[Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack")[Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged**, and possibly [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown**, [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath**, or [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"). One [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") will be delivered by each and every [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the unit whenever they perform their other attacks.

When triggered, **Poison Touch** delivers [Poison Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage"). It forces the target unit to make a certain number of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, where each failed roll results in [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1** point of [Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") being inflicted on the target unit. The target does not get to make any [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** rolls to avert damage from **Poison Touch**.

Thus, the maximum amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** that can be caused to the target is equal to the total strength of the **Poison Touch** attack. The more [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** the target has, the less [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** it will suffer, on average.

As a [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), **Poison Touch** delivers its [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** simultaneously with that of the triggering attack.

Units with [Poison Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Immunity "Poison Immunity") are impervious to **Poison Touch**. They do not need to make any [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls, instead completely ignoring the attack. Similarly, any unit that possesses [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10 Resistance** or higher is also completely immune, since it cannot possibly fail its Resistance rolls.

Conversely, any unit that's had its Resistance score reduced to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **0** or less will automatically fail each and every roll, thus suffering the *full* damage from the **Poison Touch** attack.

## Units with Default Poison Touch

There are exactly 9 units that possesses **Poison Touch** by default. 3 are [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") from different [Races](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Race "Race"), 5 are [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit"), and one is a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"):

### [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit")

[Wyvern Riders](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern_Riders "Wyvern Riders") [Wyvern Riders](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern_Riders "Wyvern Riders") - **Poison Touch** 6
[Manticores](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Manticores "Manticores") [Manticores](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Manticores "Manticores") - **Poison Touch** 6
[Nightblades](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nightblades "Nightblades") [Nightblades](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nightblades "Nightblades") - **Poison Touch** 1

### [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit")

[Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn") - **Poison Touch** 4
[Ghouls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ghouls "Ghouls") [Ghouls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ghouls "Ghouls") - **Poison Touch** 1
[Giant Spiders](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Giant_Spiders "Giant Spiders") [Giant Spiders](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Giant_Spiders "Giant Spiders") - **Poison Touch** 4
[Great Wyrm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Wyrm "Great Wyrm") [Great Wyrm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Wyrm "Great Wyrm") - **Poison Touch** 15
[Nagas](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nagas "Nagas") [Nagas](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nagas "Nagas") - **Poison Touch** 4

### [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero")

[Tumu the Assassin](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tumu_the_Assassin "Tumu the Assassin") [Tumu the Assassin](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tumu_the_Assassin "Tumu the Assassin") - **Poison Touch** 5

These are also the only sources of [Poison Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage") in the game - it cannot be delivered in any other way except a **Poison Touch** attack.

The [Great Wyrm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Great_Wyrm "Great Wyrm") is easily the strongest of the above. With a **Poison Touch** attack of strength 15, it can cause massive damage to any unit that does not possess a high [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score. [Wyvern Riders](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wyvern_Riders "Wyvern Riders") and [Manticores](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Manticores "Manticores") come second: while they only have **Poison Touch** 6, each unit contains [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **2 figures** when uninjured, resulting in a total strength of 12. (The strength of the **Poison Touch** attack does NOT increase with experience.)

## Acquiring Poison Touch

There is no known method of adding **Poison Touch** to any unit that does not already possess it. Also, since **Poison Touch** is the only source of [Poison Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage"), the units listed above are the only units that will ever deliver this type of damage.
