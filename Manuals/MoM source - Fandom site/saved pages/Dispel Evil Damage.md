**Dispel Evil Damage** is a type of [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") that can be delivered by various units as part of their [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") and/or [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"). An attack delivering **Dispel Evil Damage** stands a good chance of killing an enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** outright, regardless of the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score. However, unlike similar [types of damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Type "Damage Type"), **Dispel Evil Damage** only works against creatures associated with the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** or [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realms. Other units will suffer no harm from **Dispel Evil Damage**.

When a valid target is struck by **Dispel Evil Damage**, it does not make any [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** rolls against it. Instead, one [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** (for each attacker) in the target unit makes a single [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll. The result of this roll determines whether the figure is killed instantly or left completely unharmed.

**Dispel Evil Damage** can be delivered directly by the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life** [Combat Instant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Instant "Combat Instant") called [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil"). It is also found as a component of a [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") possessed by [Angels](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel"), thanks to their [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_(Ability) "Dispel Evil (Ability)") ability. [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") may also add a **Dispel Evil Damage** component to their [Melee](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") and [Thrown Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") by equipping a [Magical Melee Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") that contains the "[Holy Avenger](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_Avenger_(Item_Power) "Holy Avenger (Item Power)")" enchantment. Other than these, no other units or attacks will deliver **Dispel Evil Damage**.

## Effect

**Dispel Evil Damage** forces its target to make one [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** roll for each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** delivering **Dispel Evil Damage** - with each failed roll killing off one [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** in the enemy unit. **Dispel Evil Damage** will only ever affect [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** creatures (including [Chaos Channeled](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Channels "Chaos Channels") and [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") units). It will do no harm to other unit types. The [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") are particularly vulnerable to **Dispel Evil Damage**.

### Resistance and Death

Whenever an attack containing a **Dispel Evil Damage** component strikes a valid target on the battlefield (see [below](#Valid_Targets)), either ***one*** [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** within the target unit ([ability](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_(Ability) "Dispel Evil (Ability)"), [item power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_Avenger_(Item_Power) "Holy Avenger (Item Power)")), or ***all*** [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** ([spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil"))
in the unit must make a die roll - resulting in a random number between
1 and 10. This number is then compared to the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score, which is simultaneously penalized (see below).

Any [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure** that rolls a number *higher than* its unit's penalized [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score is immediately killed off - regardless of how many [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** it has remaining or how high its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score is. The figure is simply destroyed. If all remaining Figures in the unit have failed their [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls simultaneously this way, then the entire unit is destroyed.

Figures that roll *equal to or lower than* their units' penalized [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** are completely unharmed by this **Dispel Evil Damage**.

#### Resistance Penalties

:   While it is forcing the target to roll against its own [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** score, **Dispel Evil Damage** also inflicts a certain [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** penalty on the target, which depends on the target unit's type.

:   Against [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") from the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** and [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realms, as well as against any [Chaos Channeled](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Channels "Chaos Channels") units, **Dispel Evil Damage** inflicts [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-4**. This means that most targets will have a very hard time resisting the effect. Only units with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **14** or higher are completely immune to **Dispel Evil Damage** - since they cannot possibly fail their rolls.

:   Against the [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead"), **Dispel Evil Damage** inflicts [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-9** (despite at least one in-game tooltip claiming more than this), which should ensure at least one [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figure**'s destruction in most cases.

#### Irreversible Damage

:   Any **Dispel Evil Damage** actually delivered will always be [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") [Irreversible Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points#Irreversible_Damage_Points "Damage Points"). This means that these [Hit Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hit_Points "Hit Points") **Hit Points** cannot be regained until combat ends.

:   Furthermore, units killed primarily by **Irreversible Damage** can not be brought back from the dead (including by [Regeneration](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Regeneration "Regeneration")), or turned into [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead").

### Valid Targets

**Dispel Evil Damage** will only affect certain types of units. Other units will simply ignore this effect, and will suffer no harm.

Valid targets include all [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") belonging to the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**Chaos** or [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realms. These units suffer a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-4 Resistance** penalty while attempting to avert the **Dispel Evil Damage**, as explained above.

[Chaos Channeled](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Channels "Chaos Channels") units, including both [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"), are also valid targets for this effect. They too suffer [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-4**.

Finally, any [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") unit, regardless of which type or [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm") it belonged to before becoming [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead"), will be hurt by **Dispel Evil Damage**. These units suffer [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-9** while attempting to block this damage, making them extremely vulnerable.

All other units, including [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit"), [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") and [Fantastic Creatures](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Creature "Fantastic Creature") from the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life**, [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** and [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Sorcery** realms are invalid targets. Though an attack delivering **Dispel Evil Damage** can be targeted at them, these units will never suffer any harm from this damage at all.

## Immunities

Aside from invalid targets (see above), units possessing [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") will never be hurt by **Dispel Evil Damage**.

Furthermore, if **Dispel Evil Damage** inflicts a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **-4 Resistance** penalty on its target, a target with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **14** or higher cannot possibly fail its rolls and thus will never be harmed. If the target is [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead"), it requires at least [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **19 Resistance** to become completely immune - which is extremely rare if not impossible to achieve.

Note that these immunities will only block the **Dispel Evil Damage** component of an attack. If the attack also delivers any [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage"), [Ranged Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Damage "Ranged Damage") or any other type of damage, that damage may or may not go through the target's immunities unimpeded.

## Sources of Dispel Evil Damage

**Dispel Evil Damage** is a fairly rare [Damage Type](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Type "Damage Type"), which can only be delivered by [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life** sources. These sources include a spell called [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil") (which affects all [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** in the target unit), an ability also called [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_(Ability) "Dispel Evil (Ability)") (possessed only by [Angels](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel")), and Heroes wielding a [Magical Melee Weapon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") containing the "[Holy Avenger](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_Avenger_(Item_Power) "Holy Avenger (Item Power)")" enchantment.

### Dispel Evil Spell

:   *Main article: [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil")*

[Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil") is an [Uncommon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") [Combat Instant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Instant "Combat Instant") from the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life** realm. For the base cost of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **25**, it will instantly execute an attack delivering **Dispel Evil Damage** to **all** [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** of a chosen target unit.

The damage effect behaves exactly as explained above in this article - forcing each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the target unit to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resist** or die. The [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** penalties inflicted by the spell are also identical to those described above.

[Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil "Dispel Evil") can only be targeted at [Valid Targets](#Valid_Targets) as described above. The game will refuse to target any other unit.

### Units with the Dispel Evil Ability

:   *Main article: [Dispel Evil (Ability)](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_(Ability) "Dispel Evil (Ability)")*

The [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_(Ability) "Dispel Evil (Ability)") ability is a very rare ability possessed only by [Angels](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel"). Whenever an [Angel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel") makes a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), it also executes a separate [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") that delivers **Dispel Evil Damage**.

A [Touch Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") occurs *simultaneously* with the exchange of [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") between the attacker and the target. As a result, any enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **Figures** that fail their roll will die only at the end of the combat round, and may still get a chance to inflict their own [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") and/or [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack") at the [Angel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel"). Thus, [Dispel Evil](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel_Evil_(Ability) "Dispel Evil (Ability)") does not reduce retaliatory damage, and should be used carefully.

The **Dispel Evil Damage** inflicted by an [Angel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Angel "Angel") behaves the same way as described above in this article - forcing one [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** (since the Angel is a single figure itself) in the target unit to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resist** or die. The [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** penalties inflicted by the spell are also identical to those described above.

### Holy Avenger Weapons

:   *Main article: [Holy Avenger (Item Power)](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_Avenger_(Item_Power) "Holy Avenger (Item Power)")*

The final source of **Dispel Evil Damage** comes from weapons imbued with the "[Holy Avenger](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_Avenger_(Item_Power) "Holy Avenger (Item Power)")" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power"). This enchantment can be placed only on Melee weapons - [Swords](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Maces](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace") and [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe"). When the enchanted item is equipped on a Hero, it adds a **Dispel Evil Damage** component to the Hero's [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") and [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"). For [Axes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe"), it also adds this component to the Hero's [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), if any.

The **Dispel Evil Damage** inflicted by a "[Holy Avenger](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Holy_Avenger_(Item_Power) "Holy Avenger (Item Power)")" weapon behaves the same way as described above in this article - forcing one [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** (since all Heroes are single figures) in the target unit to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resist** or die. The [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** penalties inflicted by the spell are also identical to those described above.

Heroes with the [Thrown](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown "Thrown") ability, when equipped with a "Holy Avenger" [Axe](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe") (but not other weapon types) will gain two chances for **Dispel Evil** on every voluntary attack: once with their [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), and then again with their [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"). Unfortunately this does not work when counterattacking.

During the custom [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting") process, the "Holy Avenger" bonus is only available if you possess at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**3 Life** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). Naturally, it's always possible to find a weapon containing this bonus as a reward for defeating [Encounters](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter "Encounter").

The Hero's attack will only deliver **Dispel Evil Damage** while the Hero is holding the enchanted item. If the item is removed or lost, the [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero")'s attack no longer delivers **Dispel Evil Damage**.
