**Giant Strength** is a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Enchantment "Enchantment") belonging to the [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40** it may be cast on a unit on the overland map to give it a permanent [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+1** bonus (per figure), which must then be maintained with a [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1** per turn [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"). If the unit has a [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), the strength of this attack is also increased by [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **+1**.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **8**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same bonus
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
upkeep costs).

## Effects

**Giant Strength** increases the [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") potential of a unit by improving its [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") by a small amount.

This spell may be cast on any friendly unit, whether on the
overland map or within combat. Although the effect of the spell is the
same in both cases, there are different costs and long-term implications
for casting this spell outside of combat.

### Melee Attack Bonus

**Giant Strength** bestows a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+1**
bonus on each figure in the targeted unit. The bonus makes the unit's
melee attack stronger, by adding one more chance to make an extra point
of damage with every attack (that chance is based on the unit's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** bonus).

**Giant Strength** is exceptionally potent when cast on a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure unit** - the more figures, the better - since it applies to *each figure within the unit*. Therefore, an 8-figure unit of [Nomad Pikemen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nomad_Pikemen "Nomad Pikemen") actually receives 8 extra Melee points (one per figure) so long as all its figures are alive. In comparison, a 1-figure [Stone Giant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stone_Giant "Stone Giant") only receives one extra point, a largely insignificant amount considering how many [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Attack** points it already has.

### Thrown Attack Bonus

In addition to the above, **Giant Strength** will also increase a unit's [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") by [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **+1**, assuming the unit had a [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") at all to begin with.

As explained above, this is significantly more powerful a bonus when cast on [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units**, since each figure's attack becomes stronger as a result.

## Usage

**Giant Strength** may be cast during combat for the rather low [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **8**.
In this case, the spell will only affect the targeted unit until the
end of the battle - at which point it will dissipate. This is good for
instantly making a unit more potent, surprising the enemy.

Alternatively, **Giant Strength** may be cast on the overland map for a lasting effect. In this case it costs [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **40** to cast, and requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1** per turn to maintain. Failure to pay this Upkeep Cost due to lack of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") will cause the spell to dissipate. However, as long as the spell is paid for (and not dispelled by any rival [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")), the unit continues to enjoy its [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+1** bonus.

Like all other [Unit Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment"), **Giant Strength**
must targeted at a friendly unit, whether in or out of combat. If the
desired target is in an overland army stack, click on that stack to get a
list of units inside it, and then select your desired unit.

Once the spell is in effect, the unit will glow with a [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Green** aura, unless there is a more powerful spell also affecting it.

If you've placed **Giant Strength**
on a unit, you may remove it by examining the unit's details and
clicking the Enchantment's icon. The primary reason to do this would be
in order to remove the spell's [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"), thus conserving [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** for other spells.

## Acquisition

As a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** spell, **Giant Strength** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least one [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is not guaranteed unless the Wizard acquires at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**7** Spellbooks.

Customized Wizards possessing up to [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**10**
Spellbooks at creation time may choose this spell as one of their
default spells before starting the game, in which case the spell will
already be researched and available for casting immediately on the first
turn. Wizards with [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**11** Spellbooks are guaranteed to have this spell available for casting as soon as the game begins.

Wizards with [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**6** or fewer Spellbooks who have not chosen **Giant Strength** as a guaranteed spell have a random chance of being able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") it. The chance for this spell to appear increases with the number of [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**Nature** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay. With [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**−3** Spellbooks, the spell is guaranteed to appear for Research if it is not already available for casting.

**Giant Strength** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **80**.

With at least [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**1** Spellbook, the **Giant Strength** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

As mentioned above, the more figures are in a unit, the more potential benefit there is to casting **Giant Strength** on it. Units with 8 figures gain the best benefit, as their [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") potential increases by [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **8**, a significant amount.

Therefore, [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") are often the best targets for this spell.
