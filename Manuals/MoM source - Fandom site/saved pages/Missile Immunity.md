**Missile Immunity** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*.

When a unit with **Missile Immunity** is struck by a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack**, its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score is temporarily set to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50**
for the purposes of blocking this damage. This means that it is
exceptionally difficult (though still possible) to inflict Damage to
this unit using a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack**.

**Missile Immunity** does not work against other types of [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), such as [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks** or [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attacks**.

**Missile Immunity** is one of the more common immunities in the game. It is possessed by a total of 15 units, 7 of which are [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"), 6 are [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") (particularly [Magicians](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magicians "Magicians") from various [Races](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Race "Race")), and 2 are [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") from the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**Death** realm.

Furthermore, it is possible for any unit to obtain **Missile Immunity** through the use of the [Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") spell. [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") can also acquire it by wearing an item imbued with the "Guardian Wind" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power").

## Description

Bowmen and other types of units possess a [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack")
where one or more projectiles - usually small - are launched at great
speed towards the target. Arrows, small pebbles and even javelins are
among the projectiles used for this purpose. They are designed to impact
with the target with such force, concentrated often at a single point,
so as to pierce armor and cause physical damage.

The small size of these projectiles, however, allows them to be
manipulated by various protective magics. Whether it is an invisible
shield that physically blocks them, or a well-timed gust of wind that
knocks them off course, or just lack of vulnerable spots to be hit with a
missile, the final effect is that the protected unit is virtually
invulnerable to any such attack.

## Effect

**Missile Immunity** is a passive ability that protects a unit specifically from [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks**.

Whenever a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks** is performed against a unit with **Missile Immunity**, the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") rating is immediately raised to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50**. Therefore, the unit can make 50 [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** rolls, blocking on average [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **15 points of Damage**
- an impressively-high amount. It is unlikely for any attack to
actually get through this much Defense, and normally results in the unit
taking no damage whatsoever.

There are many units in the game that possess a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack**, with many being [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit"). A few [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") also possess such an attack. A [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit")'s [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack** is almost entirely nonthreatening to units with **Missile Immunity**, and will practically never cause any damage to them due to its low [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** output. [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"),
on the other hand, can potentially acquire such high attack bonuses
that they will actually be a little threatening, but this would require
tons of [Experience](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience "Experience"), [Enchantments](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Enchantment "Enchantment") and/or [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") - and thus is unlikely to happen.

Note that the **Missile-Immune** unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score is raised *only* for the purpose of blocking damage from a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack**. Once the unit has made its defense rolls against this specific attack, its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score is restored to normal.

**Missile Immunity** does not defend against any kind of [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attack** or [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attack**. When such an attack strikes the unit, **Missile Immunity** is not triggered; the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score remains unchanged.

Note that [Slingers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Slingers "Slingers") possess a [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks** that is nonetheless marked with [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Boulders**. However, it *is* in fact a Ranged Missile Attack, and is suitably blocked by **Missile Immunity**.

## Acquiring Missile Immunity

There are two separate ways to add **Missile Immunity** to a unit that does not possess it by default:

* Casting [Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") on the unit,
* Equipping a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") unit with an item imbued with the "Guardian Wind" [Item Power](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Power "Item Power").

Both of these methods adds the same kind of **Missile Immunity** described above in this article.

### Guardian Wind Unit Enchantment

[Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") is a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") belonging to the [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Sorcery** realm. When cast on a unit, it will add the **Missile Immunity** ability to that unit.

When cast on the overland map, [Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") has a basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **50**. It has a lingering effect, so that the targeted unit will keep its [Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") effect so long as the spell continues to exist. While it exists, the spell requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1** to be paid at the start of each turn.

Alternatively, it is possible to cast [Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") during combat as an impromptu [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment"). In this case the spell's basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") is only [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **10**,
but its effects will only last until the end of the battle (or until
dispelled by the enemy). Since neither the spell nor the effect will
carry over to the overland map, there is no associated [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") with this version of the spell.

The [Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") effect has its own icon and label, but its effects are nonetheless identical to those described above.

### Guardian Wind Item Power

A permanent version of the [Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") spell can be imbued into any [Sword](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sword "Sword"), [Mace](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mace "Mace"), [Axe](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Axe "Axe"), [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield"), [Chainmail](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chainmail "Chainmail"), [Platemail](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Platemail "Platemail") or [Jewelry](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Jewelry "Jewelry") piece. When a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") wears this item, he/she receives the **Missile Immunity** ability.

During [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting"), it is only possible to select the "Guardian Wind" bonus if you possess at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**3 Sorcery** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
Otherwise, "Guardian Wind" is not available. Of course, it is always
possible to find an item already enchanted with "Guardian Wind" as
reward for defeating an [Encounter](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Encounter "Encounter").

The **Missile Immunity** ability bestowed by an item is transient: it will disappear if the Hero ever removes or loses the enchanted item.

Note that the Hero's details panel will list this as "Guardian Wind" (along with the icon associated with the [Guardian Wind](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Guardian_Wind "Guardian Wind") spell). As mentioned previously, this is otherwise identical to **Missile Immunity** in its basic form.
