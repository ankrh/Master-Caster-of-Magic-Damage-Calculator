:   *This article deals specifically with Ranged **Missile** Attacks. For an overview of how Ranged Attacks work in general, see [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack").*

A **Ranged Missile Attack** is one of three classes of [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") available in *Master of Magic*;
it actually consists of two types, Bows and Bullets, which only differ
in the graphics they use and the description on the character sheet.
When a unit is capable of making a **Ranged Missile Attack**, its details panel will show the strength of the attack as a series of [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Bows** or [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Bullets** (the bullet symbol is unfortunately the same as the one used for [Rocks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack"), but it will say Bullets instead of Rocks down below). The [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Bows** icon is used on this wiki to represent **Ranged Missile Attacks** and/or their strength.

**Ranged Missile Attacks** are generally weaker than other [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"). For one, all [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks** suffer from [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**
penalties based on the distance to the target: the further away the
target, the higher the penalty. Thus they are significantly less useful
at long range. Furthermore, [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks** are generally weak in strength, and generally have little or no chance of harming opponents possessing [Missile Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Missile_Immunity "Missile Immunity").

Mitigating this somewhat, most [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attackers** are [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units** containing plenty of figures, increasing the statistical chance of causing at least some damage. Also, a **Ranged Missile Attack** can harm opponents possessing [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity"), making it more useful than [Ranged Magical Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") in this particular case.

Like all other [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), **Ranged Missile Attacks** may only be used a limited number of times (usually 8) per battle. This number is displayed in the unit's details panel.

**Ranged Missile Attacks** are fairly common compared to other [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") types. There are 15 different types of [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") possessing this attack, and an additional 4 [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"). There are no [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") possessing this type of attack. Note that all of the [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") possessing a **Ranged Missile Attack** may use a [Bow](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow") to increase the strength of this attack.

## Ranged Missile Attack Properties

A [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack** is one of three different types of [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") in *Master of Magic*, with the other two being [Ranged Magical Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") and [Ranged Boulder Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack"). There are several important differences between each of these attack types. The advantages and disadvantages of **Ranged Missile Attacks** are explained below - for an overall comparison, see [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack").

### Distance Penalties

[Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks**
suffer from an important disadvantage that seriously affects the way
they are used in combat, especially when compared to the simpler (and
deadlier) [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks**: they suffer [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** penalties based on distance to the target.

The penalty equals [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10% To Hit** for every 3 tiles of distance between the shooter and the target. Therefore, the shooter suffers [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10%** at a distance of 3 tiles, and [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-20%** at a distance of 6 tiles. The game's original manual indicates that [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-20%** is the largest penalty possible, but the Strategy Guide implies that the bonus could be even higher (i.e. [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-30%** at 9 tiles, and so on).

At a distance of 1 or 2 tiles from the target (i.e. either
adjacent to the shooter or one empty tile apart), the shooter suffers no
[To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** penalty at all.

As a result of this penalty, [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attackers**
will generally want to close the distance with their target before
spending any of their ammunition. Attempting a long-range shot may
reduce the shooter's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** score by such a large amount that there is very little chance to actually inflict any damage on the target whatsoever.

The same principle is also important to remember when fighting against enemy [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attackers**:
if you wish to minimize damage, keep your units as far away from the
enemy Missile troops until they've depleted all of their ammunition.
After that you can close the distance safely.

### Immunity to Missiles

**Ranged Missile Attacks** directed at a target possessing [Missile Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Missile_Immunity "Missile Immunity") will trigger that ability - raising the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** for purposes of blocking damage from this attack.

[Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **50** is virtually impossible to defeat; only extremely-powerful [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") will ever have enough [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Attack Strength** to have any real chance of inflicting damage through this much armor. Thus, [Missile Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Missile_Immunity "Missile Immunity") usually makes a unit completely impervious to [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks**.

### No Immunity to Magic

On the other hand, **Ranged Missile Attacks** do not trigger a target's [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity"), if it possesses one. Therefore, **Ranged Missile Attacks** may be useful against such units, where [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks** will likely have no effect whatsoever.

## Units with Ranged Missile Attacks

The game contains exactly 19 units that possess a **Ranged Missile Attack**. These are the only units that possess such an attack: no unit may acquire a **Ranged Missile Attack** under any circumstance if it does not have one by default.

### Normal Units

15 of the **Ranged Missile Attack** units in the game are [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") from various [Races](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Races "Races"). [Bowmen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bowmen "Bowmen") are the most common of these, and several races also possess unique racial units with a stronger and/or more useful **Ranged Missile Attack**.

Most of these units may make up to 8 **Ranged Missile Attacks** per battle, though some can only make 6. Since these units are all [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units**, their overall attack strength is often higher than implied by the raw attack score, but generally **Ranged Missile Attack** [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") are fairly weak overall.

[Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") equipped with [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Mithril Weapons** or [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Adamantium Weapons** receive a bonus of [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+1** or [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+2** (respectively) to their **Ranged Missile Attack**. Those weapons as well as [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Magical Weapons** will improve Missile Attacks in 2 further ways: firstly ignoring [Weapon Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Weapon_Immunity "Weapon Immunity") ability, secondly giving a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **+10% To Hit** bonus.

[Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") acquire [Experience](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience "Experience") over time, which slowly increases the strength of their **Ranged Missile Attack**.

### Heroes

There are 4 [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") in the game who possess a **Ranged Missile Attack**. Each of these Heroes can use his/her [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") up to 8 times per battle, but fortunately most of them can then switch to a respectable [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") if necessary.

Since each [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") is a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Single-Figure_Units "Figure") **Single-Figure unit**, the strength of his/her **Ranged Missile Attack** can be considered quite low (at least initially) when compared to other units. [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") will generally use their speed to get very close to their targets before firing, this making the most of each attack.

All 4 of these Heroes are also capable of holding a [Bow](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bow "Bow"). Any attack-strength bonuses and/or [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** bonuses on the item will improve the user's [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack** strength. Bonuses on [Jewelry](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Jewelry "Jewelry") items also apply to the wearer's **Ranged Missile Attack**, if he/she possesses one.

[Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") gain [Experience](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience "Experience") over time, and their [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack** strength increases at a rate of [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+1** per level. [Leadership](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Leadership "Leadership") is the only [Hero Ability](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero_Ability "Hero Ability") that increases this further with each [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Experience Level**, and sadly will give no more than [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+1** or [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **+2** at most, even when the Hero reaches Level 9 ("Demi-God"). Note however that bonuses from [Leadership](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Leadership "Leadership") apply to all units in the Hero's army.

The [Blademaster](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Blademaster "Blademaster") ability also influences the Hero's [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attack**, and to a much greater degree than [Leadership](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Leadership "Leadership"), but it does so indirectly by raising the Hero's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** score. This helps the attack inflict more of its potential damage output. Note that the same bonus applies to *all* of the Hero's attacks.
