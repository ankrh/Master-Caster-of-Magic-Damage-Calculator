**Black Prayer** is an [Uncommon](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") of the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"). It may only be cast during combat, for a basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **35**.
As long as this spell is in effect on the battlefield, each and every
enemy unit suffers a number of small, but nonetheless significant
penalties. They lose **1** point each from all of their conventional [Attack Strengths](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") and [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense**, and their [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** is reduced by [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **2** points. **Black Prayer** lasts until the end of the battle or until it is dispelled. It does not carry over to the overland map, and thus has no [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost").

## Effects

**Black Prayer** engulfs
all enemy units in dark energy, which weakens them both physically and
spiritually. This results in a small, yet significant drop in the enemy
army's effectiveness and, more importantly, makes all opposing units
more susceptible to [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse") and other ill effects.

### Attack Strength Penalty

While **Black Prayer** is in effect, each and every enemy unit possessing any [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") attack loses **1** point from the strength of all such attacks. This applies equally to [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Strength**; [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile-**, [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical-**, and [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder** [Attack Strengths](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength"); [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown-** and [Breath Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Breath_Attack "Breath Attack") **Breath Attacks**; and even the Conventional component of [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"), hidden or otherwise. However, it does not affect [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Spell Damage**, although this type is only dealt by the [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") ability, and actual spells cast by units.

This reduction means that the unit has one less die to use on its [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll"), reducing both its potential- and, as a result, average [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** output. Naturally, this effect is more severe the more [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** and [Attack Types](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Type "Attack Type")
a unit has; but it also has an additional consequence in the original
game. Should one of a unit's Attack Strengths be reduced to **0** as a result of **Black Prayer**, the unit will no longer be able to execute "added effects" with that attack. This not only applies to [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), such as [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch") [Poison Touch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Touch "Poison Touch"), but also most [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack")
as well, as an undocumented feature of these is that they are performed
alongside an otherwise hidden Conventional Damage attack. Since this
attack normally has a strength of only [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **1**, it usually goes unnoticed, until it is reduced to [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **0**, at which point the Gaze Attack stops working altogether.

This is true for all Gaze units in the game except for the [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**[Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")**, whose [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") attacks are instead attached to its Conventional [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") instead, which has a base Attack Strength of [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **4**. Since this behaviour is widely regarded as a bug rather than a feature, it is changed in the [Unofficial Patch 1.50](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unofficial_Patch_1.50 "Unofficial Patch 1.50") such that "added effects" get executed even if the attack they are affixed to has a strength of **0**.

### Defense Penalty

While **Black Prayer** is in effect, each and every enemy unit loses [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **1** off its [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score. This decreases both their potential- and average [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") reduction, as they have one less die to use on their [Defense Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll"). However, this effect can not reduce a unit's score below [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **0**
- it will simply be ignored in that case. In fact, this limitation
applies before, and independently of any conditional modifiers to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense**. For example, a [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Phantom Beast](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Phantom_Beast "Phantom Beast")** (base score of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **0**) afflicted with **Black Prayer** would still have [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **3** against the [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") of a [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** creature if it was also under the effect of a [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Bless](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Bless "Bless")** [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") (which grants a conditional [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+3**).

### Resistance Penalty

While **Black Prayer** is in effect, each and every enemy unit suffers a penatly of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-2** to its [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score. As a result, they become more susceptible to [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse") and other ill effects, including nearly all attacks that deal [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage"). This is often considered **Black Prayer**'s most important effect, as it increases the chance of success for other [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** magic spells and [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") later on in the battle - which this [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm") has in great abundance.

## Usage

**Black Prayer** may only be cast during combat, for a basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **35**. If the casting is successful, the spell's icon will appear in one of the *enchantment windows* at the bottom of the screen, underneath the name of the [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") whose side has cast the spell (left or right depending on whether it is cast by the defending or attacking force). **Black Prayer**
will then start exerting its effect immediately, and remains active
until the end of the battle, or until it is dispelled. Since it does not
carry over to the overland map, the spell has no [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost").

## Acquisition

As an [Uncommon Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Uncommon_Spell "Uncommon Spell") of the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), **Black Prayer** may become available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") who acquires at least [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**1** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
With none, the spell can naturally not be learned during the campaign.
This is the minimum requirement for it to be traded for, appear in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be found in the spoils of victory when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival Wizard who already knows it.

The base chance for **Black Prayer** to be [researchable](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research "Spell Research") (at some point) in the campaign is roughly 10% (with [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**1** book), which gradually increases with the amount of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** Spellbooks possessed or found during gameplay. With [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**8** or more, the spell is certain to show up sooner or later, unless acquired from another source. It has a [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research#Research_Cost "Spell Research") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **450**, although its research is quicker for Wizards possessing the [Sage Master](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sage_Master "Sage Master") [Retort](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort"), or a bookshelf containing [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**8** or more.

**Black Prayer** may also be selected as a starting spell by players who allocate all of of their picks into [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")** books when creating their Wizard. In this case, it will be available for casting as soon as the game begins.

In addition, **Black Prayer** is available as a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") spell. It is known by [Morgana the Witch](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Morgana_the_Witch "Morgana the Witch"), although it's worth noting that this feature is bugged in the latest official version of the game, and **Black Prayer**
is one of the few affected spells. This means that Morgana will likely
not be able to actually access this spell unless the player installs one
of the unofficial patches. Even then, she can't cast it at her initial
level either. She needs to become at least a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Commander** (level 4), become a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Captain** (level 3) if one of her [Random Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Random_Abilities "Random Abilities") grants extra [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**, or otherwise equip [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") to gain enough [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** to use **Black Prayer**.

## Strategy

Casting **Black Prayer**
will instantly reduce the combat effectiveness of all enemy units on the
battlefield, making it easier to win the battle. The low **-1**
penalties to the various attributes may seem very small, but they can be
quite significant considering that the entire enemy army suffers this
simultaneously. Therefore, **Black Prayer**
is best cast when the opposing force is large - the more enemy units
there are, the more cost-effective this spell becomes. This is even more
true if the opponent is fielding [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units**, as both the [Attack Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") and [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") penalties apply on a per-[Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** basis, not to mention several [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") effects, such as [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Wrack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wrack "Wrack")**, or [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Word of Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Word_of_Death "Word of Death")**.

**Black Prayer** is one of only a few ways to bring the [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score of a unit below the treshold of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** to make it possible to affect them with ill effects. In addition, as a [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment"), it can not be resisted, and no enemy unit is exempt from it, even those possessing [Death Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity") [Death-](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Immunity "Death Immunity") or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity"). Because of this, **Black Prayer** has a good synergy with all [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistable** spells from every [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), not just those of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**.

Because of the way [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack") are implemented in the official game, **Black Prayer** can completely nullify the [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") of enemy [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**[Basilisks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk")** and [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**[Gorgons](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gorgons "Gorgons")** encountered outside of the enhancement aura of a [Nature](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature "Nature")**[Nature Node](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Nature_Node "Nature Node")**. This can then only be reversed by dispelling **Black Prayer**. It can also negate the [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze") [Death Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death_Gaze "Death Gaze") of [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Night Stalkers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Night_Stalker "Night Stalker")**, although in this case only if the [Death](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Death "Death")**[Darkness](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Darkness "Darkness")** effect is not also in play.
