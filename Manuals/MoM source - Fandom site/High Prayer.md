**High Prayer** is a [Very Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Very_Rare_Spell "Very Rare Spell") [Combat Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Combat_Enchantment "Combat Enchantment") from the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"). For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **60** it may be cast during combat to give a lasting effect that grants no less than 5 different bonuses to *all* friendly units on the battlefield: [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+2**, [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2**, [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+3**, [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **+10%**, and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **+10%**. As a result, the caster's units become significantly more deadly, and much harder to damage, hinder, or incapacitate.

## Effects

**High Prayer** raises the
morale of all friendly units on the battlefield, encouraging them to
perform better in almost every respect. As a result, units will strike
harder and more truly with their attacks, dodge incoming damage more
deftly, and become very resilient in the face of curses and combat
maledictions.

### Melee Attack Bonus

While in effect, **High Prayer** grants a [Melee Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength#Melee_Strength "Attack Strength") bonus of [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+2** to each and every friendly unit on the battlefield - assuming they had any [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") ability to begin with. This increases both the unit's [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** potential and average [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** output by a small amount. Naturally, such a bonus is more significant for [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units**, as it affects the attacks of each individual [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**.

### To Hit Bonus

While **High Prayer** is in effect, each and every friendly unit on the battlefield receives a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **+10% To Hit** bonus. This means that every die of their [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll") is more likely to cause a point of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**, thus increasing their overall average [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") output. The bonus applies equally to all attacks, with the exception of those that deliver [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **spell damage**. That is, it will affect [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee-** and [Counter Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"); [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") / [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") / [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"); [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown-** and [Lightning Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lightning_Breath "Lightning Breath")[Breath Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Breath_Attack "Breath Attack") **Breath Attacks**; and even the hidden Conventional component of [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"). On the other hand, it does *not* affect [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation"), or any spells, spell-like abilities, or [Spell Charges](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Charges_(Item_Power) "Spell Charges (Item Power)") used by the unit.

#### Example

:   We have a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with a [Ranged Attack Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength#Ranged_Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") of [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **10**. She does not have any [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") bonuses, which puts the success chance of her [Attack Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll") at [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%**, like most other units. The maximum amount of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** this Hero can inflict with her [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") is [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **10**, as she has 10 dice to roll, corresponding to her Attack Strength. However, on average, she will deliver only [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **10** × [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **3** points of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**, as each those 10 dice only have a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%** chance of being successful in scoring a hit.

:   With **High Prayer**, this Hero's [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** score is raised to [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **40%**. She can still inflict a maximum of only [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **10**, as her Ranged Attack Strength did not change, but *on average* will now deliver [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **10** × [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **40%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4** points of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**. Therefore, although the Hero's attack did not become *stronger*, on average, she will now realize more of her damage potential with each Ranged Attack she makes.

:   If this Hero also has a [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") with the same strength, that will receive an even greater benefit, as **High Prayer** will also increase her [Melee Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength#Melee_Strength "Attack Strength"), and thereby her [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") potential. This would improve her average [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** output from [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **10** × [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **30%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **3** points to an even higher [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **12** × [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **40%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **4.8**.

### Defense Bonus

While in effect, **High Prayer** grants a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") bonus of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2** to each and every friendly unit on the battlefield. As a result, they will be able to use two more dice on any [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") they make in an attempt to avoid incoming [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage"). Coupled with the bonus below, this yields a typical average increase of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.8** in the unit's [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** reduction from attacks against which a Defense Roll can be made. That is, nearly all Conventional Damage attacks, including [Melee-](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") and [Counter Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), [Thrown Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack"), [Breath Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Breath_Attack "Breath Attack"), hidden components of [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"), and even [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **direct damage** spells - but excluding [Doom Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Damage "Doom Damage") attacks and, in most cases, [Illusion Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusion_Damage "Illusion Damage") attacks.

### To Block Bonus

While **High Prayer** is in effect, all friendly units on the battlefield get a +10% chance of success for each die of every [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") they make to reduce incoming [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage"). Essentially, this raises the chance that each [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Shield** will manage to block a point of "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**. Therefore, the unit is likely to block more *on average*.
Naturally, this does not apply against attacks which do not allow a
Defense Roll in the first place, but the benefit is otherwise
proportionate to the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** attribute.

#### Example

:   We have a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **5**. Like all units without the [Lucky](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lucky "Lucky") [Lucky](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lucky "Lucky") trait, her chance [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** is the default [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30%**. Whenever this Hero is hit with a [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") attack that allows a [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll"), she can use 5 dice (equal to her [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense**), each of which has a [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30%** chance to reduce the incoming "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** by [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1** point. The average reduction will thus be [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **5** × [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1.5**.

:   With **High Prayer**, the success chance of each die is raised to [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **40%**. However, in addition to that, the Hero's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** also receives a boost of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2**. As such, even without the increase in success chance, her reduction potential would go from [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **5** to [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **7**, yielding an average reduction of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **7** × [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **30%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **2.1** points. When paired with the increased success chance, **High Prayer** will allow this Hero to block, on average, [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **7** × [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **40%** = [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **2.8** points from Conventional Damage attacks directed at her.

### Resistance Bonus

While **High Prayer** is in effect, each and every friendly unit on the battlefield receives a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") bonus of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+3**.
In other words, their chance to shrug off malicious effects is
increased by +30%. As a result, they will be significantly harder to
affect with [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"), and will stand up much better against [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") attacks like [poison](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage") or [petrification](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage").

### Enemy Melee To Hit Malus

While **High Prayer** is in effect, each and every enemy unit on the battlefield receives a [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **-10% To Hit** malus to their [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"). That is, every time they perform such an attack against a unit affected by **High Prayer**, including during [Counter Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack"), the success chance of each die of their [Attack Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Roll "Attack Roll") is reduced by [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **10%**. While this does not affect their *potential* [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**, it does decrease their *average* "raw" [Melee Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Damage "Melee Damage") output by an amount proportionate to their [Melee Strength](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength#Melee_Strength "Attack Strength"). Other [Attack Types](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Type "Attack Type") are not affected.

It should be noted that this effect is removed from the game starting with the unofficial [Insecticide](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Insecticide "Insecticide")
patch. Examination of the game code has led multiple patch-makers to
the conclusion that this is actually not an intentional benefit. It is
most likely left over from an earlier stage of the game development,
where Melee Attacks were handled differently from other Conventional
Damage types (hence why the effect only triggers for Melee Damage).

### High Prayer vs. Prayer

**High Prayer** is essentially a "souped-up" version of the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Prayer "Prayer")** spell, adding all of its bonuses along with another set: [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **+2**, [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2**, and [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+2**. However, the identical effects are *not cumulative*. Therefore, if a [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") has both spells active during combat, only **High Prayer** will have any effect so long as it is still in play. If **High Prayer** is dispelled in such a situation, but [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Prayer "Prayer")** remains in effect for whatever reason, friendly units get to keep their [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit** and [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block** bonuses, and a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") bonus of only [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **+1**. The rest of the bonuses will be lost.

## Usage

**High Prayer** may only be cast during combat, for a basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **60**. If the casting is successful, the spell's icon will appear in one of the *enchantment windows* at the bottom of the screen, underneath the name of the [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") whose side has cast the spell (left or right depending on whether it is cast by the defending or attacking force). **High Prayer**
will then start exerting its effect immediately, and remains active
until the end of the battle, or until it is dispelled. Since it does not
carry over to the overland map, the spell has no [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost").

## Acquisition

As a [Very Rare Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Very_Rare_Spell "Very Rare Spell") of the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), **High Prayer** may become available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") who acquires at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**3** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
With any less, the spell can normally not be learned during the
campaign. This is the minimum requirement for it to be traded for,
appear in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be found in the spoils of victory when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival Wizard who already knows it.

The base chance for **High Prayer** to be [researchable](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research "Spell Research") (at some point) in the campaign is roughly 10% (with [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**3** books), which gradually increases with the amount of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")** Spellbooks possessed or found during gameplay. With [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**10** or more, the spell is certain to show up sooner or later, unless acquired from another source. It has a [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research#Research_Cost "Spell Research") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **1,850**, although its research is quicker for Wizards possessing the [Sage Master](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sage_Master "Sage Master") [Retort](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort"), or a bookshelf containing [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**8** or more.

**High Prayer** may not be acquired at the start of the game, regardless of the amount of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**
books selected from the starting picks. It must always be either
researched or found. However, a bug exists in the official game, that
may occasionally allow **High Prayer** to be researched with only [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**2** books despite its [Rarity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Rarity "Spell Rarity"). This can happen if, and only if, the second [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**
Spellbook comes from Treasure, as it then allows a random Very Rare
Spell to be researched, which has a flat 10% chance of being this spell.
This oversight is corrected in the [Unofficial Patch 1.50](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unofficial_Patch_1.50 "Unofficial Patch 1.50").

## Strategy

**High Prayer** is the strongest army-enhancement spell in the game. In addition to providing the same [To Hit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/To_Hit "To Hit") **To Hit**, [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **To Block**, and [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** bonuses as the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Prayer "Prayer")** spell, it also improves [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Strength**, [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense**, and boosts [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance**
even further. This makes it an extremely useful spell to cast during a
large battle, where it can instantly make an entire army much more
powerful. Even the weakest affected units will be significantly harder
to destroy, and a much greater threat to the enemy.

At the same time though, **High Prayer** costs exactly twice as much [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** as [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Prayer "Prayer")**, and is not cumulative with it in any way. Therefore, **High Prayer** is not necessarily a good spell to cast in every situation - most notably, when the available [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** or [Spell Casting Skill](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Casting_Skill "Spell Casting Skill")
is limited, when the "army" only consists of one or two units, or when
the majority of it is not expected to make use of the extra benefits,
then using other spells, including the afore mentioned [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**[Prayer](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Prayer "Prayer")**, may be much more cost-effective. Naturally though, if [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** is abundant, **High Prayer** can make virtually any battle easier.
