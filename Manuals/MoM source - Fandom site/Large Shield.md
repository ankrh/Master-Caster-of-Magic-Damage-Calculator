**Large Shield** is one of the many [Unit Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Abilities "Unit Abilities") available in *Master of Magic*.

When a unit with a **Large Shield** is struck by certain types of attacks, it temporarily receives a bonus of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2 Defense**. This often results in the unit suffering slightly less [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **Damage** from such attacks.

**Large Shield**'s bonus applies against all types of [Ranged Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack"), as well as [Thrown Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") and [Breath Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Breath_Attack "Breath Attack"). It does not apply against [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"). All other [Attack Types](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Type "Attack Type") ignore the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense**, so **Large Shield** has no effect against them.

**Large Shield** is a fairly-common ability, possessed by no fewer than 15 different [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") by default. The absolute majority of these are [Swordsmen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Swordsmen "Swordsmen") from the various [Races](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Races "Races") (each Race has one such unit available).

The **Large Shield** bonus is also given to any [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") wielding a [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield") item, regardless of any other bonus the item also provides. This is the only way to add the **Large Shield** ability to any unit that does not already possess it.

## Description

Though heavy and unwieldy, a shield is an excellent way to protect a
fighter in combat. A large piece of solid material, placed between the
fighter and the source of an attack, has a good chance of blocking
whatever is trying to hurt that fighter. Though it is often a liability
in close combat, where opponents can easily circumvent the shield's
protection, it is still nonetheless particularly useful against a
majority of longer-ranged attacks.

A shield is a standard piece of equipment for [Swordsmen](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Swordsmen "Swordsmen"),
who will greatly enjoy the protection it gives them as they strive to
close in with the enemy and initiate hand-to-hand combat. [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") may also find it beneficial, though they can easily gain access to far more effective armor instead.

## Effect

The effect of the **Large Shield** ability is to raise the [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score of the unit by [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2**, allowing it to block some extra damage when it comes under attack.

This bonus is applied automatically by the game, whenever the unit is targeted and struck by the following [Attack Types](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Type "Attack Type"):

* [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") **Ranged Missile Attacks**
* [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **Ranged Magical Attacks**
* [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") **Ranged Boulder Attacks**
* [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown Attacks**
* [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Breath Attacks**
* [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation")
* **direct damage spells**

When any such attack is executed against the **Large Shield** unit, the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score is temporarily increased by exactly [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **2**. Therefore, it can make 2 more [Defense Roll](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") **Defense Rolls**, each of which has a chance to block an extra [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1 point of Damage** from the attack. On average, most units will block an extra [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.6** as a result.

Note that the bonus is temporary - it applies only when making
Defense Rolls against the types of attacks listed above. It does not
apply to any other type of attack, even if delivered simultaneously with
the above attacks. The bonus disappears immediately once the attack is
concluded.

Also note that **Large Shield** has no effect against [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack"), and neither does it apply against any [Special Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") that is not listed above. The majority of [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack") ignore the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** score anyway, forcing the target to make [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** rolls instead - in which case Defense bonuses wouldn't help even if they were applied.

## Acquiring Large Shield

There is only one method of adding **Large Shield** to any unit: by equipping a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with any kind of [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield") item.

Note that not every [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") has an Armor slot; mage-type Heroes in particular lack such a slot, and thus cannot equip a [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield") at all.

While a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") is wearing a [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield") item, he/she enjoys the **Large Shield** bonus whenever applicable (as described in the article above). The properties of the equipped [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield") are irrelevant, even a shield with a +1 [Movement Allowance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Movement_Allowance "Movement Allowance") bonus will also give the **Large Shield** ability as well.

Of course, it is important to note that [Chainmail](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chainmail "Chainmail") and [Platemail](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Platemail "Platemail") are generally superior to a [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield") when obtained through [Item Crafting](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Item_Crafting "Item Crafting"). Though they don't give a **Large Shield** bonus, they apply a constant Defense bonus of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+1** and [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2** respectively while worn. In the case of the [Chainmail](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chainmail "Chainmail") this is a little weaker - numerically - than the **Large Shield** bonus, but it applies against [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") (while **Large Shield** does not!). The [Platemail](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Platemail "Platemail")'s advantage over a [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield") is obvious: it gives a constant [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2** against all attacks, thus matching the **Large Shield** bonus *and* protecting against [Melee Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") with the same results.

However, since neither of the above bonuses apply to prefabricated items, in the cases of armor found as [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure") or bought from a [Merchant](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Merchant "Merchant"), a [Shield](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Shield "Shield") with the same [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** value will usually provide better protection than the other types of armor.
