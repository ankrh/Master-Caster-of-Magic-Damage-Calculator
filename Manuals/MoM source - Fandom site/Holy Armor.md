**Holy Armor** is a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Unit Enchantment](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Enchantment "Unit Enchantment") belonging to the [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life Magic** realm. For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **90** it may be cast on a friendly [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") or [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") on the overland map to empower its armor with divine energy. As a result, the armor becomes much harder, bestowing a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") bonus of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2** on the unit. This allows the unit to block a little more incoming damage from any attack.

Alternatively, a cheaper version of the same spell may be cast for [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **18**
during combat, in which case it will temporarily bestow the same bonus
on a unit for the duration of the battle (with no permanent effects or
upkeep costs).

## Effects

**Holy Armor** imbues the armor of a [Normal Unit](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") or [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") with divine energy. This allows the unit to block more damage from incoming attacks, making it more resilient in combat.

:   **Note:**  Once a unit is enchanted with [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Holy Armor**, the description of the enchantment in the *unit statistics window* incorrectly states the amount of the bonus as [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+3**. It is in fact [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2**, as the original spell description says.

### Defense Bonus

**Holy Armor**'s only bonus is a simple [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2** bonus to the unit's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense")
score. Though seemingly small, this bonus is cheap to maintain, and can
significantly improve the unit's survivability during combat.

Whenever an attack registers hits against the enchanted unit, it may make 2 extra [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") rolls to see if it has blocked any extra damage. Each roll has a default chance of 30% to block [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **1 Point of Damage**. Therefore, the unit can block up to 2 extra damage from an attack - and will statistically block about [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **0.6** on average.

This may not seem like much, and often isn't when the attacker is
a single, powerful unit or spell. However, weaker attacks, such as
attacks from enemy [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units**, will have a significantly harder time causing damage to the enchanted unit.

Note that if the enchanted unit is itself a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure unit**, the bonus is applied to the [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") of each [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure**. As per the normal combat rules, when one figure dies, the next figure gets to make another set of Defense rolls - so a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2** bonus will be a slightly more significant benefit to such units.

## Usage

**Holy Armor** may be cast during combat for the basic [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **18**. In this case, the spell will only affect the targeted unit until the end of the battle - at which point it will dissipate.

Alternatively, **Holy Armor** may be cast on the overland map for a lasting effect. In this case it has a base [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **90**, and requires an [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **1** per turn to maintain. Failure to pay this Upkeep Cost due to lack of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") will cause the spell to dissipate. However, as long as the spell is paid for (and not dispelled by any rival [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")), the unit continues to enjoy its bonus.

**Holy Armor** may only be cast on friendly [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") and [Heroes](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero"). It may not be targeted at [Fantastic Creatures](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Creature "Fantastic Creature").
If the desired target is in an overland army stack, click on that stack
to get a list of units inside it, and then select your desired unit.

Note that during battle, **Holy Armor** may not be cast on any units mutated by the [Chaos Channels](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Channels "Chaos Channels") spell. Nonetheless you may target these units freely on the overland map, and the effects will carry into combat. The [Undead](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Undead "Undead") are not valid targets in either case.

Once the spell is in effect, the unit will glow with a [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**white** aura, unless there is a more powerful spell also affecting it.

If you've placed **Holy Armor**
on a unit, you may remove it by examining the unit's details and
clicking the Enchantment's icon. The primary reason to do this would be
in order to remove the spell's [Upkeep Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Upkeep_Cost "Upkeep Cost"), thus conserving [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** for other spells.

## Acquisition

As a [Common](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Common_Spell "Common Spell") [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life** spell, **Holy Armor** may become available to any Wizard who possesses at least one [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life** [Spellbook](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook"). However, its availability during the game is not guaranteed unless the Wizard acquires at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**7** Spellbooks.

Customized Wizards possessing up to [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**10**
Spellbooks at creation time may choose this spell as one of their
default spells before starting the game, in which case the spell will
already be researched and available for casting immediately on the first
turn. Wizards with [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**11** Spellbooks are guaranteed to have this spell available for casting as soon as the game begins.

Wizards with [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**6** or fewer Spellbooks who have not chosen **Holy Armor** as a guaranteed spell have a random chance of being able to [Research](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research "Research") it. The chance for this spell to appear increases with the number of [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**Life** Spellbooks the Wizard possesses or obtains during gameplay. With [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**−3** Spellbooks, the spell is guaranteed to appear for Research if it is not already available for casting.

**Holy Armor** has a base [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Cost "Research Cost") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **130**.

With at least [Life](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Life "Life")**1** Spellbook, the **Holy Armor** spell *may be* acquired as a reward for winning encounters in creature [Lairs](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Lair "Lair"), [Towers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Tower "Tower"), et cetera, or when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival wizard who has already researched this spell.

## Strategy

The [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") bonus bestowed by **Holy Armor** may seem low, but it is actually quite potent. In particular, it will defend the unit well against other [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit") - especially against [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure units** with relatively low individual attack strength per figure.

This is a good way to protect your own Normal Units, allowing them to survive for longer during battles.

Although it can save the life of a low-tier unit, **Holy Armor** should probably be cast on your *stronger* [Normal Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Normal_Unit "Normal Unit"), as this can make them quite tough - and capable of surviving *many* battles (thus winning plenty of experience). Of course, as explained above, the protection from **Holy Armor** is somewhat less useful if the unit is being attacked by a single powerful creature or spell - in which case a [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") bonus of [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **+2** may not be good enough on its own.
