[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** is a [Rare](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** [Unit Curse](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"). For [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **35** it may be cast on an enemy unit during combat. The target does not get to make a [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") roll to avert this curse - it immediately receives **-5** to all of its [Attack Strengths](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength"), [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** and [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance**! These penalties will continue to affect the unit until the end of the battle or until [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** is [dispelled](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel "Dispel").

Units possessing the [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") abilities may not be targeted by this spell.

## Effects

[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm**
can significantly reduce an enemy unit's combat potential across the
board - both offensively and defensively - possibly rendering the unit
entirely combat-incapable. It also makes the target more susceptible to
additional [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse") and other ill effects.

### Attack Penalties

While a unit is under the effect of [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm**, all of its non-spell [Conventional](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") [Attack Strengths](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Attack_Strength "Attack Strength") are reduced by **5**. This includes both [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee-** and [Ranged Missile Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Missile_Attack "Ranged Missile Attack") / [Ranged Boulder Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Boulder_Attack "Ranged Boulder Attack") / [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") [Ranged Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Attack "Ranged Attack") Strength, but also [Thrown Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Thrown_Attack "Thrown Attack") **Thrown** and [Fire Breath](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fire_Breath "Fire Breath") **Fire Breath**, and even the (often hidden) Conventional component of [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"). The only exceptions are the strengths of [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation") [Immolation](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Immolation "Immolation"), spell-like abilities (e.g. [Fireball Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fireball_Spell "Fireball Spell") [Fireball Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fireball_Spell "Fireball Spell")), and any actual spells castable by the unit.

If the unit's Melee or Ranged Attack is reduced to a score of 0 or lower, that unit will no longer be able to use that attack *at all*. If [Melee Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") **Melee Strength** is reduced to 0 or less, the unit will also be unable to [Counter Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Counter_Attack "Counter Attack") against any opponent. In [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Units**, these penalties are even more severe, as they affect each and every [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in the unit. For example, a unit containing [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **6 figures** will have actually lost up to -30 from its total Attack Strength! That equals [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **30** points of potential [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** per attack.

Although [Special Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Damage "Special Damage") attacks are not directly affected by [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm**,
they may still be disabled by it if the strength of the conventional
attack they are affixed to is reduced to zero. This applies to all [Touch Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Touch_Attack "Touch Attack"), whether delivered in [Melee](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Melee_Attack "Melee Attack") or at range, and will even affect [Gaze Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Gaze_Attack "Gaze Attack"). For example, a [Basilisk](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Basilisk "Basilisk") struck by this curse will no longer be able to use its [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") [Stoning Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Gaze "Stoning Gaze") at all, as this ability is triggered through a normally hidden Conventional Damage attack with a strength of [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **1**. Even the [Chaos Spawn](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Spawn "Chaos Spawn")'s dreaded "Multi-Gaze" can be nullified with [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm**, although in this case, a nearby [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**[Chaos Node](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Node "Chaos Node")** or active [Chaos](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos "Chaos")**[Chaos Surge](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Chaos_Surge "Chaos Surge")** enchantment will effectively undo this effect, as the [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") [Doom Gaze](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Doom_Gaze "Doom Gaze") has a base Attack Strength of [Ranged Magical Attack](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Ranged_Magical_Attack "Ranged Magical Attack") **4**. At any rate, the [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resistance** penalties or other attributes of the target's [Special Attacks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Special_Attack "Special Attack"), if any, are not affected by [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** in any other way.

### Defense Penalty

The [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** spell also reduces the target's [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") score by [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **-5**, meaning that whenever its [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures** are struck by a [Conventional Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Conventional_Damage "Conventional Damage") attack, they will have 5 less dice to use on their [Defense Rolls](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense_Roll "Defense Roll") to try and block the "raw" [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage**. As a result, the unit will likely suffer more [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** from any such attack, including direct-damage spells; although it should be noted that [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **Defense** can never actually go below [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **0**, so targets that had less than [Defense](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Defense "Defense") **5** to begin with will be less impacted by this. On the other hand, since the penalty applies to every [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figure** in a [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure#Multi-Figure_Units "Figure") **Multi-Figure Unit**, they will have their defenses significantly more impaired against large amounts of [Damage Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Damage_Points "Damage Points") **damage** that can chain to multiple [Figure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Figure "Figure") **figures**.

### Resistance Penalty

One of the most important effects of [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** is a penalty of [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-5** to the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") score. This makes the unit less capable of avoiding further [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse") or other maledictions inflicted during combat, including [Stoning Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Stoning_Damage "Stoning Damage"), [Poison Damage](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Poison_Damage "Poison Damage"), and other similar attacks delivered by the caster's units. If a target's Resistance drops to [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **0** or less as a result of this effect, it becomes unable to resist almost *any* ill-effects.

[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm**'s Resistance-reducing effect can also be used to bring a unit's Resistance score below [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10**. Any unit with [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** or higher is immune to effects that do not inflict a Resistance penalty, so lowering Resistance below [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **10** makes an otherwise immune unit vulnerable to such effects.

## Usage

 [A unit of affected by](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/masterofmagic/images/f/fa/Screenshot_MindStorm.png/revision/latest?cb=20120121204717)

A unit of [Orc Halberdiers](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Orc_Halberdiers "Orc Halberdiers") affected by [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm**.



[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** may only be cast during combat. It has a base [Casting Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Casting_Cost "Casting Cost") of [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **35**, and must always be targeted at an enemy unit that is not already under the effect of this curse. This spell can not be [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance") **Resisted** and will always take effect immediately. The curse will last until the end of the battle, until it is [dispelled](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel "Dispel"),
or until the target unit is destroyed; whichever occurs first. While
the spell is in effect, the cursed unit will have a series of flashing [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**blue** lights spiraling around it, as seen in the image to the right.

[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** may not be targeted at units possessing either the [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") [Illusions Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Illusions_Immunity "Illusions Immunity") or [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") [Magic Immunity](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magic_Immunity "Magic Immunity") abilities. The game will refuse to target such units, asking the player to pick another target instead.

## Acquisition

As a [Rare Spell](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Rare_Spell "Rare Spell") of the [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** [Realm](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Realm "Realm"), [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** may become available to any [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard") who acquires at least [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**2** [Spellbooks](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spellbook "Spellbook").
With any less, the spell can not be learned during the campaign. This
is the minimum requirement for it to be traded for, appear in [Treasure](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Treasure "Treasure"), or be found in the spoils of victory when conquering the [Fortress](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fortress "Fortress") of a rival Wizard who already knows it.

The base chance for [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** to be [researchable](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research "Spell Research") (at some point) in the campaign is roughly 10% (with [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**2** books), which gradually increases with the amount of [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** Spellbooks possessed or found during gameplay. With [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**10** or more, the spell is certain to show up sooner or later, unless acquired from another source. It has a [Research Cost](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Spell_Research#Research_Cost "Spell Research") of [Research Points](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Research_Points "Research Points") **1,400**, although its research is quicker for Wizards possessing the [Sage Master](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sage_Master "Sage Master"), and/or [Sorcery Mastery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery_Mastery "Sorcery Mastery") [Retorts](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Retort "Retort"); or a bookshelf containing [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**8** or more.

[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** may also be selected as a starting spell by players who allocate all of of their picks into [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**[Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")** books when creating their Wizard. In this case, it will be available for casting as soon as the game begins.

In addition, [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** is available as a [Hero](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Hero "Hero") spell. It is known by [Aerie the Illusionist](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Aerie_the_Illusionist "Aerie the Illusionist"), although it's worth noting that she can't actually cast it at her initial level. She needs to either become at least a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Commander** (level 4), become a [Experience Level](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Experience_Level "Experience Level") **Captain** (level 3) if one of her [Random Abilities](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Random_Abilities "Random Abilities") is extra [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana**, or otherwise equip [Magical Items](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Magical_Item "Magical Item") to gain enough [Mana](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Mana "Mana") **Mana** to use [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm**.

## Strategy

When cast on a low-tier or even a mid-tier unit, [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm**
can render that unit completely incapable of doing combat and/or
defending itself in battle. Such units can then be destroyed very
easily. However, [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** is more often used to weaken very powerful units - especially because it ignores the target's [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance "Resistance")
score. Therefore, even the most Resistant enemy units can be afflicted
with this Curse and weakened considerably. As an additional bonus, the [Resistance](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Resistance#Spell_Save_Penalties "Resistance") **-5** penalty inflicted by [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** makes such units susceptible to further [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse") or other combat maledictions, allowing a powerful [Wizard](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Wizard "Wizard")
to cast more spells upon them and weaken them further or eliminate them
altogether. This is an exceptionally effective way of getting rid of
very powerful [Fantastic Units](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Fantastic_Unit "Fantastic Unit") or high-level [Champions](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Champion "Champion")!

## Known Bugs

Like other [Unit Curses](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Unit_Curse "Unit Curse"), [Sorcery](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Sorcery "Sorcery")**Mind Storm** is susceptible to a bug where even the lowest strength [dispel](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel "Dispel") will remove it automatically, with no [dispel attempt](https://masterofmagic.fandom.com/wiki/Dispel "Dispel") checked for a random chance of success.
